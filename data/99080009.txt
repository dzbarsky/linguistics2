Overseas Private Investment Corporation, Renewable Energy & Clean Technology: Accessing Opportunities in Emerging Markets, May-05-2010 through May-07-2010.
"Overseas Private Investment Corporation, Renewable Energy & Clean Technology: Accessing Opportunities in Emerging Markets, May-05-2010 through May-07-2010. Venue: Shangri-La Hotel, New Delhi, India. Conference sessions will outline strategies needed to undertake successful renewable energy and clean technology projects. Special emphasis will be given to regulatory development issues, new technologies, and accessing appropriate debt and private equity financing. OPIC investment conferences provide a unique mix of entrepreneurs and financiers with the right blend of decision makers to make them highly successful. The program is designed to inform the audience on real, achievable and profitable projects."
