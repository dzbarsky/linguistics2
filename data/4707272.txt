Derek C. Hathaway to Retire as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Harsco Corp. in April 2008.
Harsco Corp. announced that its Chairman and Chief Executive Derek C. Hathaway will retire in April 2008. Effective Jan. 1, Hathaway will be succeeded as CEO by Salvatore D. Fazzolari, who currently serves as president, chief financial officer and treasurer. Fazzolari is expected to become chairman after Hathaway's retirement.
