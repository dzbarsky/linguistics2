Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Tazo Tea Company; Buyer(s): Starbucks Corp..
"Starbucks Coffee Company (Nasdaq: SBUX) signed an agreement to acquire Tazo. Specific terms of the cash agreement were not disclosed. Tazo's current management will remain in place as will the Tazo brand and their high-quality, authentic products. The cash purchase, which is subject to satisfaction of certain conditions, is expected to close by the end of January 1999. Tazo Tea Company was acquired by Starbucks Coffee Company on 01/20/1999."
