Illumina Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 2, 2005; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2005.
"Illumina Inc. announced earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 2, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $1,426,000 or basic and diluted loss per share of $0.03 on total revenue of $19,516,000 against net loss of $2,026,000 or basic and diluted loss per share of $0.05 on total revenue of $13,512,000 for the same quarter a year ago. The company reported loss from operations of $1,538,000 against loss from operations of $1,780,000 for the same quarter a year ago. The company provided earnings guidance for the fourth quarter and full year 2005."
