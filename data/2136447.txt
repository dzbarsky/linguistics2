Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Gibraltar Mausoleum Construction Company, Inc.; Buyer(s): Matthews International Corp.; Seller(s): Service Corp. International; Transaction Size: $10 mm.
"Matthews International Corporation (Nasdaq: MATW) announced that it signed an agreement to acquire the assets of Gibraltar Mausoleum Construction Company, Inc., a subsidiary of Service Corporation International (NYSE: SRV) for $10 million in cash. Gibraltar Mausoleum Construction Company, Inc. was acquired by Matthews International Corp. on 09/30/1998."
