Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Provides Earnings Outlook for Second Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2005.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. provides earnings outlook for second quarter and full year of fiscal 2005. The company expects non-GAAP EPS of $0.05 per share on revenues of $157 to $162 million. The FC mean is $0.05 per share on revs of $155 million. For the fiscal year, company expects revenues of $660 to $672 million, vs. the Street view of $650 million."
