Harsco Corporation Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010; Revises Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter 2010.
Harsco Corporation reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, sales increased by 6.5% to $742 million, compared with $697 million in the first quarter of last year. Diluted earnings per share from continuing operations were $0.10 compared with $0.25 per share in the first quarter of last year. Results in the quarter were negatively impacted by continued poor end-market conditions in the Company's Harsco Infrastructure business segment. Capital expenditures were $29.8 million, a 17% decrease from $36.0 million for the same period last year. Operating income from continuing operations was $28.24 million, income from continuing operations was $9.68 million and net Income attributable to common stockholders was $8.03 million compared to operating income from continuing operations of $37.17 million, income from continuing operations of $20.98 million and net Income attributable to common stockholders of $18.59 million for the same period last year.
