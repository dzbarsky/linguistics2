Molex Inc. Re-Appoints Joe W. Laymon to Its Board.
Molex Inc. announced that Joe W. Laymon has been appointed to its board of directors. Mr. Laymon, 55, is Group Vice President, Corporate Human Resources and Labor Affairs, for Ford Motor Company. Mr. Laymon previously served on Molex's board from 2002 to 2006. His election expands the Molex Board to 13 directors, including eight outside directors. Mr. Laymon is the third outside director to be appointed in the past nine months.
