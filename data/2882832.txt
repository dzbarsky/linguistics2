Brocade and Packeteer Announce Strategic Agreement to Deliver Branch Office Infrastructure Solutions.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. and Packeteer Inc. announced a strategic Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) agreement to develop and deliver innovative branch office solutions that optimize the management and consolidation of an enterprise's distributed file data. This relationship extends the existing product and distribution collaboration that Brocade and Tacit Networks entered into in May 2005. Under the terms of the agreement, Packeteer will provide to Brocade, as an OEM, the Packeteer Wide Area File Services (WAFS) product set originally available from Tacit Networks. Brocade will become the exclusive OEM distributor of Packeteer's new WAFS solutions and have access to ongoing enhancements.
