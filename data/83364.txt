Health Net Inc. Announces Jeff Folick as Executive Vice President of Regional Health Plans and Specialty Companies.
Health Net Inc. announced that it has named Jeffrey M. Folick, 54, as executive vice president of Regional Health Plans and Specialty Companies. In this role, Folick will oversee Health Net health plans in the Northeast and Arizona, as well as MHN, the company's behavioral health care subsidiary. Folick is also charged with developing health care performance management strategies for all Health Net health plans. This is a new position for Health Net that reports to Jay Gellert, president and chief executive officer of Health Net Inc. Most recently, he served as president and chief operating officer of PacifiCare Health Systems.
