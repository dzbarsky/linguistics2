Service Corp. International Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2007.
Service Corp. International reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported that consolidated revenues increased $165.6 million or 37.5% to $607.6 million from $442.0 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations excluding special items increased to $51.7 million, or $0.17 per diluted share compared to $30.5 million, or $0.10 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2006, primarily reflecting the acquisition of the Alderwoods Group Inc. on November 28, 2006. Net income was $37.6 million or $0.13 earnings per diluted share compared to $26.9 million or $0.09 earnings per diluted share a year ago.
