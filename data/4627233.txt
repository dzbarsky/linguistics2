Starbucks Corp. Appoints Martin Coles as Chief Operating Officer, Effective September 4, 2007.
Starbucks Corp. named Martin Coles as chief operating officer, a new position, as part of a management reorganization, effective Sept. 4, 2007. Coles is currently president of Starbucks Coffee International. In his new post he will manage global operations and be responsible for U.S. and international store operations and store development, Starbucks global consumer products group and supply chain operations.
