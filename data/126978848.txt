Panera Bread Co. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 28, 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter of Fiscal 2011.
"Panera Bread Co. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 28, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $36,384,000 and net income attributable to the company was $36,520,000 or $1.22 per basic share and $1.21 per diluted share on total revenue of $428,161,000 against net income of $29,696,000 and net income attributable to the company was $29,696,000 or $0.96 per basic share and $0.95 per diluted share on total revenue of $366,972,000 for the same period a year ago. Operating profit was $60,809,000 compared to $47,307,000 a year ago. Income before income taxes was $59,177,000 compared to $47,962,000 a year ago. For the fourth quarter, the company generated approximately $51 million of cash from operations net of capex and tax payments.
