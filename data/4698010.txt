Dolby Laboratories Seeks Acquisitions.
Dolby Laboratories Inc is set to seek acquisitions to accelerate growth. The company recently acquired BrightSide Technologies Inc. We will continue to look for acquisitions where we think that the technology will fit in with what we're doing both on the audio side, but more importantly on the video side as we continue to expand our market offerings, said Bill Jasper, Dolby Laboratories' President and CEO.
