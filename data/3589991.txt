Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 27, 2007.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended January 27, 2007. Revenues for the quarter were a record $224.2 million. Revenues for first quarter of 2007 increased 7% from $170.1 million reported in the first quarter of fiscal year 2006. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net income for first quarter of 2007 was $33.3 million, or $0.12 per share basic and diluted. This reflects an increase in GAAP net income of 67% from $20.0 million, an increase of 245% from GAAP net income of $9.7 million, or $0.04 per share basic and diluted in first quarter of 2006. Non-GAAP net income for first quarter of 2007 was $49.4 million or $0.18 per share basic and $0.17 per share diluted. This reflects an increase of 91% from non-GAAP net income of $25.9 million, or $0.10 per share basic and diluted in first quarter of 2006.
