Illumina Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 4, 2010.
Illumina Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended April 4, 2010. For the period, the company's total revenues were $192,131,000 against $165,757,000 a year ago. Income from operations was $38,225,000 against $34,508,000 a year ago. Income before income taxes was $33,361,000 against $29,351,000 a year ago. Net income was $21,208,000 or $0.16 per diluted share against $18,811,000 or $0.14 per diluted share a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $55,364,000 against $50,748,000 a year ago. Purchases of property and equipment were amounted to $10,447,000 against $12,569,000 a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $26,558,000 or $0.21 per diluted share against $25,423,000 or $0.20 per diluted share a year ago. Non-GAAP operating profit was $57,763,000 against $53,957,000 a year ago.
