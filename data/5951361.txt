Fidelity National Financial Inc. Declares Reduced Quarterly Cash Dividend, Payable on December 31, 2008.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.15 per share, a 50% reduction from the previous quarterly dividend of $0.30. The dividend will be payable on December 31, 2008, to stockholders of record as of December 17, 2008.
