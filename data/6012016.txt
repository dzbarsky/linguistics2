Update to Harsco Corp.'s Equity Buyback on November 11, 2008.
On November 11, 2008, Harsco Corp. announced that it repurchased 2,000,000 shares since its quarterly black-out period expired on October 31, 2008. As of November 11, 2008, the company repurchased 3,053,633 shares of common stock, under the repurchase plan.
