Starbucks Corp. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2007 ; Plans to Open About 2,400 Net New Stores.
"Starbucks Corp. provided earnings guidance for the full year of fiscal 2007. The company expects total net revenue growth of about 20%, same-store sales growth in the range of 3% to 7% and earnings per share in the range of 87 cents a share to 89 cents a share for the fiscal year. The company announced that it plans to open about 2,400 net new stores around the world in fiscal 2007."
