AK Steel Holding Corp. Appoints Ralph S. Michael lII to Its Board.
AK Steel Holding Corp. announced that on July 19, 2007, the Board of Directors expanded its number of authorized directors from nine to ten and elected Mr. Ralph S. Michael III to fill the vacancy thereby created and to be a director of the Company, effective July 20, 2007. Mr. Michael currently serves as the President and Chief Operating Officer of the Ohio Casualty Insurance Company.
