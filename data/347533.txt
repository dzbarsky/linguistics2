Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Record Second Quarter 2002 Results.
"Hansen Natural Corp. reported record gross and net sales for the second quarter ended June 30, 2002, supported by core beverage lines and new product introductions. Net sales for the second quarter increased 16.7 percent to $26.3 million from $22.5 million a year ago. Net income for the second quarter was$1.3 million, or $0.12 per diluted share, compared with $1.1 million, or $0.11 per diluted share, in the comparable period in 2001. Net sales for the same period increased 13.8 percent to $44.9 million from $39.4 million last year. Net income for the first six months was $1.7 million, or $0.16 per diluted share, compared with$1.4 million, or $0.14 per diluted share, in the comparable six months in 2001."
