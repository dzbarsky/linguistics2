Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Provides Cash Flows Guidance for Fourth Quarter of 2009; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2009.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. provided earnings guidance for the fourth quarter and full year of 2009. The company expect cash flows to be in a range of $120 to $150 million in fiscal fourth quarter which is typically a seasonally strong cash flow quarter. Consistent with the fiscal 2009 outlook the company provided in February, expect revenues to be in the range from $1.9 to $2.0 billion. The company expect non-GAAP EPS to be in a range from $0.48 to $0.52 which is up from $0.40 to $0.50 range given on the February call."
