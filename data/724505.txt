Brocade Announces Appointment of L. William Krause to Board of Directors.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that L. William Krause has joined its Board of Directors. Krause, one of the best known leaders in the computer networking industry, is presently Chairman of the Board of Caspian Networks, an IP networking systems provider, as well as the President of LWK Ventures, a private investment firm. Krause, who also served as Chief Executive Officer of Caspian Networks between April 2002 and June 2004, has been active in the computer and networking industry for 35 years. Under his leadership in the 1980s, 3Com Corporation grew from a start-up to a global data networking company.
