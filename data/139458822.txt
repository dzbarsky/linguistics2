Advance Publications, Inc. acquired Magazine Publishing Group from New York Times Co. for approximately $440 million..
"Advance Publications, Inc. acquired Magazine Publishing Group from New York Times Co. for approximately $440 million on April 2, 2001. The properties included Golf Digest, Golf Digest Woman, Golf World, Golf World Business and GolfDigest.com. Sabin Bermant & Gould LLP acted as legal advisor to Advance Publications, Inc. Advance Publications, Inc. completed the acquisition of Magazine Publishing Group from New York Times Co. on April 2, 2001."
