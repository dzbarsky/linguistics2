Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Reports Consolidated Unaudited Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended May 1, 2010.
"Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. reported consolidated unaudited earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended May 1, 2010. For the quarter, total net revenues were $500,983,000 against $506,300,000 a year ago. Income from operations was $41,712,000 against loss from operations of $61,920,000 a year ago. Income before provision for (benefit from) income taxes was $21,540,000 against loss before provision for (benefit from) income taxes of $89,863,000 a year ago. Net income was $22,380,000 or $0.05 per basic and diluted share against net loss of $66,093,000 or $0.17 per basic and diluted share a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $67,722,000 against $107,349,000 a year ago. Purchases of property and equipment were $62,070,000 against $37,634,000 a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $62,734,000 or $0.13 per diluted share against $47,126,000 or $0.11 per diluted share a year ago.
