Starbucks Corp. Announces Revenue Results for the Month of September and Fiscal Year Ended October 1, 2006.
"Starbucks Corp. announced revenue results for the five-week period ended October 1, 2006. The company reported consolidated net revenues of $790 million for the month of September ended October 1, 2006, an increase of 20% from consolidated net revenues of $656 million for the same period in fiscal 2005. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 6% for the September ended October 1, 2006, compared to the same period ended October 2, 2005. For the fiscal Year ended October 1, 2006, consolidated net revenues were $7.8 billion, an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $6.4 billion for the same period in fiscal 2005. Comparable store sales increased 7% for the fiscal Year ended October 1, 2006, compared to the same period ended October 2, 2005."
