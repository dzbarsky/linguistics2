Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Unaudited Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported unaudited earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $12,305,000, or $0.06 per basic and diluted share on total revenue of $1,357,483,000 compared to net earnings of $27,299,000, or $0.14 per basic and diluted share on total revenue of $1,125,109,000 for the same period last year. The company reported earnings from continuing operations before income taxes of $141,000, net loss attributable to common shareholders of $12,398,000, or $0.06 per share, net loss from continuing operations of $11,978,000 compared to earnings from continuing operations before income taxes of $42,105,000, net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $27,245,000, or $0.13 per share, net earnings from continuing operations of $29,530,000 for the same period last year.
