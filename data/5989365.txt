Service Corp. International Announces Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2008 and Full Year of 2009 ; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Service Corp. International announced unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $14.6 million or $0.06 diluted earnings per share on the revenue of $516.4 million compared to net income of $28.2 million or $0.10 diluted earnings per share on the revenue of $539.3 million for the same period last year. Earnings from continuing operations excluding special items of $23.9 million or $0.09 diluted earning per share compared to earnings from continuing operations of $28.7 million or $0.10 diluted earning per share for the same period last year. Revenues decreased $22.9 million, or 4.2%, as a result of decreased cemetery property sales production and significant divestiture activity throughout 2007. Income from continuing operations was $14.6 million or $0.06 per diluted share against $28.8 million or $0.10 per diluted share last year. For the nine months, the company reported net income of $87.6 million or $0.33"
