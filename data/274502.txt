Molex Reports 2002 Fiscal Second Quarter Results.
"Molex Incorporated has reported results for its fiscal second quarter and first six months ended December 31, 2001. Revenues for the 2002 fiscal second quarter of $416.5 million decreased 33.8 percent versus last year's second quarter revenues of $629.3 million. Net income after the charge and tax adjustments was $4.3 million. Earnings per share after the charge and tax adjustments were $0.02. Revenues for the six months ended December 31, 2001 were $846.9 million, compared with $1.25 billion in the prior fiscal year period, a 32.5 percent decrease. Net income after the charge and tax adjustments was $29.5 million, or 3.5 percent of sales. Earnings per share after the charge and tax adjustments were $0.15."
