Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2007.
"Atheros Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $9.3 million or $0.16 per diluted share compared to $6.8 million or $0.12 per diluted share. Revenues were $100.8 million up 38%from the $73.2 million reported in the second quarter of 2006. Non-GAAP net income was $14.7 million or $0.25 per diluted share, compared to $9.5 million or $0.17 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2006. For the six months ended June 30, 2007, the company reported net income of $16.9 million or $0.29 per diluted share compared to $13.6 million or $0.25 per diluted share. Revenues were $196.3 million against $134.3 million reported in 2006. Non-GAAP net income was $28.3 million or $0.48 per diluted share, compared to $17.4 million or $0.32 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2006."
