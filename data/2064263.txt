Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Stomp Inc., CD Stomper Product Line; Buyer(s): Avery Dennison Corp.; Seller(s): Stomp Inc..
Stomp Inc. sold its CD Stomper product line to Avery Dennison Corp. The financial terms of the deal were not disclosed. Sales in 2000 for the CD Stomper product line were nearly $20 million.
