Kirby Corp. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ended September 30, 2007.
Kirby Corp. revised earnings guidance for the third quarter ended September 30, 2007. The company announced that it expects its 2007 third quarter net earnings to exceed $0.60 per share, above earnings guidance of $0.53 to $0.58 per share, and substantially above 2006 third quarter earnings of $0.48 per share.
