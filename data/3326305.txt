Starbucks to Open Coffee Stores in India by Mid 2007.
Starbucks Corp. of Seattle, US, has appointed RPG Group (RPG) as the Indian partner and master franchisee for its chain of coffee shops. The company will open coffee stores in India by mid-2007 with a target to scale up the number to 200 in two years and to 500 in five years. The domestic coffee market in India is expected to grow five times to INR 10,000 million by 2009.
