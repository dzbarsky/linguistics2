Brocade Announces New Family of Solutions for Information Technology Resource Management.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced a new family of hardware and software solutions aimed at simplifying the provisioning, allocation, and management of shared IT resources across the enterprise. Beginning with two products introduced, the Brocade Tapestry(TM) family of application infrastructure solutions will help organizations leverage shared storage to better manage complexity, achieve compliance, and reduce the cost of acquiring, deploying, and maintaining IT systems that include servers, software and storage. The first products in the new Tapestry family, both featured in demonstrations at the seven Brocade Conferences worldwide beginning June 1 in Santa Clara, California, are: Brocade Tapestry Application Resource Manager (ARM) and Brocade Tapestry Wide Area File Services (WAFS).
