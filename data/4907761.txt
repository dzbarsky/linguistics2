"Illumina Inc. announced financial results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30,2007. The Company reported third quarter net income of $14.5 million, or $0.24 per diluted share, compared to net income of $16.2 million, or $0.32 per diluted share in the comparable period of 2006.
