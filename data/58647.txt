Brocade and EMC to simplify Delivery of Open Storage Management Applications.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. (Brocade(R)) and EMC Corporation announced that EMC will integrate the Brocade Fabric Access Application Programming Interface into the EMC WideSky storage management middleware. This will enable applications developed using EMC WideSky to manage the Brocade SilkWorm family of fabric switches. Using Brocade fabric switches, companies can network servers with storage devices through a storage area network, creating a highly available, manageable, scalable, and secure environment for storage applications.
