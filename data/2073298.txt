Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Beau Interconnect Systems; Buyer(s): Molex Inc.; Seller(s): Axsys Technologies Inc.; Transaction Size: $32.5 mm.
"Molex Incorporated (NASDAQ:MOLX and MOLXA) signed an agreement to acquire Beau Interconnect Systems from Axsys Technologies Inc. for $31.8 million in cash. Net after-tax cash proceeds from the sale of approximately $21.0 million will be used to repay outstanding bank debt and provide funds for working capital and future acquisitions of Axsys. Beau had sales of approximately $19.5 million in 1999. The closing of the transaction is subject to the satisfaction or waiver of various conditions, but is currently expected to occur in the first quarter of 2000. Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette acted as financial advisor to Axsys Technologies. William Blair & Co. L.L.C. served as a financial advisor to Molex Incorporated. Molex Incorporated acquired Beau Interconnect Systems from Axsys Technologies Inc. on 3/20/2000 for $32.5 million in cash."
