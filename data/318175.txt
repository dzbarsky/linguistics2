AK Steel Reports 2002 Third Quarter Results.
"AK Steel Holding Corp. reported a net loss of $3.3 million, or $0.03 per diluted share of common stock, for the third quarter of 2002. Excluding the extraordinary charge, the company earned $16.6 million, or $0.15 per diluted share of common stock. Operating profit was $56.8 million, or $40 per ton shipped. Sales were $1,117.6 million as compared to the sales of $960.7 million for the same period of last year. For the nine months ended sales were $3.2 billion and net loss was $12.7 million or $0.12 per basic and diluted share as compared to the sales of $2.9 billion and net loss of $16.0 million or $0.16 per diluted and basic share for the same period of last year."
