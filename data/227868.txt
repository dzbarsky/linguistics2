Top Research Institution Chooses the BeadLab Solution from Illumina.
Illumina Inc. announced an agreement with an undisclosed research institution for the purchase of Illumina's BeadLab, a production-scale SNP genotyping laboratory. The BeadLab will be used for large-scale genotyping projects that will provide the fundamental DNA information necessary to transform genetic research into clinical medicine. The genotyping laboratory solution is built around Illumina's proprietary BeadArray technology and features high sample throughput from unprecedented multiplex levels.
