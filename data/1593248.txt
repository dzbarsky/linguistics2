Kirby Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for Second Quarter 2005 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2005.
"Kirby Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported record net earnings of $13,279,000, or $0.52 per diluted share, against net earnings of $9,020,000, or $0.36 per diluted share, for the 2004 first quarter. Consolidated revenues were a record $184,444,000, an increase of 17% over $157,315,000 reported for the 2004 first quarter. For the 2005 year, the company increased its guidance to $2.45 to $2.55 per share from previous guidance of $2.20 to $2.30 per share. This guidance compares with 2004 net earnings of $1.97 per share."
