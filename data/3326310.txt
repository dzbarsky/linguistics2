Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Announces Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended October 29, 2006.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. announced earnings results for the nine months ended October 29, 2006. For the period, the company reported a net income of $128.5 million or $2.19 per diluted share on a total revenue of $1.53 billion against a net income of $88.8 million or $1.44 per diluted share on a total revenue of $1.45 billion a year ago. Asper the Non-GAAP rules, the company reported a net income of $122.05 million or $2.16 per diluted share on a total revenue of $1.53 billion against a net income of $88.8 million or $1.62 per diluted share on a total revenue of $1.45 billion a year ago."
