Harsco Corp. Signs $200 Million Credit Agreements with Royal Bank of Scotland.
Harsco Corp. entered credit agreements worth $200 million with the Royal Bank of Scotland. The credit agreements provide the company with additional financial flexibility to pursue its growth strategies. The first agreement with the Royal Bank of Scotland Finance is for up to $125 million, while the second with the Royal Bank of Scotland PLC is for up to $75 million. Each ends on December 31, 2007.
