AK Steel Reports First Quarter Results of 2003.
AK Steel reported a net loss of $40.8 million, or $0.38 per diluted share of common stock, for the first quarter of 2003. Net sales were $1,002.4 million, on shipments of 1,365,400 tons. The company's EBITDA were a positive $19 million, equal to $14 per ton shipped, which is reconciled to the reported net loss in the supplemental schedules included with this earnings report.
