Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for First Quarter Ended September 30, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter Ending Dec. 31, 2005; Announces Repurchase of 1,430,000 Shares.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for first fiscal quarter ended September 30, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $46.67 million or $0.25 per diluted share on net revenue of $659.82 million against net income of $52.48 million or $0.28 per diluted share on net revenue of $640.23 million for the same period last year. During the September quarter, the Company repurchased 1,430,000 shares of Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and 543,000 shares of Common Stock (MOLX) at a total cost of $50.1 million.
