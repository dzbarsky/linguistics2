Brocade Announces New Products and Capabilities to Address Broad IT Systems Requirements.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that it launched an innovative family of information technology solutions designed to address the complexity, compliance, and cost challenges faced by enterprise data centers, and announced product and service extensions to its industry-leading portfolio of infrastructure solutions for Storage Area Networks (SANs). The broad range of new products and capabilities will be showcased at the upcoming Brocade Conferences, reaching seven cities worldwide beginning June 1 in Santa Clara, California.
