Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Month Ended September 30, 2009.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine month ended September 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company has reported earnings from continuing operations before taxes of $108,299,000, net earnings from continuing operations of $76,729,000, net earnings of $74,881,000 and net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $73,444,000 or $0.32 per diluted share compared to loss from continuing operations before taxes of $317,247,000, net loss from continuing operations of $196,524,000, net loss of $200,103,000 and net loss attributable to common shareholders of $198,302,000 or $0.95 per diluted share for the same period last year. For the nine months, the company has reported earnings from continuing operations before taxes of $238,955,000, net earnings from continuing operations of $156,880,000, net earnings of $154,999,000 and net earnings attributable to common shareholders of $152,989,000 or $0.67 per diluted share on total revenue of $4,373,089,00"
