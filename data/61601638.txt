Cadence Design Systems Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 4, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ending October 3, 2009 and Full Year Ending January 2, 2010.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended July 4, 2009. The company reported second quarter 2009 revenue of $210 million, compared to revenue of $308 million reported for the same period in 2008. On a GAAP basis, Cadence recognized a net loss of $74 million, or $0.29 per share on a diluted basis, in the second quarter of 2009, compared to a net loss of $19 million, or $0.07 per share on a diluted basis in the same period in 2008. Using this non-GAAP measure, net loss in the second quarter of 2009 was $13 million, or $0.05 per share on a diluted basis, as compared to net income of $24 million, or $0.09 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2008. Loss from operations for the period was $53.8 million and loss before provision for income taxes was $63.6 million against income from operations of $0.6 million and income before provision for income taxes of $7.9 million in the year ago period.
