Fluor Joint Venture Reconfirmed Prime EPCM Contractor for Execution of $2.6 Billion Oil Field Development Project in Kazakhstan.
Fluor Corporation announced that its PFD UK Limited joint venture has been reconfirmed by Tengizchevroil (TCO) as contractor for engineering, procurement and construction management (EPCM) services for the restarted Second Generation and Sour Gas Injection (SGP/SGI) Projects at the Tengiz and Korolev Fields in Kazakhstan. Partnered with Parsons E&C, the PFD joint venture will manage SGP/SGI project execution with a value of the remaining scope to be executed at an estimated $2.6 billion. The contract value to Fluor is $1.3 billion and is subject to annual budget authorizations by the client.
