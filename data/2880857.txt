"Packeteer (Nasdaq: PKTR) entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Tacit Networks from RRE Ventures LLC, Canaan Venture Partners, Silicon Alley Seed Investors, Chevron Technology Ventures LLC, Brocade Communications Systems Inc. and Panorama Capital on May 8, 2006, for a reported consideration of approximately $78 million. Under the terms of the definitive agreement, Packeteer will also assume vested and unvested Tacit stock options which will be exercisable for an aggregate of approximately 0.3 million shares of Packeteer Common Stock. Michele S. Harrington and Andrea Ryan of Hogan & Hartson was legal advisor to the Tacit Networks. DLA Piper Rudnick was legal advisor to Packeteer Inc. The transaction is expected to close in the second quarter of 2006, subject to customary closing conditions including Tacit shareholders and regulatory approvals.
