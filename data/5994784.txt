Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Financial Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008.
"Hansen Natural Corporation reported financial results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net sales increased 15.3% to $285.0 million from $247.2 million in the same quarter last year. Net income increased 14.5% to $52.4 million, or $0.54 per diluted share, compared with $45.8 million, or $0.46 per diluted share last year. For nine months, the company reported Net income increased 26.1% to $131.5 million, or $1.34 per diluted share, compared with $104.3 million, or $1.06 per diluted share, for the same period last year. Net sales increased 18.5% to $779.4 million from $657.8 million a year ago."
