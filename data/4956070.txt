Dolby Laboratories Inc. (NYSE: DLB) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Coding Technologies AB for $250 million net of cash on November 8, 2007. The transaction is subject to legal procedures relating to acquisitions in Sweden and is expected to close very soon. Certain minority equity interests of Coding Technologies will remain outstanding and will be subject to mandatory buyout procedures under Swedish law. JPMorgan Chase & Co acted as financial advisor to Dolby Laboratories, Inc. Baker & McKenzie, LLP acted as legal advisor to Dolby Laboratories. Mr. Peter Morawetz, Mr. Ulrich Ziche, Mr. Mats H?vermark and Ms. Anna Sofia Nilsson-Prevell of Advokatfirman Vinge acted as legal advisor to Dolby Laboratories.
