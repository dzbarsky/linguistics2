Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: CA La Electricidad de Caracas; Buyer(s): AES Corp..
The AES Corporation (NYSE:AES) has offerd to acquire 51% shares, including shares represented by American Depositary Shares, of C.A. La Electricidad de Caracas for $863 million. Under the terms of the deal, The AES Corporation will pay $0.47 per share. AES Corporation will make an offer of 926,462,000 shares and 17,719,350 ADS's. Grupo EDC received financial advice from Chase Securities, Inc., which in turn took legal counsel from Simpson Thacher & Bartlett. AES Corp. received legal counsel from law firm Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom and financial advice from Morgan Stanley Dean Witter. Shearman & Sterling provided legal advice to Morgan Stanley Dean Witter on the transaction. Burson-Marsteller acted as the public relations firm and Georgeson Shareholder Communications, Inc. the proxy solicitor for AES Corp. in the transaction.
