Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Financial Results for Its First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010.
Atheros Communications Inc. reported unaudited consolidated financial results for its first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company's revenue was a record $214.7 million, up 16% compared to $185.7 million reported in the fourth quarter of 2009. First quarter 2010 revenue increased 144% compared to $87.9 million reported in the first quarter of 2009. In accordance with U.S. GAAP, the company recorded net income of $19.7 million or $0.27 per diluted share. This compares with GAAP net income of $15.6 million or $0.24 per diluted share in the fourth quarter of 2009. Net loss in the first quarter of 2009 was $7.6 million or $0.12 per diluted share. Non-GAAP operating income was 21.4% of revenue, compared to 23.4% in the fourth quarter of 2009 and 3.2% in the first quarter of 2009.
