Tela Innovations, Inc. announced that it has received $5.5 million in funding from Cadence Design Systems Inc., KT Venture Group, LLC, QUALCOMM Ventures, and other investors..
"Tela Innovations, Inc. announced that it has raised $5.5 million in its third round of funding on December 15, 2008. The round included participation from new investors Cadence Design Systems Inc., KT Venture Group, LLC, and QUALCOMM Ventures. The round also included participation from certain existing investors of the company. On December 15, 2008, Tela Innovations, Inc. closed the transaction."
