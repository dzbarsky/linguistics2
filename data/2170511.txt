Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Walnut Creek Mining Co; Buyer(s): Kiewit Mining Group; Seller(s): ConocoPhillips.
Kiewit Construction Co. acquired the remaining 50% it did not already own in Walnut Creek Mining Co. on October 12, 2000 from Phillips Petroleum Co. In parallel transactions Phillips Petroleum Co. also sold its 50% interest in Dry Fork coal assets in Wyoming to Western Fuels-Wyoming Inc. and Phillip's coal interests in Texas, Louisiana, and Mississippi to North American Coal Corp. In all Phillips Petroleum Co. received reportedly $191 million in cash from all the three transactions. JP Morgan Chase & Co. was the financial advisor for Phillips Petroleum Co.
