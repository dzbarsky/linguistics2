Brocade Communications Systems Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended Jan. 29, 2005.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended Jan. 29, 2005. Net revenues for first quarter 2005 were $161.6 million, an increase of 4% from $155.6 million reported in the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2004, and an increase of 11% from $145.0 million reported in the first quarter of fiscal 2004. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net income for first quarter 2005 was $27.9 million, or $0.10 per share basic and diluted against a GAAP net income for fourth quarter 2004 of $20.4 million, or $0.08 per share, and GAAP net loss for first quarter 2004 of $68.8 million, or $0.27 loss per share.
