Panera Bread Co. Announces Consolidated Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 29, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2010 ; Announces Expansion Plans.
"Panera Bread Co. announced consolidated unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 29, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $335,018,000 compared to $315,195,000 for the same period a year ago. Operating profit was $31,922,000 compared to $22,721,000 for the same period a year ago. Income before income taxes was $30,511,000 compared to $22,035,000 for the same period a year ago. Net income attributable to the company was $18,894,000 or $0.61 diluted per share compared to $13,740,000 or $0.45 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported total revenue of $986,521,000 compared to $941,041,000 for the same period a year ago. Operating profit was $93,591,000 compared to $71,038,000 for the same period a year ago. Income before income taxes was $91,964,000 compared to $68,834,000 for the same period a year ago.
