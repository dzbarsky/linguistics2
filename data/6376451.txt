AK Steel Holding Corp., Annual General Meeting, May 28, 2009.
AK Steel Holding Corp., Annual General Meeting, May 28, 2009, at 13:30 Central Daylight. Location: The Ritz Carlton Hotel Chicago, 160 E. Pearson Street, Chicago, IL 60611,United States. Agenda: To elect ten directors of the company; to ratify the audit committee's appointment of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the company's independent registered public accounting firm for 2009; and to transact such other business as properly may come before the meeting.
