Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2008.
"Hansen Natural Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $50,232,000 or $0.51 per diluted share on net sales of $282,244,000 compared to the net income of $38,311,000 or $0.39 per diluted share on net sales of $244,763,000 for the same period a year ago. For six months, the company reported net income of $79,043,000 or $0.80 per diluted share on net sales of $494,422,000 compared to the net income of $58,509,000 or $0.59 per diluted share on net sales of $410,615,000 for the same period a year ago."
