Dreier LLP Announces Class Action Lawsuit Against Hansen Natural Corporation.
Dreier LLP announced that a class action lawsuit was commenced in the U.S. District Court for the Northern District of California on behalf of all purchasers of the common stock of Hansen Natural Corporation from May 23, 2007 through November 8, 2007, inclusive. The complaint charges Hansen and certain of its officers and directors with violations of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934. As a result of defendants' false statements and omissions during the Class Period, Hansen common stock traded at artificially inflated prices.
