Service Corp. International Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year 2009.
"Service Corp. International reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported operating income of $69,070,000, income from continuing operations of $23,580,000 or $0.09 per diluted share and net income attributable to common stockholders of $23,104,000 or $0.09 per diluted share on revenues of $513,949,000 compared to the operating income of $83,339,000, income from continuing operations of $31,774,000 or $0.12 per diluted share and net income attributable to common stockholders of $31,397,000 or $0.12 per diluted share on revenues of $548,782,000 for the same quarter year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities, as reported was $69.7 million and total capital expenditures were $19.0 million against net cash provided by operating activities, as reported of $70.2 million and total capital expenditures of $38.8 million for the same period in the last year.
