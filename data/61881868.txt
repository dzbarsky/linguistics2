Magma Design Automation Inc. (NASDAQ: LAVA) announced the acquisition of Silicon Metrics Corporation in a transaction valued at $32 million on October 16, 2003. Austin Ventures, L.P. and Needham Capital Partners sold their stake in the company. Magma Design paid $18 million in cash and $14 million will be paid as earnout payment. Out of initial merger consideration of $18 million, $2.6 million will be retained. Fenwick & West was the legal advisor for Magma Design Automation Inc. while JMP Securities LLC and Andrews & Kurth, LLP were the legal advisors for Silicon Metrics Corporation.
