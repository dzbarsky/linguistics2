Illumina Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
The Board of Directors of Illumina, Inc. (NasdaqGS:ILMN) authorized a share repurchase program on July 27, 2010. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $200 million of its shares. The company will repurchase $100 million worth of shares under a 10b5-1 plan over the next 12 months and the remaining $100 million at management's discretion during open trading windows.
