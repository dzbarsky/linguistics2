Service Corporation International Announces the Completion of a New$185 Million Credit Facility.
Service Corp. International the completion of a new revolving credit facility. The secured credit facility matures in July 2005 and has a total commitment of $185 million, including facilities available to be used to issue letters of credit and for the repurchase of certain currently outstanding public debt. The facilities available for the purpose of issuing letters of credit will assist the Company in releasing approximately $60 to $70 million in cash currently deposited with various third parties.
