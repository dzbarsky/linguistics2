Starbucks Reports Earnings Results for the Five-Week and Twenty Six Week Period Ended April 3, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $599 million for the five-week period ended April 3, 2005, an increase of 22% from consolidated net revenues of $493 million for the same five-week period in fiscal 2004. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 6% for the five weeks ended April 3, 2005, as compared to the same five-week period in fiscal 2004. For the 26 weeks ended April 3, 2005, the company's consolidated net revenues were $3.1 billion, an increase of 23% from consolidated net revenues of $2.5 billion for the same 26-week period in fiscal 2004. Comparable store sales increased 9% for the 26 weeks ended April 3, 2005, as compared to the same 26-week period in fiscal 2004."
