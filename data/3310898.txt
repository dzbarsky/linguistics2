Starbucks Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2006.
Starbucks Corp. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2006. For the quarter, the company reported net income fell to $117.3 million or 15 cents a share from $123.7 million or 16 cents a share a year ago. Excluding items, the company earned 17 cents a share. Total net revenue rose to $2 billion from $1.66 billion a year ago. The decline in profit is due to the adoption of new accounting requirements.
