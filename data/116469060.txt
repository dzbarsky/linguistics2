Gentex Announces Shipping Mirrors for Two New Citroen Vehicles.
Gentex Corp. has announced that it is shipping interior auto-dimming rearview mirrors for the new Citroen C4, a small family car, and the stylish DS4 high-riding coupe, produced by French automaker PSA Peugeot Citroen. The new C4 is sold throughout the European Union, Japan, Korea, the Middle East, South Africa, Australia, and New Zealand. The new C4 offers an auto-dimming mirror as standard equipment on the top trim level. This feature is standard or optional on the middle trim levels in certain markets. The new DS4 will initially be sold in Europe and features an interior base feature auto-dimming mirror on the top two trim levels. The mirror is also available as part of a package on the introductory trim level.
