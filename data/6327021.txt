Atheros Communications Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter of 2009.
Atheros Communications Inc. provided earnings guidance for the first quarter of 2009. For the quarter, the company currently anticipate revenue to be down sequentially 12% to 18% and gross margins to be in the range of 48.5 to 49.5% and once again well above long-term target model. The company also anticipate EPS to be in a range of $0.03 to $0.05 based on fully diluted shares outstanding of 62.2 million.
