Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010.
Atheros Communications Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $238.2 million, up 11% compared to $214.7 million reported in the first quarter of 2010. Revenue increased 112% compared to $112.2 million reported in the second quarter of 2009. In accordance GAAP, the company recorded net income of $29.7 million or $0.41 per diluted share. This compares to GAAP net income of $19.7 million or $0.27 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2010. Net loss in the second quarter of 2009 was $0.3 million or $0.00 per diluted share. Income from operations was $30.6 million compared to loss from operations of $1.5 million for the same period of last year. Non-GAAP net income was $49.2 million or $0.67 per diluted share compared to $40.7 million or $0.57 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2010 and $12.3 million or $0.20 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2009.
