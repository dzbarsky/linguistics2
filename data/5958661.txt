Starbucks Introduces Starbucks Thanksgiving Blend.
Starbucks Corp. has introduced Starbucks Thanksgiving Blend, a new, limited edition coffee created by Starbucks coffee experts in collaboration with Tom Douglas, a chef and culinary entrepreneur. The new coffee blend will be available from November 4, 2008 at Starbucks stores across the US. According to the company, Starbucks Thanksgiving Blend is a flavorful combination of full-bodied beans from Sumatra, with hints of herbs, and beans from Guatemala.
