Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: AES Corp., Greystone Gas-Fired Peaker Assets; Buyer(s): Tenaska Power Equipment, LLC; Seller(s): AES Corp.; Transaction Size: $36 mm.
AES Corp. announced the sale of the Greystone gas-fired peaker assets currently under construction in Haywood County, Tennessee to Tenaska Power Equipment, LLC. for $36 million including cash and assumption of certain obligations.
