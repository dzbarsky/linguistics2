Fidelity National Information Services Forms Strategic Alliance with Actimize.
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. has formed a strategic alliance with Actimize Inc. to deliver risk case management across the enterprise. This alliance is expected to provide institutions an advantage by enabling them to connect and investigate identified and suspected fraud activities across their organizations, replacing traditional information silos and other disconnected approaches. The alliance is unique in its ability to deliver enterprise fraud and compliance investigation and management capabilities across the entire financial services marketplace, from small community-based institutions to retail banks independent of the core platform or payment infrastructure.
