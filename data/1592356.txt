Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending June 30, 2005 ; Declares Dividend ; Plans to Realign Part of Manufacturing Capacity ; Announces Board Changes ; Increases Repurchase of Shares Up to $250 Million.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine onths ended March 31, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $612.8 million increased 7.7% from last year's third fiscal quarter of $569.2 million. Net income was $44.8 million decreased 1.4% from last year's third fiscal quarter of $45.5 million. Earnings per share were $0.24 against $0.24 for last year's third quarter. For the quarter, the company declared a dividend of $0.0375 per share against $0.0250 per share a year ago"
