Health Net's Fourth Quarter 2001 Eps Rise 22 Percent To $0.45 ; Anticipates 2002 Earnings.
"Health Net Inc. reported earnings for the fourth quarter of 2001. In the fourth quarter of 2001, total revenues for Health Net rose 5.3 percent to $2,484,694,000, compared with $2,359,805,000 in the fourth quarter of 2000. In the fourth quarter of 2001, Health Net recorded net income of $55,993,000, or $.45 per diluted share, compared with net income of $46,226,000, or $.37 per diluted share in the fourth quarter of 2000. The company currently anticipates that earnings for 2002"
