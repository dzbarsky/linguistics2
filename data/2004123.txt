Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Stimsonite Corporation; Buyer(s): Avery Dennison Corp.; Transaction Size: $156.15 mm.
Avery Dennison Corporation (NYSE: AVY; PSE) and Stimsonite Corporation (Nasdaq: STIM) jointly announced that they have signed a definitive merger agreement under which Avery Dennison will offer to acquire all of the outstanding shares of Stimsonite for $14.75 per share in cash, or a total of approximately $137 million. Avery Dennison plans to commence a cash tender offer for all outstanding shares of Stimsonite's common stock. The acquisition will be accounted for as a purchase and is expected to be funded with debt. A termination fee of $6 million will be paid by Stimsonite Corporation to Avery Dennison in case the agreement is terminated. Merrill Lynch & Co. acted as financial advisor to Stimsonite Corporation in connection with the transaction, and Latham & Watkins acted as legal advisor to Avery Dennison Corporation.
