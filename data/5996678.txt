Discovery Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008.
"Discovery Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $134 million or $0.44 per diluted share on total revenues of $845 million compared to net income of $7 million or $0.03 per diluted share on total revenues of $15 million for the same period last year. Net income from continuing operations was $94 million or $0.31 per diluted share compared to net income from continuing operations was $2 million or $0.03 per diluted share reported for the same period last year. For the nine months, the company reported net income of $211 million or $0.74 per diluted share on total revenues of $2,539 million compared to net income of $102 million or $0.36 per diluted share on total revenues of $59 million for the same period last year.
