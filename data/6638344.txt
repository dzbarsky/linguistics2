Eaton Vance Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended April 30, 2009; Reports Impairment Losses for the Second Quarter of 2009.
"Eaton Vance Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended April 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported earnings per basic and diluted share of $0.22 in the second quarter of fiscal 2009 compared to earnings per basic and diluted share of $0.21 in the first quarter of fiscal 2009 and $0.43 in the second quarter of fiscal 2008. Total revenue was $198.4 million compared to $273.4 million a year ago. Operating Income was $45.1 million compared to $96.1 million a year ago. Income before income taxes, non-controlling interest and equity in net income of affiliates was $37.9 million compared to $90.7 million a year ago. Net Income was $25.8 million on total revenues of $198.4 million, compared to net income of $53.2 million on total revenues of $273.4 million, a year ago. Adjusted operating income was $55 million compared to $104.7 million a year ago. For the six months, the company's net Income was $50.5 million or $0.42 per diluted share on total revenues of $407.9 million,"
