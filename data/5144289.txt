Dolby Laboratories Revised its Earnings Guidance for Full Year Ending September 2008.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. revised its earnings guidance for full year ending September 2008. For the period, the company raised its earnings per share and revenue estimates. The company expects earnings per share to fall between $1.34 and $1.44. It expects revenue for the fiscal year to range from $575 million and $615 million. In November, the company expected to earn $1.27 to $1.37 per share on $560 million to $600 million in revenue in the current fiscal year.
