Service Corp. International Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2009 and Full Years of 2009 and 2010.
Service Corp. International reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to common stockholders of $31.2 million or $0.12 per basic and diluted common share on revenue of $497.2 million, compared to net income attributable to common stockholders of $14.6 billion or $0.06 per basic and diluted common share on revenue of $516.4 million, for the same period in 2008. Operating income was $78.0 million compared to $49.0 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations excluding special items were $32.4 million or $0.13 per diluted common share, compared to earnings from continuing operations excluding special items of $23.9 billion or $0.09 per diluted common share a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities and net cash provided by operating activities, excluding special items was $94.2 million compared to $116.9 million a year ago.
