Kirby Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for Third Quarter and Full Year of 2007.
"Kirby Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $30,137,000, or $0.56 per share, compared with net earnings of $23,643,000, or $0.44 per share, for the 2006 second quarter. Consolidated revenues for the 2007 second quarter were a record $288,008,000, an increase of 18% over $243,292,000 reported for the 2006 second quarter. The company reported EBITDA of $74,560,000 against $57,015,000 for the same period earlier. For the first six months, the company reported net earnings of $54,559,000, or $1.02 per share, compared with $46,154,000, or $0.87 per share, for the first six months of 2006. Consolidated revenues for the 2007 first six months were a record $562,219,000, a 20% increase compared with $468,195,000 for the first half of 2006.
