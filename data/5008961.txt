Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
The Board of Directors of Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. (NYSE:PVH) authorized a share repurchase plan on November 30, 2007. Under the plan, the company intends to repurchase up to $200 million worth of the companys common stock representing 8% of the companys market capitalization at the closing stock price on November 30, 2007. Share repurchases may take place through open market purchases, privately negotiated transactions, including accelerated and guaranteed share repurchase agreements, and other means. The specific timing and amount of repurchases will vary based on market conditions and other factors. The Companys authorization is effective through the end of fiscal 2008.  Repurchases shall be funded by the companys existing cash on hand. Shares repurchased shall be placed into treasury.
