KeyCorp, Annual General Meeting, May 11, 2006.
"KeyCorp, Annual General Meeting, May 11, 2006, at 09:30 US Eastern Time. Location: Portland Museum of Art, Seven Congress Square, Portland, Maine,United States. Agenda: To elect four directors to serve for three-year terms expiring in 2009; to ratify the appointment by the Audit Committee of the Board of Directors of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors for KeyCorp for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2006; and to transact such other business as may properly come before the meeting or any postponement or adjournment thereof. ."
