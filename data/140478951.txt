ONI Systems Corp. announced it expects to receive $76.87 million in funding from a group of investors..
"Optical Networks, Inc. announced that it will receive $76.87 million in fourth round of funding from a consortium of investors led by new investor Bowman Capital Management in December 1999. The company will issue 12,163,418 shares of series G convertible preferred stock at $6.32 per share. Investors in the transaction will also include new investor Integral Capital and other investors Amerindo Investment Advisors, J. & W. Seligman, Mohr, Davidow Ventures, Brocade Communications Systems, Inc., Juniper Networks, Inc., E-TEK Dynamics, and Williams Communications. The round brings the total funding raised till date by ONI Systems Corp. to $125 million. On January 22, 2000, Optical Networks, Inc. invested in the transaction."
