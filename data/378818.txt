Harsco Corp. Reports Second Quarter 2002 Results.
Harsco Corp. reported net income of $26.2 million, or 64 cents a share, for the quarter that ended June 30. In the second quarter last year, net income totaled $24.7 million, or 62 cents a share. Analysts had been expecting second-quarter earnings of 68 cents a share. That offset a gain of $1.8 million, or 4 cents a share, from the sale of Harsco's Capitol Manufacturing operations. Sales in the second quarter totaled $487 million, up 1 percent from $480 million a year earlier.
