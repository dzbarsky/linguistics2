Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Tribune Education Co.; Buyer(s): McGraw-Hill Companies Inc.; Seller(s): Tribune Co.; Transaction Size: $634.71 mm.
Tribune Co. announced to sell its subsidiary Tribune Education Co. to The McGraw-Hill Companies Inc. for $634.7 million. Tribune Education, had 1999 pro-forma revenue of $384 million. It is anticipated the transaction will be completed in the third quarter of 2000, subject to approval of The McGraw-Hill Companies' Board of Directors and obtaining necessary government approvals as required under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Act. Tribune Co. received legal advice from Larry Barden from Sidley & Austin. Salomon Smith Barney was the financial adviser for Tribune Co.
