Molex to Take 2003 Fourth Quarter guidance Updates Previous Revenue and EPS Guidance for 2003 Fiscal Fourth Quarter.
Molex Incorporated announced its fiscal fourth quarter ending June 30, 2003, of approximately $38 to $40 million. The Company now expects that revenues for the fourth quarter ending June 30, 2003, will be at the higher end of its previous guidance range of $450 to $460 million. Earnings per share after the charge are expected to be approximately $0.01 for the quarter ending June 30, 2003. Prior to the charge, earnings per share are now expected to be $0.15 to $0.16 as compared to the previous guidance of $0.14.
