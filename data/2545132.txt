KeyCorp Increases Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on March 15, 2006.
The Board of Directors of KeyCorp increased the quarterly cash dividend to $0.345 from $0.325 per common share. On an annualized basis, the increase would raise Key's dividend to $1.38. The dividend is payable on March 15, 2006, to shareholders of record on Feb. 28, 2006.
