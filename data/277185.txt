Phillips-Van Heusen Corporation Reports 40% Improvement in 2002 Third Quarter Net Income.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corporation reported third quarter net income of $17.7 million, or $0.63 per diluted share, an increase of 40% over last year's third quarter net income of $12.6 million, or $0.45 per diluted share. For the nine months, net income was $24.7 million, or $0.88 per diluted share, in the current year compared with $20.1 million, or $0.72 per diluted share, last year. Third quarter net sales were $409.1 million in the current year, an increase of 1% over the prior year's $405.0 million. For the nine months, net sales were $1,089.7 million in the current year, compared with $1,106.3 million last year.
