Fidelity National Information Services Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Financial Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2009.
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. reported unaudited consolidated financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. Consolidated revenue of $797.8 million declined 3.9% compared to $830.3 million in the first quarter of 2008. Non-GAAP adjusted net earnings increased 19.2% to $0.31 per share, compared to $0.26 in the prior year. The increase is attributable to improved operating performance, lower interest expense and a lower share count, partially offset by a slightly higher tax rate. GAAP net earnings from continuing operations attributable to common stockholders totaled $34.3 million, or $0.18 per share compared to $0.06 per share in the prior period. Adjusted EBITDA increased 0.7% to $181.2 million in the first quarter of 2009 compared to $180.0 million in the 2008 quarter. The adjusted EBITDA margin improved 100 basis points to 22.7% compared to 21.7% in the prior-year quarter, driven by increased operating leverage and ongoing expense management.
