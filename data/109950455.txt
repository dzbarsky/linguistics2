Illumina Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 4, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Year 2010.
Illumina Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended July 4, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $212,003,000 compared to $161,643,000 for the same period a year ago. Income from operations was $47,428,000 compared to $36,102,000 for the same period a year ago. Income before income taxes was $46,526,000 compared to $35,023,000 for the same period a year ago. Net income was $29,796,000 or $0.21 diluted per share compared to $24,688,000 or $0.18 diluted per share for the same period a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $74,796,000 compared to $39,369,000 for the same period a year ago. Purchases of property and equipment were $13,142,000 compared to $14,022,000 for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $34,036,000 or $0.26 diluted per share compared to $29,166,000 or $0.22 diluted per share for the same period a year ago.
