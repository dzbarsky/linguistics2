Cephalon Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter 2010.
Cephalon Inc. provided earnings guidance for the second quarter 2010. For the period, the company anticipates that sales will be between $705-715 million, up from the previous sales guidance of $645-670 million, resulting in basic adjusted income per common share for the second quarter 2010 of between $2.17-2.22, up from the previous guidance of $1.65-1.75.
