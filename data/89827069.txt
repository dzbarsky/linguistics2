News Corp. Announces Consolidated Financial Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended December 31, 2009; Announces Impairment Charges for the Second Quarter of Fiscal 2010; Declares Dividend on Class A Share and Class B Share Payable on April 14, 2010.
News Corp. announced consolidated financial results for the second quarter and six months ended December 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported revenues of $8,684 million, income before income tax of $421 million, net income of $284 million, net income attributable to News Corporation stockholders of $254,000 or $0.10 per diluted share against revenues of $7,871 million, loss before income tax of $7,905 million, net loss of $6,391 million, net loss attributable to News Corporation stockholders of $6,417 million or $2.45 per diluted share a year ago. These results include a one-time $500 million pre-tax charge ($315 million or $0.12 per share, net of tax) related to the settlement of litigation filed against the Company's Integrated Marketing Services business.
