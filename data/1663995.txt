Brocade Launches Partner-Ready Professional Services and Support Offerings.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. introduced a comprehensive suite of professional services and support offerings designed to supplement partner and customer expertise and resources in designing, installing, operating, and supporting shared storage infrastructures. The new Brocade Enterprise Support Service provides options for flexible and rapid support of enterprise storage infrastructure. It includes Brocade Enterprise Supplemental Support and Brocade Onsite Resident Engineer Staffing. The Brocade Enterprise Supplemental Support program provides direct access to Brocade expertise for organizations with existing support contracts from their system providers. Brocade Onsite Resident Engineering offering is available for organizations that need assistance with day-to-day management, design, and planning reviews, best practices implementation and standardization, and operational expertise.
