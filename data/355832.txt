Starbucks Reports Strong 2002 Third Quarter Results.
"Starbucks Corp. announced revenues and earnings for the third quarter of fiscal 2002 and raised its long-term store target from 20,000 to 25,000 locations worldwide. For the third quarter ended June 30, 2002, consolidated net revenues increased 26 percent to $835 million from $663 million for the third quarter of fiscal 2001. Retail revenues increased 27 percent to $712 million and specialty revenues increased 19 percent to $123 million. Comparable store sales increased 8 percent as compared with the third quarter of fiscal 2001. Net earnings increased by 20 percent to $56.2 million, or $0.14 per share, for the 13-week period ended June 30, 2002, compared to$46.8 million, or $0.12 per share, for the third fiscal quarter of 2001. For the 39 weeks ended June 30, 2002, consolidated net revenues increased 24 percent to $2.4 billion from $2.0 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001.
