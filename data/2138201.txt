Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Bell Labs Design Automation Group; Buyer(s): Cadence Design Systems Inc.; Seller(s): Lucent Technologies Inc.; Transaction Size: $58 mm.
"Lucent Technologies, Inc. has entered into an agreement to sell its Bell Labs Integrated Circuit Design Automation Group (BLDA) to Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN). Under the terms of the multi-faceted agreement, Cadence will engage in a three-year relationship involving continued provision of BLDA electronic design automation services to Lucent Technologies. As a result of the acquisition, more than 100 BLDA employees, will join Cadence Design Systems. Cadence Design Systems, Inc. completed the acquisition of Bell Labs Integrated Circuit Design Automation Group in September 1998 for a cash purchase price of $58 million. The transaction was accounted for as a purchase."
