Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended June 30, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter of September 2009 ; Announces Impairments Charges.
"Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $219,740,000, or $1.27 per diluted share on net revenues of $570,589,000 against net income of $52,611,000 or $0.29 per diluted share on net revenues of $871,892,000 for the same period a year ago. Loss from operations was $216,058,000 against income of $84,483,000 for the same period a year ago. Loss before income taxes was $215,289,000 against income of $90,976,000 for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP pretax profit was $1,338,000. For the full year, the company reported net loss of $321,287,000, or $1.84 per diluted share on net revenues of $2,581,841,000 against net income of $215,437,000 or $1.19 per diluted share on net revenues of $3,328,347,000 for the same period a year ago.
