Panera Bread Co. Announces Sales Results for the Month of December 2005; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter 2005; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2005.
"Panera Bread Co. announced sales results for the month of December 2005. The company reported a 7.2% increase in same-store sales for December, ahead of the company's target. In December only, systemwide same-store sales, or sales at bakery and cafes open for at least 18 months, increased 7.2%, above the company's targeted range of 5.3% to 5.8%. The company provided earnings guidance for the full year 2005. The Panera's full-year profit outlook to $1.64 to $1.65 per share, an increase of 31% to 32% from fiscal 2004."
