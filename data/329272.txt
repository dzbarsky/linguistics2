Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Finalizes Appointment of KPMG as Independent Accountant.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that KPMG has been appointed as independent accountant for the company, succeeding independent accountant Arthur Andersen LLP. KPMG was approved as Brocade's new independent accountant after careful consideration of several firms by the company's Board of Directors and Audit Committee. Brocade has notified the Securities and Exchange Commission of the change as required by federal securities law.
