Update to Eaton Vance Corp.s Equity Buyback on May 20, 2009.
On May 20, 2009, Eaton Vance Corp. announced that during the first six months of fiscal 2009, it has repurchased and retired approximately 400,000 shares of its non-voting common stock. As of April 30, 2009, the company has repurchased 5,538,143 shares for a total cost of $225.04 million under the program.
