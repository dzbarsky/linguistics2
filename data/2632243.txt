Fidelity National Information Services Inc. Provides Earnings Outlook for the Full Year 2006.
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. provided earnings outlook for the full year 2006. The company expects pro forma fiscal year 2006 diluted EPS of $1.50 to $1.55 compared to $1.28 pro forma diluted earnings per share in 2005, and pro forma diluted cash earnings per share of $2.11 to $2.17, compared to $1.92 pro forma diluted cash earnings per share in 2005.
