Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Mitsui Bussan Digital Corp., Emulation Division; Buyer(s): Cadence Design Systems Inc.; Seller(s): Mitsui Bussan Digital Corp.
Mitsui Bussan Digital Corporation sold its emulation division to Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN). The financial terms of the deal were not disclosed. The acquisition is expected to enhance Cadence's Quickturn product portfolio.
