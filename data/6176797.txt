Update to Kimberly-Clark Corporation's Equity Buyback on December 31, 2008.
On January 26, 2009, Kimberly-Clark Corporation announced that during the quarter ended December 31, 2008, it repurchased 1,300,000 shares at an aggregate cost of $75 million. As of December 31, 2008, the company has repurchased 16,732,411 shares for a total cost of $1.2 billion, under the program.
