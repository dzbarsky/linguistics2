AK Steel Holding Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 30, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2007.
"AK Steel Holding Corp. reported earnings results for the nine months ended September 30, 2007. For the first nine months of 2007, the company's net income was $281.0 million, or $2.51 per share. In the comparable 2006 period, net income was $61.3 million, or $0.55 per share. Sales for the first nine months of 2007 were a record $5,311.1 million, compared to $4,486.8 million in the first nine months of 2006. The company said it expects to report an operating profit of approximately $70 per ton in the fourth quarter of 2007."
