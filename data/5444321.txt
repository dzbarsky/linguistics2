Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2008.
Hansen Natural Corporation reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2008. The company reported gross sales for the 2008 first quarter increased 28.4% to $244.0 million, from $190.1 million a year earlier. Net sales for the first quarter increased 27.9% to $212.2 million from $165.9 million a year ago. Gross and net sales for the quarter were impacted by purchases made by customers in the fourth quarter of 2007 in advance of the price increase, effective January 1, 2008 for Monster Energy brand energy drinks in 16-ounce cans and for the Java Monster line of non-carbonated dairy-based coffee drinks. Net income for the 2008 first quarter increased 42.6% to $28.8 million, or $0.29 per diluted share, compared with $20.2 million, or $0.21 per diluted share for the first quarter last year.
