Illumina Inc. Appoints Stephen Pentoney as Vice President of Array Biochemistry.
Illumina Inc. appointed Stephen Pentoney as Vice President of Array Biochemistry reporting to Joel McComb, Senior Vice President and General Manager of company's Life Sciences Business Unit. In Stephen's new role, he is responsible for leading Illumina's assay and application development efforts. Prior to joining the company, Stephen held multiple positions at Beckman Coulter including his most recent role as Director of Technology Management for the Molecular Diagnostics Business Center.
