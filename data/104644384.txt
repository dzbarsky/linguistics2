Dolby Laboratories Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended March 26, 2010; Provides Earning Guidance for the Full Year of 2010.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended March 26, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to company of $85.89 million or $0.74 per diluted share on total revenue of $423.42 million compared to net income attributable to company of $69.45 million or $0.60 per diluted share on total revenue of $204.12 million for the same period a year ago. Net income including controlling interest was $86.32 million compared to $69.79 million for the same period a year ago. Income before provision for income taxes was $133.93 million compared to $108.22 million for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $132.08 million compared to $105.51 million for the same period a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $86.02 million compared to $32.64 million for the same period a year ago.
