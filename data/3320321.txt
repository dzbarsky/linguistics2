Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter of 2006.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter of 2006. The company reported that fourth-quarter net income rose, as revenue gained, to $20 million, or 7 cents a share, from $1.06 million, or breakeven on a per-share basis, during the same period in the prior year. Before items, quarterly per-share income rose to 14 cents from 7 cents in the prior year. The company said quarterly revenue rose to $208.8 million from $145.5 million.
