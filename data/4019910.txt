Gentex Corp. Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2007.
Gentex Corp. announced earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2007. For the quarter its net sales increased by 13% from $139.0 million in the first quarter of 2006 to a record $157.2 million in the first quarter of 2007. Current first quarter net income increased by 12% to $29.5 million compared with $26.4 million in the first quarter last year. Earnings per diluted share increased by 22% to 21 cents in the first quarter of 2007 compared with 17 cents in the first quarter of 2006.
