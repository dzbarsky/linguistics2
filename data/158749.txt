Cephalon Inc. Reports Third Quarter 2002 Financial Results.
Cephalon Inc. reported results for the third quarter of 2002 which included total revenue of $130.4 million, product sales of $122.4 million, and diluted earnings per share of $0.35. The total revenue of $130.4 million in the third quarter of 2002 was a 56 percent increase compared to total revenue of $83.8 million in the third quarter of 2001.
