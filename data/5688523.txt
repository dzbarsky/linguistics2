Principal Financial Group Inc. Appoints Naim Munir as Vice President and Chief Medical Officer.
Principal Financial Group Inc. announced that Naim Munir, M.D. has been named to the newly created position of vice president and chief medical officer. He will have direct oversight of all areas of medical management with a focus on prevention, quality and cost-effective care delivery. Munir most recently served as executive medical director for quality and care management at Horizon Blue Cross and Blue Shield of New Jersey.
