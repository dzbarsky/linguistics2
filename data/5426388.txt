Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended March 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Year 2008.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended March 30, 2008. For the second quarter, the company reported total revenue of $172.6 million, compared to $129.0 million for the second quarter of fiscal 2007, an increase of 34%. Second-quarter net income was $56.8 million, or $0.49 per diluted share, compared to $39.1 million, or $0.34 per diluted share, for the second quarter of fiscal 2007. The company now expects fiscal 2008 revenue to be $585 million to $615 million. Net income is now expected to be $170 million to $182 million. Earnings per diluted share is now expected to be $1.47 to $1.57."
