Health Net Inc. Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010.
Health Net Inc. reported consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. The company announced 2010 first quarter GAAP net income of $16.1 million, or $0.16 per basic and diluted share, compared with $22.0 million, or $0.21 per basic and diluted share, for the first quarter of 2009. The GAAP results include: $37.7 million in pretax losses from run-off and other operations related to the companys Northeast businesses that were divested on December 11, 2009; and $14.5 million in pretax losses from the companys operations strategy cost reduction efforts and other expenses. Total revenues decreased 13.1% in the first quarter of 2010 to $3.4 billion from $3.9 billion in the first quarter of 2009. Investment income decreased to $19.9 million in the first quarter of 2010 compared with $24.3 million in the first quarter of 2009. Income from operations before income taxes was $26.594 million against $24.095 million of previous year period. Net cash provided by operating activities was $127.
