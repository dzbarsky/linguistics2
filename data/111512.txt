Brocade OEM and Channel Partners Deliver Brocade SilkWorm 3200 Entry-Level Intelligent Fabric Switch and Pre-Certified SAN Solutions to Market.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that twelve of its Original Equipment Manufacturer partners and over twenty channel partners are now offering to market the Brocade SilkWorm 3200, the industry's first 8-port, 2 Gigabit per second intelligent fabric switch designed to meet the price and performance requirements of entry-level SAN customers. Using Brocade SilkWorm fabric switches, companies can network servers with storage devices through a SAN, creating a highly available, scalable, secure, and manageable environment for storage applications.
