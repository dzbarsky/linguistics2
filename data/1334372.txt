Starbucks Corp. Reports Results for the Four Week and 17 Weeks Ended January 30, 2005.
"Starbucks Corp. reports results for the four week and 17 weeks ended January 30, 2005. For the four weeks ended January 30, 2005 it reported consolidated net revenues of $452 million against consolidated net revenues of $368 million for the same period a year ago. Comparable store sales increased 7% against comparable store sales for the same period a year ago. For the 17 weeks ended January 30, 2005 it reported consolidated net revenues of $2.0 billion against consolidated net revenues of $1.6 billion for the same period a year ago, an increase of 24%. Comparable store sales increased 10% against comparable store sales for the same period a year ago."
