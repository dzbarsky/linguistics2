New California Academy of Sciences Installs Dolby Laboratories Inc.'s Dolby 3D Digital Cinema System.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. announced that its Dolby(R) 3D Digital Cinema system has been installed at the new California Academy of Sciences, a combination aquarium, planetarium, natural history museum and scientific research institution located in San Francisco's Golden Gate Park. The California Academy of Sciences and the veteran media and technology consultants at Visual Acuity chose the Dolby 3D system for the new Hearst Forum's 3D theater. The theater is currently screening Bugs!, an educational 3D movie complementing the Academy's rainforest exhibit.
