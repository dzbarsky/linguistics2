Big Lots Inc. Revised Earnings Guidance for Fiscal 2009.
Big Lots Inc. revised earnings guidance for fiscal 2009. For fiscal 2009, the company now sees continuing operations generating $1.90 to $2 a share of profit, against an adjusted $1.41 for fiscal 2008. That's up from previous guidance for $1.80 to $1.90 and is in line to above the mean analyst estimate for $1.90. Same-store sales for the year should rise 2%.
