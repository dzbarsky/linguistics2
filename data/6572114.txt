Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 4, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter and Full Year of 2009; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended April 4, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported loss from operations of $48,416,000, loss before provision for income taxes of $61,613,000 and net loss of $63,257,000 or $0.25 basic and diluted loss per share on total revenues of $206,302,000 compared to the loss from operations of $43,442,000, loss before provision for income taxes of $44,593,000 and net loss of $33,142,000 or $0.13 basic and diluted loss per share on total revenues of $270,750,000 for the same quarter year ago. For the quarter, the company reported net cash used for operating activities of $7,338,000 compared to the $19,081,000 for the same quarter year ago. Using this non-GAAP measure, net loss in the first quarter of 2009 was $25 million, or $0.10 per share on a diluted basis, as compared to net loss of $0.2 million, or $0.00 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2008.
