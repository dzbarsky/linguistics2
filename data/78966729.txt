Cadence Design Systems Inc. Announces Condensed Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended Oct. 3, 2009; Provides Outlook for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2009 ; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. announced results for the third quarter 2009. Cadence reported third quarter 2009 revenue of $216 million, compared to revenue of $232 million reported for the same period in 2008. On a GAAP basis, Cadence recognized a net loss of $14 million, or $(0.05) per share on a diluted basis, in the third quarter of 2009, compared to a net loss of $171 million, or $(0.67) per share on a diluted basis in the same period in 2008. Using this non-GAAP measure, net income in the third quarter of 2009 was $7 million, or $0.03 per share on a diluted basis, as compared to a net loss of $23 million, or $(0.09) per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2008. The company reported loss from operations of $112.335 million, loss before provision for income taxes of $139.692 million and net loss of $151.66 million or $0.59 per basic and diluted share on total revenue of $632.353 million against loss from operations of $152.058 million, loss before provision for income taxes of $176.455 million"
