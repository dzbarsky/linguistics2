AES says it plans to refinance $2 billion in Debt.
The AES Corp. announced a refinancing plan to push back the maturities of more than $2 billion in debt over the next year. AES, a major provider of electricity in the United States, Britain and Latin America, opened a $1.6 billion secured credit facility to replace bank debt expiring next year and made an offer to exchange $500 million in bonds coming due over the next eight months for cash and new secured three-year notes.
