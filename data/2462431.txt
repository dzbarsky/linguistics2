Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Announces Agreement with Nortel to Develop Branch Office Storage Solutions.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced an agreement with Nortel to develop software for Nortel's WAFS (Wide Area File Services) solutions that provide cost-effective management and protection of data in remote offices. The agreement expands an existing relationship between the companies, and is expected to include development of technology based on the Brocade Tapestry(TM) family of application infrastructure solutions. The agreement calls for Brocade to deliver software that leverage Nortel's expertise in end-to-end communications solutions for enterprises and service providers.
