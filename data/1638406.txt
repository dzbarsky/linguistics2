Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Financial Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended April 30, 2005.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported financial results for its second quarter ended April 30, 2005. For the period, the company reported net revenues were $144.8 million against revenues for the quarter decreased 1%t from $145.6 million reported in the second quarter of fiscal year 2004. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net income was $19.4 million, or $0.07 per share basic and diluted. This compares to GAAP net income for second quarter of 2004 of $1.1 to $2.2 million, or $0.00 to $0.01 per share basic and diluted. For the six months, the company reported net income of $47.4 million or $0.17 earnings per share on net revenue of 306.3 million against net loss of $0.3 to $68.2 million or $0.00 to $0.26 earnings per share on net revenue of 306.3 million for the same period a year ago."
