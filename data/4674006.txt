Update to Molex Incs Equity Buyback on August 1, 2007.
On August 1, 2007, Molex Inc announced that it has repurchased 415,000 shares of Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and 130,000 shares of Common Stock (MOLX) at a total cost of $14.9 million. As of June 30, 2007, the Company has repurchased approximately $234.8 million of its Common Stock under its stock repurchase program for $250 million announced on April 25, 2005.
