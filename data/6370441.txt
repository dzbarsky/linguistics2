AK Steel Holding Corp. Lays Off about 300 Workers at Middletown Works.
AK Steel Holding Corp. confirmed about 300 workers are on layoff at Middletown Works. Some of the layoffs are in connection with the shutdown of the Middletown blast furnace for maintenance. The company is replacing the hearth on the blast furnace, the bottom inside section of the furnace where molten iron collects. The maintenance should take about 45 days to complete.
