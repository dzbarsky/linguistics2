KeyCorp Announces Amendments to Articles of Incorporation.
KeyCorp announced that, on June 17, 2008, the company filed a Certificate of Amendment to the Articles of Incorporation with the Secretary of State of the State of Ohio, to amend its Articles of Incorporation and establish the preferences, limitations and relative rights of a new series of preferred stock designated as 7.750% Non-Cumulative Perpetual Convertible Preferred Stock, Series A, $1.00 par value, and with a liquidation preference of $100 per share.
