The McGraw-Hill Companies Inc. Reports Net Debt as on June 30, 2009 ; Provides Free Cash Flow Guidance for the Year 2009.
"The McGraw-Hill Companies Inc. reported net debt of $731 million as on June 30, 2009. The revised estimate is due to changes in scope and timing of conversions. The company expects free cash flow for the year, 2009 to be in the range of $430 million to $450 million."
