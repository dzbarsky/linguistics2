Big Lots Reports Sales for the Second Quarter Fiscal 2004.
"Big Lots Inc. announced that second-quarter fiscal 2004 same-store sales increased 0.2% over the year-earlier period, as a 2.3% rise in the value of the average basket offset a 2.1% decline in the number of customer transactions. Total sales for the quarter increased 4.9% to $985.1 million, just shy of the average analyst estimate compiled by Thomson First Call of $1.03 billion. July same-store sales fell 3.8%, as a 4.4% decline in the number of customer transactions more than offset a 0.6% increase in the value of the average basket. Total sales rose 1% to $280 million."
