Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2006.
"Atheros Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2006. For the first quarter, the company reported net income of $6,814,000 or $0.13 per diluted share on net revenue of $61,084,000 against net income of $1,669.000 or $0.03 per diluted share on net revenue of $41,233,000 for the same period last year. In the first quarter of 2006, non-GAAP net income was $7.9 million or $0.14 per diluted share, compared with non-GAAP net income of $2.1 million or $0.04 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2005. Craig Barratt, president and CEO of Atheros commented that their core WLAN business continued to be strong, especially in the PC OEM market. They shipping 802.11n products in volume with multiple customers, and expect to see significant growth in the .11n space over the next several quarters as customers make more end-products available.
