Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Ambit Design Systems, Inc.; Buyer(s): Cadence Design Systems Inc.; Transaction Size: $260 mm.
Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE:CDN) announced that it has agreed to acquire Ambit Design Systems, Inc. Under the terms of the agreement, Cadence will pay $260 million in cash and the transaction will be accounted for under the purchase method of accounting. The acquisition has been approved by the boards of directors of both companies and is subject to regulatory and Ambit shareholder approval. Steven J. Tonsfeldt of Venture Law Group and Morgan Stanley Dean Witter advised Ambit Design Systems, Inc., while Gordon K. Davidson of Fenwick & West, LLP and Goldman, Sachs & Co. advised Cadence Design Systems Inc., on the deal.
