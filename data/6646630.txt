KeyCorp Appoints Kristen Manos to Its Board.
KeyCorp announced that Kristen Manos, former president of Herman Miller's North American office business, has been elected to KeyCorp's Board of Directors. Most recently, Manos led the $1.5 billion commercial furniture business for Herman Miller, and has served as the corporate marketing executive for two furniture companies. She has also led product development, customer service, business development and manufacturing teams. Manos earned a bachelor's degree in economics from Harvard University and a master's from the Yale School of Management.
