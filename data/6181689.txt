Concho Resources Inc. Announces Retirement of Steven L. Beal as President and Chief Operating Officer, Effective from June 30, 2009.
Concho Resources Inc. announced that its President and Chief Operating Officer, Steven L. Beal has informed the Board of Directors that he intends to retire effective June 30, 2009. Mr. Beal plans to continue his association with the company as a consultant and member of its Board of Directors upon his retirement.
