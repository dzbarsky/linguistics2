Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 24, 2009.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended January 24, 2009. For the quarter, the company revenues of $431.6 million compared to revenue of $347.8 million for the same quarter a year ago. GAAP net loss was $26.0 million or $0.07 per basic and diluted share compared to net income of $19.8 million or $0.05 per basic and diluted share for the same quarter a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $63.6 million or $0.15 per diluted share compared to Non-GAAP net income of $64.2 million or $0.16 per diluted share for the same quarter a year ago. The revenue results were fueled by a healthy mix of products and services, including IP networking solutions from the recently integrated Foundry business.
