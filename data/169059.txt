Ak Steel Holding Announces Executive Changes.
Ak Steel Holding Corp. announced that both its chairman and CEO, Richard M. Wardrop, Jr., and its president, John Hritz, had resigned. The board of directors has started looking for a new chief executive but in the meantime has named AK Steel CFO, James Wainscott, as acting CEO. Wainscott joined the company in 1995 as vice president and treasurer.
