Update to Molex Inc.'s Equity Buyback on June 30, 2008.
As of June 30, 2008 the company has repurchased 6,705,500 shares of Class A common stock and 1,400,000 shares of common stock for an aggregate amount of $199.6 million under the program. During the quarter, the company repurchased 1,054,500 shares of Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and 175,000 shares of Common Stock (MOLX) at a total cost of $33.4 million.
