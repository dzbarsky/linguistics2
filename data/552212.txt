Phillips-Van Heusen Corporation Reports 2004 First Quarter Results; Provides outlook for the second quarter and full year of 2004.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported first quarter net income of $1.6 million which, after deducting preferred stock dividends, resulted in a net loss of $0.12 per diluted common share. net income in the current year's first quarter improved to $11.1 million, or $0.18 per diluted common share, which was $0.05 ahead of the Company's previous earnings guidance and the First Call consensus estimate. In the prior year's first quarter, net loss was $2.2 million, or $0.22 per diluted common share. Excluding restructuring and other items, net income in the prior year's first quarter was $7.8 million, or $0.11 per diluted common share. Total revenues in the first quarter decreased 1% to $381.3 million from $383.7 million in the prior year. Given the company's first quarter results, the company is raising its 2004 earnings per share guidance (excluding restructuring and other items) to a range of $1.13 to $1.18, with second quarter earnings in the range of $0.24 to $0.25 per share.
