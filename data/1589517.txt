AK Steel Holding Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2005; Provides Outlook for the Second Quarter of 2005.
"AK Steel Holding Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2005. For the period, the company reported net income of $59.2 million, or $0.54 per share of common stock, against net income of $165.4 million, or $1.52 per share, which included an after-tax gain of $174.9, or $1.61 per share, on the sale of a non-core asset for the same period a year ago. Net sales for the quarter were $1,422.5 million on shipments of 1,520,500 tons against net sales and shipments of $1,134.4 million and 1,514,300 tons for the same period a year ago. Operating profit was $113.6 million, or $75 per ton, substantially higher than the $1 per ton earned in the first quarter of 2004. The company announced that it expects second quarter shipments to be approximately 1,600,000 tons, reflecting continued strong shipments to carbon, stainless and electrical markets and no significant planned maintenance outages.
