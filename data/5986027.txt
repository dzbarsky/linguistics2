AK Steel Holding Corp. Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2008.
AK Steel Holding Corp. revised earnings guidance for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008. The company reacting to weak economic conditions by lowering expectations for shipments and operating profit in the fourth quarter. The West Chester-based steel maker expects fourth-quarter shipments of about 1.2 million tons, compared with previous guidance of 1.4 million tons. The company also expects operating profit to be less than the $100 per ton it predicted when it released its third-quarter results in October, but declined to specify the projected decline.
