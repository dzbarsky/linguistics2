Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: AK-ISG Steel Coating Company; Buyer(s): AK Steel Holding Corp..
AK Steel Holding Corp. purchased AK-ISG Steel Coating Company. Terms were not disclosed. L-S Electro-Galvanizing Company had been a venture between the former LTV Corporation and Sumitomo Metal USW Corporation prior to ISG's acquisition of LTV's steelmaking assets in April.
