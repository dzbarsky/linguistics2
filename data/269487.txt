Brocade Silkworm Fabric Application Platform Rapidly Embraced By Storage Software Companies.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. the world's leading provider of infrastructure solutions for Storage Area Networks, has announced that seven storage management companies, including Alacritus Software, CommVault Systems, FalconStor Software, Incipient Inc. InterSAN Inc. StoreAge Networking Technologies Inc. and Topio have selected the Brocade SilkWorm(R) Fabric Application Platform on which to design new, fabric-based storage management software, demonstrating the rapidly growing industry support for fabric applications.
