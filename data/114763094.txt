Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Announces Change of CEO.
Fidelity National Financial, Inc. announced that Chief Executive Officer Alan L. Stinson has stepped down from that role, but will remain with the company as an Executive Vice President. Additionally, Chief Operating Officer George P. Scanlon has been named the company's new Chief Executive Officer. Stinson will remain with the company as an Executive Vice President, reporting to Chairman Bill Foley. He will focus on oversight and board membership for the companys portfolio companies, with an emphasis on accounting and finance activities, FNF investor relations and aiding in mergers and acquisitions activities.
