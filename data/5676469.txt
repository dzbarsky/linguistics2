Update to Fidelity National Financial, Inc.'s Equity Buyback on June 30, 2008.
As of June 30, 2008, Fidelity National Financial, Inc. has repurchased 11,875,000 shares for an aggregate value of $216.19 million, under the program announced in October 2006. Till May 7, 2008, the company had repurchased 10,025,000 shares for a total cost of $188.89 million.
