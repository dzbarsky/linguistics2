Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 27, 2008.
Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the Nine Months Ended September 27, 2008. For the period, the company reported net loss of $215,083,000 or $0.84 per diluted share on total revenue of $811,279,000, compared to net income of $176,749,000 or $0.60 per diluted share on total revenue of $1,157,070,000 for the same period last year.
