Fidelity National Signs Reinsurance Deals with Commonwealth Land Title Insurance, Lawyers Title Insurance, Landamerica NJ Title Insurance and United Capital Title Insurance.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. (FNF) has signed title insurance reinsurance agreements with LandAmerica Financial Group's (LFG) two principal title insurance underwriters, Commonwealth Land Title Insurance and Lawyers Title Insurance, as well as LandAmerica NJ Title Insurance and United Capital Title Insurance. Chicago Title Insurance will reinsure policies underwritten by Commonwealth and LandAm NJ and Fidelity National Title Insurance will reinsure policies underwritten by Lawyers and United during the transition period between the signing of the stock purchase agreement and the closing of the transaction.
