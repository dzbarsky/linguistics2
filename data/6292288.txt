Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Impairment Charges for the Year Ended December 31, 2008.
Hansen Natural Corporation reported impairment charges for the year ended December 31, 2008. At December 31, 2008, the company held auction rate securities with a face value of $112.5 million. The company determined that an impairment of $14.9 million had occurred at December 31, 2008, of which $14.4 million was deemed temporary and $0.5 million was deemed other than temporary.
