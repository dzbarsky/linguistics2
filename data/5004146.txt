Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended October 27, 2007.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended October 27, 2007. For the quarter, the company earned $32 million, or 8 cents per share compared with profit of $20 million, or 7 cents per share, in the fourth quarter last year. On an adjusted basis, earnings rose to $66.5 million, or 16 cents per share, from $49.5 million or 12 cents per share in the year-ago quarter. Revenue surged 63% to $340 million from $208.8 million. Profit surged 60% on strong product and services revenue and its acquisition of McData Corp. The quarter included results from McData, which the company bought for $973 million in January 2007. For the full year, profit climbed 14% to $76.9 million, or 21 cents per share, from $67.6 million, or 25 cents per share. Revenue jumped 65% to $1.24 billion from $750.6 million."
