Kimberly-Clark Corp. completed the acquisition of Linostar Spa from Bolton Group B.V..
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. (NYSE: KMB) entered into a definitive agreement to acquire Linostar Spa from Bolton Group B.V. on January 24, 2001. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. The completion of the transaction is expected to occur shortly. Kimberly-Clark Corp. completed the acquisition of Linostar Spa from Bolton Group B.V. on January 31, 2001."
