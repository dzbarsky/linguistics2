Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2010; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Hansen Natural Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company's net sales decreased 2.5% to $238.1 million from $244.2 million in the same period a year ago. Net income was $32.6 million, or $0.35 per diluted share, compared with $41.6 million, or $0.44 per diluted share in the same quarter last year. Operating income was $50.78 million against $65.77 million a year ago. Income before provision for income taxes was $51.77 million against $63.25 million a year ago. The company determined that an impairment of $7.0 million existed at March 31, 2010, of which $3.0 million was deemed temporary and $4.0 million was deemed other-than-temporary."
