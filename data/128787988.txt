Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Enters into Licensing Agreement with Comfort Revolution.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. and Comfort Revolution announced they have entered into a licensing agreement under which Comfort Revolution will market and distribute memory foam bedding and stadium seat cushions under PVH's IZOD brand. The licensing agreement has an initial term of over two years and provides for a three year renewal at PVH's option. The collection will debut at the Home Textiles Market Week during the week of March 14, and will be distributed at major department stores and specialty stores throughout the United States. The IZOD bedding line includes non-coil memory foam mattresses, mattress toppers and pillows, will incorporate IZOD branded packaging, and will be available in twin, queen, and king sizes.
