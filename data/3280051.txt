AES Corp. won a bid to acquire a 75% stake in Kyivoblenergo from the State Property Fund for a reported value of approximately UAH 248.686 million in cash on April 17, 2001. The starting price of the stake was UAH 174.032 million. On May 4, 2001, AES Corp. signed an agreement to acquire a 75% stake in Kyivoblenergo from the State Property Fund on the same terms.
