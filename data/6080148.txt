Principal Financial Group Inc. Plans to Cut 300 Jobs at its Headquarters and Around 250 in 45 Other Locations.
Principal Financial Group Inc. announced that it is cutting 300 jobs at its headquarters and around 250 in 45 other locations. The insurance, banking, retirement and asset management company blamed the cuts on continued deterioration of U.S. and global markets. The cuts represents about 3.5% of Principal's work force. The company announced that most affected employees will leave December 31, 2008. All affected employees will receive severance and career assistance.
