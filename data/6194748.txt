Illumina Inc. Announces Unaudited Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended December 28, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Fiscal Year 2009.
Illumina Inc. announced unaudited earnings results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended December 28, 2008. For the fourth quarter of 2008, the company reported revenue of $160.9 million, a 43% increase over the $112.6 million reported in the fourth quarter of 2007 and a 7% increase over revenue of $150.3 million in the third quarter of 2008. This represents the Company's 30th consecutive quarter of revenue growth. The Company reported GAAP net income for the quarter of $28.9 million, or $0.22 per diluted share, compared to a GAAP net loss of $4.1 million, or 0.04per basic and diluted share in the comparable period of 2007. The GAAP net loss in the fourth quarter of 2007 was attributable to the recognition of litigation settlement expenses.
