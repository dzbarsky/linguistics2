Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Dry Fork Mine; Buyer(s): Western Fuels-Wyoming, Inc; Seller(s): ConocoPhillips.
Western Fuels-Wyoming Inc. acquired the remaining 50% in the Dry Fork mine on October 12, 2000 from Phillips Petroleum Co. In parallel transactions Phillips Petroleum Co. also sold its 50% interest in Walnut Creek Mining Co. to Kiewit Construction Co. and Phillip's coal interests in Texas, Louisiana, and Mississippi to North American Coal Corp. In all Phillips Petroleum Co. received reportedly $191 million in cash from all the three transactions. JP Morgan Chase & Co. was the financial advisor for Phillips Petroleum Co.
