Dolby Laboratories Opens Office in Taiwan.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. announced that it has opened a new office in Taipei, Taiwan. The Taiwan office will house sales, support, and business development personnel for the digital entertainment technology company. Dolby has been supporting consumer electronics manufacturers in Taiwan for more than 30 years. The new Taipei office joins the recently opened Seoul office as the latest additions to Dolby's growing presence in Asia, which also includes offices in Shanghai, Beijing, Hong Kong, and Tokyo.
