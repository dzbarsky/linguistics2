Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended April 30, 2011.
Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended April 30, 2011. For the quarter, the company recorded revenues of $550 million, representing an increase of 9.8% year-over-year compared to $501 million reported a year ago. GAAP net income was $28 million or $0.06 per diluted share compared to $22 million or $0.05 per diluted share reported a year ago. Non-GAAP net income was $63 million or $0.13 per diluted share compared to $63 million or $0.13 per diluted share reported a year ago. Adjusted EBITDA was $123 million compared to $116 million reported a year ago. The company reported income from operations of $47.73 million and income before income tax provision of $27.00 million compared to income from operations of $41.71 million and income before income tax provision of $21.54 million reported a year ago.
