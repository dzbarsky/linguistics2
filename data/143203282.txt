Theta Microelectronics, Inc. announced that it has received $3.45 million in funding from Cadence Design Systems Inc., Emporiki Venture Capital, NBG Venture Capital SA, Nexit Ventures Oy, Telos Venture Partners..
"Theta Microelectronics Inc. announced that it will receive $3.45 million in its first round of financing in November 2001. Participants in the round were Telos Venture Partners, Cadence Design Systems Inc., NBG Venture Capital SA, Emporiki Venture Capital, and Nexit Ventures Ov. NBG Venture Capital SA invested through its fund NBG Technology LP. In April 2002, Theta Microelectronics Inc. closed the transaction."
