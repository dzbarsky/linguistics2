Principal Financial Group Inc. Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter Ended September 30, 2008 ; Declares Annual Dividend Payable on December 5, 2008.
"Principal Financial Group Inc. provided earnings guidance for the third quarter ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company expects operating earnings to be in the range of $0.92 to $0.97 per diluted share. Net income available to common stockholders to be in the range of $0.30 to $0.40 per diluted share. Net income available to common stockholders per diluted share reflects estimated net realized/unrealized capital losses of $140 to $170 million after tax, and a loss of approximately $5 million after tax related to discontinuing the commercial mortgage backed securitization operations. The company also announced that its Board of Directors has declared an annual dividend of $0.45 per share, payable on December 5, 2008, to shareholders of record as of November 14, 2008."
