Principal Financial Group Inc. Opens Boise Business Center.
Principal Financial Group Inc. has opened a Boise Business Center on the 11th floor of the Banner Bank Building, 950 W. Bannock St. The Principal will compete in a rapidly expanding market, and will be looking to hire quality professionals. An open house is scheduled from 4 to 6 p.m. on February 26, 2008. The Principal's products and services include investments, retirement plans, life and health insurance, and financial instruments that can be used for tax and estate planning. Key client groups for the Boise Business Center include owners, managers and employees of small and midsized businesses.
