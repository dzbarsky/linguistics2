Rohm and Haas and Tasnee Sahara Olefins Joint Venture in Saudi Arabia Names Fluor Corporation as Project Management Consultant.
Rohm and Haas Company announced that Fluor Corporation has been awarded a contract to act as the Project Management Consultant for its Saudi Acrylic Monomer Company joint venture with Tasnee and Sahara Olefins Company (TSOC). Fluor also has been awarded the Process Design Package for the plant to be built in Al Jubail, Saudi Arabia, and work is under way.
