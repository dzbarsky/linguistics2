The AES Corporation Reports Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2008 and 2009.
"The AES Corporation reported unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company posted net income of $145 million or $0.22 diluted per share on revenue of $4,345 million against net income of $104 million or $0.15 diluted per share on revenue of $3,484 million for the year ago period. Income from continuing operations was $145 million or $0.22 per diluted share against $92 million or $0.14 per diluted share for the year ago period. Consolidated revenues were up 25% to $4.3 billion, primarily due to higher prices across all regions and increased volume in Latin America, as well as favorable foreign currency translation and recovery of pass-through expenses. For the nine months, the company posted net income of $1,281 million or $1.87 diluted per share on revenue of $12,595 million against net loss of $103 million or $0.15 diluted per share on revenue of $9,915 million for the year ago period. Income from continuing operations was $1,282 mill"
