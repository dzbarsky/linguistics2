Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2009; Provides Revenue Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending June 2009.
Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company's revenue of $505.5 million was at the lower end of the range estimated in the release dated January 27, 2009. Revenue decreased 38.5% from $822.3 million in the same period last fiscal year, and 24.2% from the December 2008 quarter. Revenue continued to be impacted by the global slowdown, made worse by customer initiatives to reduce their inventory. Net loss was $58.6 million or $0.34 per diluted share. The March quarter compares with net income of $50.3 million or $0.28 per diluted share in the prior year quarter that included a pretax restructuring charge of $6.4 million or approximately $0.03 per share after-tax. Capital expenditures were $31.1 million compared with $61.0 million in the prior year March quarter and $51.3 million in the December 2008 quarter.
