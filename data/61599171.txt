Kirby Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2009.
"Kirby Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. The company announced net earnings for the second quarter ended June 30, 2009 of $33.7 million, or $0.63 per diluted share, compared with net earnings of $40.3 million, or $0.74 per diluted share, for the 2008 second quarter. Consolidated revenues for the 2009 second quarter were $272.7 million compared with $348.3 million reported for the 2008 second quarter. Operating income for the period was $57.7 million and income before income taxes was $55.0 million against operating income of $69.2 million and income before income taxes of $65.7 million for the same period a year ago. EBITDA for the period was $80.0 million against $91.3 million for the same period a year ago. Capital expenditure for the period was $51.8 million against $57.8 million for the same period a year ago.
