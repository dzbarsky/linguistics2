Fluor Corporation Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2008 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year 2008.
"Fluor Corporation announced earnings results for its first quarter ended March 31, 2008. Net earnings for the first quarter rose 63% to $138 million, from $85 million in the first quarter of 2007. Earnings per diluted share were $1.50, compared with $0.94 per diluted share for the same period last year. Revenues for the quarter increased 32% to $4.8 billion, up from $3.6 billion in the first quarter of 2007. The company increased its guidance for the year 2008. For the year, the Company expects earnings per share to a range of $6.25 to $6.55, up from the previous range of $5.10 to $5.50 per share. This guidance is not adjusted for the effect of the recently announced two-for-one stock split, which will be completed at a later date."
