Brocade Communications Systems Appoints Barbara Spicek as New Vice President of Worldwide Channels.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that it has appointed Barbara Spicek as its new Vice President of Worldwide Channels, signifying the company's commitment to bolster its global sales and high-touch customer engagements through a network of go-to-market partners. Spicek previously held the role of Senior Director, Partner Sales and Marketing, EMEA/LATAM, where she led a team of over 70 driving initiatives with Brocade's OEMs and channel partners. In her new role, Spicek will be responsible for developing channel strategies and working with Brocade's global sales network to define and accelerate channel recruitment and sales programs.
