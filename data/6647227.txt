KeyCorp's Board of Directors Approve Dividends for the Second Quarter, 2009.
KeyCorp announced that its Board of Directors approved a regular cash dividend of $0.01 per share of its common shares, or $0.04 per share on an annualized basis. The dividend is payable June 15, 2009 to shareholders of record on June 2, 2009. A cash dividend of $1,250.00 per share of its 5.0% Fixed Rate Perpetual Preferred Stock, Series B. The dividend is payable August 15, 2009 to shareholders of record on July 15, 2009, for the dividend period commencing on May 15, 2009.
