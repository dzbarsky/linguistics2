Illumina Signs Genotyping Services Agreement With Johns Hopkins Medical University.
Illumina Inc. announced that it has signed a commercial agreement with Johns Hopkins Medical University, Institute of Genetic Medicine to provide single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) genotyping services on a sample collection provided by the Institute. Under the terms of the agreement, Illumina will use its BeadArray technology to score, or determine the frequency of specified SNPs in the sample set. Illumina will also design functional assays for most of the SNP loci provided by the Institute.
