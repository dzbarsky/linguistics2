Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Financial Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended August 1, 2009.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported unaudited consolidated financial results for the third quarter and nine months ended August 1, 2009. For the quarter, the company has reported income from operations of $21,076,000, loss before income taxes of $1,012,000 and net loss of $21,033,000 or $0.05 per diluted share on total net revenues of $493,280,000 compared to income from operations of $44,424,000, income before income taxes of $52,157,000 and net income of $20,266,000 or $0.05 per diluted share on total net revenues of $365,696,000 for the same period last year. Net cash provided by operating activities were $16,631,000 against $71,651,000 reported last year. In the quarter, the company has incurred $44,826,000 on purchases of property and equipment against $94,218,000 reported last year. On Non-GAAP basis, the company has net income for the quarter of $55,376,000 or $0.12 per diluted share against $61,220,000 or $0.16 per diluted share reported last year.
