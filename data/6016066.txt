Fidelity National Financial Inc. Appoints Theodore L. Chandler to the Board of Directors.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. and LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. announced the signing of a definitive merger agreement under which FNF will acquire LFG. Theodore L. Chandler, Jr., LFG's Chairman and CEO, will join the FNF Board of Directors as Vice Chairman after the closing of the transaction.
