Wincanton plc Renews Transport and Warehousing Contract with HJ Heinz Co.
Wincanton plc has renewed its contract with HJ Heinz Co. The new contract will see a three-year extension to the transport contract and four years for both warehousing and co-packing. Wincanton distributes all the HJ Heinz brands including HP and Amoy. Based at the HJ Heinz national distribution center in Wigan, Wincanton will continue to manage the 350,000 sqft automated warehouse and provide HJ Heinz with a fleet supported by the wider Wincanton network.
