Starbucks Reports Revenues For Five Weeks and 13 Weeks Ended December 30, 2001.
Starbucks Corporation reported consolidated net revenues of $343 million for the five-week period ended December 30, 2001, an increase of 22 percent from consolidated net revenues of $281 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. For the 13 weeks ended December 30, 2001, consolidated net revenues were $805 million, an increase of 21 percent from consolidated net revenues of $667 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 2 percent for the 13 weeks ended December 30, 2001, as compared to the same 13-week period in fiscal 2001.
