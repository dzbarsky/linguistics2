Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 28, 2006.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended January 28, 2006. Net revenues for the quarter were a record $170.1 million. Revenues for the quarter increased 5% from $161.6 million reported in the first quarter of fiscal year 2005. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net income for the quarter was $12.6 million, or $0.05 per share basic and diluted. This compares to GAAP net income for first quarter of 2005 of $27.9 million, or $0.10 per share basic and diluted. Non-GAAP net income for the quarter was $25.9 million or $0.10 per share basic and diluted, as compared to non-GAAP net income for first quarter of 2005 of $28.3 million, or $0.11 per share basic and $0.10 per share diluted.
