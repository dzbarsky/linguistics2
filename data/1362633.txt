Illumina Reports Financial Results for Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended January 2, 2005; Provides Outlook for the First Quarter of 2005.
"Illumina Inc. announced financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended January 2, 2005. For the fourth quarter, Illumina reported revenue of $14.8 million, compared to revenue of $10.7 million in the fourth quarter of 2003. For the quarter ended January 2, 2005, the Company reported a net income of $3.2 million, or $0.08 per diluted share, compared to a net loss of $4.0 million, or $0.12 per share, in the fourth quarter of 2003. The Company's revenue for the first quarter of 2005 may be lower than revenue in the fourth quarter of 2004."
