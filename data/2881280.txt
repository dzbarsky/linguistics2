Hansen Natural Corp. and Anheuser-Busch Announce Distribution Deal.
Hansen Natural Corp. and Anheuser-Busch Inc. announced they have concluded agreements following which certain Anheuser-Busch wholesalers will become distributors of Hansen's Monster Energy(R) and Lost(R) Energy(TM) drinks, Rumba(TM) energy juice drinks as well as an additional Hansen energy brand to be designated. Monster, Lost and Rumba are currently distributed by Hansen in the United States through a network of beer and liquor distributors and soft drink bottlers. Hansen will transition distribution of Monster and Lost Energy drinks and Rumba energy juice as well as the additional energy brand to select Anheuser-Busch wholesalers in markets to be designated by Hansen. The transition process is expected to begin in the near future and will continue over the next year.
