Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Financial Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended Dec. 31, 2005.
"Atheros Communications Inc. reported financial results for the fourth quarter and year ended Dec. 31, 2005. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $53.1 million, up 27% from the $41.7 million reported in the fourth quarter of 2004. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the company recorded net income of $13.1 million or $0.24 per diluted share compared with a GAAP net income of $2.2 million or $0.04 per diluted share in the fourth quarter of 2004. Non-GAAP net income was $5.9 million or $0.11 per diluted share, compared with non-GAAP net income of $2.8 million or $0.05 per diluted share in the fourth quarter of 2004. For the year ended, the company reported revenue of $183.5 million, up 8% from the $169.6 million reported in 2004. Net income in accordance with GAAP for 2005 was $16.7 million or $0.31 per diluted share against GAAP net income of $10.8 million or earnings of $0.21 per diluted share.
