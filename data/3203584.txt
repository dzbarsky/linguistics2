Fallout from AK Steel Lockout Felt Throughout Southwest Ohio Town.
The eight-month long lockout at AK Steel Holding Corp.'s Middletown Works isn't just a battle between a company and its union workers. The effects of the lockout, which already has resulted in the loss of hundreds of jobs, are spreading across the community, home to steelmaking for more than a century. Small businesses are feeling the crunch, and the city government faces cutbacks in police and firefighters.
