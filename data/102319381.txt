Starbucks Corp. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended March 28, 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2010.
"Starbucks Corp. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended March 28, 2010. For the quarter, the company's total net revenues were $2,534.7 million against $2,333.3 million a year ago. The higher revenues were attributable to a 7% increase in comparable stores sales and the impact of foreign currency translation related to stronger Canadian and UK currencies, partially offset by a smaller store base. Operating income was $339.8 million against $40.9 million a year ago. Earnings before income taxes was $336.5 million against $35.0 million a year ago. Net earnings attributable to the company was $217.3 million or $0.28 per diluted share against $25.0 million or $0.03 per diluted share a year ago. Non-GAAP operating income was $347.7 million against $193.0 million a year ago.
