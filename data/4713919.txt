Luminant Renews Long-term Maintenance Agreement with Fluor.
Fluor Corporation announced that it was awarded a five-year renewal contract to continue supplying power-plant maintenance and capital project support services to multiple Luminant sites (TXU Corp.). The contract value was not disclosed and was booked during second quarter 2007. The services will be performed at 18 Luminant power generating stations in Texas with a capacity of approximately 16,000 megawatts, making this one of the largest maintenance contracts in the U.S. electric utility industry.
