Illumina Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 3, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2005 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2005.
"Illumina Inc. announced earnings results for the first quarter ended April 3, 2005. For the quarter, net loss was $1.2 million or $0.03 basic and diluted net loss per share against a net loss of $3.9 million or $0.12 basic and diluted net loss per share in the first quarter of 2004. Revenue was $15.1 million against $10.8 million in the first quarter of 2004. The company reaffirmed earnings guidance for the full year of 2005. For the full year, the company expects total revenue to range from $75 million to $82 million and loss per share to range from $0.14 to $0.01."
