Atheros Communications Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended Dec. 31, 2004.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year 2004, ended Dec. 31, 2004. Revenue in the fourth quarter was $41.7 million, against $38.3 million in the third quarter, an increase of 9%. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the company's fourth quarter net income was $2.2 million, or earnings of $0.04 per diluted share on 53.5 million average shares outstanding, against net income of $767,000, or $0.01 per diluted share on 52.4 million average shares outstanding in the third quarter of fiscal 2004. For the year ended Dec. 31, 2004, revenue was $169.6 million, up 94% from the $87.4 million in the year ended Dec. 31, 2003. Net income in accordance with GAAP for 2004 was $10.8 million or $0.21 per share. For the prior year, the GAAP net loss was $13.2 million or a loss of $1.07 per share."
