Hansen Natural Corp. Reports Financial Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2006.
Hansen Natural Corp. reported financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2006. For the quarter, the company's gross sales increased 88.9% to $137.8 million from $73.0 million a year earlier. Net sales increased 99.5% to $119.7 million from $60.0 million a year ago. Operating income more than doubled to $34.8 million from $14.7 million a year ago. Net income also more than doubled to $21.1 million, or $0.84 per diluted share, from $8.8 million or $0.37 per diluted share last year. The sales increase was partially offset primarily by decreased sales of Hansen's energy drinks, Joker Mad Energy drinks and Smoothies in cans.
