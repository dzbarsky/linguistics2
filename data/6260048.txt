Eaton Vance Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 31, 2009 ; Reports Impairment Losses on Investments.
"Eaton Vance Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended January 31, 2009. Net income in the first quarter of fiscal 2009 was $24.7 million compared to $57.9 million in the first quarter of fiscal 2008. Revenue in the first quarter of fiscal 2009 decreased $80.3 million, or 28% to $209.5 million from revenue of $289.8 million in the first quarter of fiscal 2008. The company reported earnings per diluted share of $0.21 in the first quarter of fiscal 2009 compared to earnings per diluted share of $0.46 in the first quarter of fiscal 2008. Net realized and unrealized investment losses, including impairment losses, reduced earnings per diluted share approximately $0.01 in the first quarter of fiscal 2009 and approximately $0.13 in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008. The company reported impairment losses on investments of $106,000 for the first quarter of fiscal 2009."
