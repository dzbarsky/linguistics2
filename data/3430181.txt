AK Steel Holding Corp. Appoints John S. Brinzo to its Board of Directors.
AK Steel Holding Corp. announced that John S. Brinzo has been elected to its board of directors, effective January 19, 2007. Mr. Brinzo is chairman of the board of directors of Cleveland-Cliffs Inc. Mr. Brinzo served as the chief executive officer of Cleveland-Cliffs from 1997 to 2006, and has served as its chairman since 2000.
