Principal Financial Group Inc.'s Equity Buyback in the amount of $500 mm has been suspended.
"The Board of Directors of Principal Financial Group Inc. (NYSE:PFG) authorized a share repurchase plan on November 27, 2007. Under the plan, the company intends to repurchase up to $500 million worth of the companys outstanding common stock. Repurchases shall be made in the open market or through privately negotiated transactions, from time to time, depending on market conditions. The stock repurchase program may be modified, extended or terminated at any time by the Board of Directors. On November 30, 2007, Principal Financial Group entered into an accelerated common stock repurchase agreement with an investment bank to repurchase up to $250 million worth of the companys own shares. The company received 2,917,380 shares as an initial delivery under the ASR. The ASR was completed in January 2008, with the company receiving 900,831 additional common shares under this agreement. Principal Financial Group Inc. suspended its share repurchase plan on October 13, 2008, in light of prudent capital management"
