Fluor Seeks Buys.
Fluor Corp. which reported earning $138 million during the first quarter, an increase of 63% over the same period last year, is looking at acquisitions. Alan Boeckmann, Chairman and Chief Executive officer of the company said that said Fluor is eyeing possible acquisitions in the areas of operations and maintenance, infrastructure and nuclear fuel cycle. The company reported having $1.9 billion in cash and marketable securities.
