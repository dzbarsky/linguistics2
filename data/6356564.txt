AK Steel Holding Corp. Announces Layoffs of Employees.
AK Steel Holding Corp. reported there were 150 employees on a layoff 75 from each plant. A total of 145 on a layoff at both plants 60 from Zanesville and 75 from Coshocton. In addition to the layoffs, 120 at the Zanesville plant and 30 at the Coshocton plant received a reduced schedule.
