Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. (NasdaqGS: BRCD) completed the acquisition of Foundry Networks, Inc. (NasdaqGS: FDRY)..
"Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. (NasdaqGS: BRCD) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Foundry Networks, Inc. (NasdaqGS: FDRY) for $2.5 billion in cash and stock on July 21, 2008. The consideration is payable by $18.5 in cash and by issuing 0.0907 shares of Brocade common stock in exchange for each share of Foundry common stock. Also, Foundry options shall be converted into options to purchase Brocade common stock. Brocade anticipates financing the acquisition through a combination of cash on hand of $1.4 billion, issuance of approximately 12 million shares of common stock and approximately $1.6 billion of debt financing. A termination fee of $85 million will be payable by Foundry Networks and $125 million will be paid by Brocade Communications to Foundry Networks upon the termination of the merger agreement under certain circumstances.
