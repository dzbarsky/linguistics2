Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 27, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2009.
Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 27, 2009. For the third quarter of 2009, Illumina reported revenue of $158.4 million, a 5% increase over the $150.3 million reported in the third quarter of 2008. The Company reported net income for the quarter of $17.1 million, or $0.12 per diluted share, compared to net loss of $10.1 million, or $0.08 per diluted share in the comparable period of 2008. The net loss in the prior year period was directly attributable to a $24.7 million in process research and development charge associated with the Company's acquisition of Avantome Inc.
