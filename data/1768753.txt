AK Steel Holding Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2005.
"AK Steel Holding Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2005. For the second quarter, the company reported operating profit of $74.2 million, net income of $9.0 million or $0.08 per diluted share on net sales of $1,454.6 million, against the operating profit of $56.4 million, net income of $92.7 million or $0.85 per diluted share on net sales of $1,311.8 million for the same period a year ago. For the six months period, the company reported operating profit of $187.8 million, net income of $68.2 million or $0.62 per diluted share on net sales of $2,877.1 million, against the operating profit of $57.9 million, net income of $258.1 million or $2.37 per diluted share on net sales of $2,446.2 million for the same period a year ago."
