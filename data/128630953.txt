Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Licenses Harbor Wholesale Ltd. to Produce and Market Casual and Dress Casual Footwear Under IZOD Brand.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. (PVH) has licensed Harbor Wholesale Ltd. to produce and market men's, women's and children's casual and dress casual footwear, leisure athletic shoes and non-beach sandals under the IZOD brand in the U.S., its possessions and territories and Canada. Harbor has been granted rights to PVH's IZOD trademark as well as the IZOD PerformX, IZOD Jeans, IZOD Classix, IZOD SPORT and IZOD Luxury Sport sub-brands. The initial term of the agreement is through 2015 with possible extensions through 2020 at PVH's option.
