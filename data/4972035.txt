Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended September 29, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2008.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended September 29, 2007. For the fourth quarter, the company reported total revenue of $129.0 million, compared to $102.1 million for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2006, an increase of 26%. Fourth quarter net income was $44.2 million, or $0.39 per diluted share, compared to $25.2 million, or $0.22 per diluted share, for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2006. The company expects fiscal 2008 revenue to be $560 million to $600 million, which includes approximately $20 million in anticipated licensing revenue from Coding Technologies.
