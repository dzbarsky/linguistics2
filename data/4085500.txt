Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2007.
Atheros Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported revenues of $95.5 million, up 56% from the $61.1 million reported in the first quarter of 2006. GAAP net income was $7.6 million or $0.13 per diluted share compared to net income in the first quarter of 2006 was $6.8 million or $0.13 per share. Non-GAAP net income was $13.6 million or $0.23 per diluted share, compared to $7.9 million or $0.14 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2006.
