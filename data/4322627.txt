Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended May 6, 2007.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended May 6, 2007. The company announced that its first quarter profit climbed 9%, helped by higher royalty revenues at Calvin Klein. For the quarter ended May 6, earnings grew to $53 million, or 92 cents per share, from $48.7 million, or 87 cents per share in the prior-year quarter. Revenue rose 17%, to $591.9 million from $506.4 million. The company said its revenue boost was also helped by a 43% jump in revenue at its Calvin Klein licensing business, mainly from fragrances.
