Principal Financial Group Inc. Declares Cash Dividend on Preferred Stock, Payable on March 30, 2009.
Principal Financial Group Inc. announced that its Board of Directors has declared a cash dividend of $1.39075 per share on its 5.563% Series A non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock and a cash dividend of $0.407375 per share on its 6.518% Series B non-cumulative perpetual preferred stock. The preferred dividends are payable on March 30, 2009, to preferred stockholders of record as of March 12, 2009.
