Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Citizens Communications, Gas and Electric Assets; Buyer(s): Unisource Energy Corp.; Seller(s): Citizens Communications Co.; Transaction Size: $230 mm.
Citizens Communications (NYSE:CZN) sold its Arizona gas and Arizona electric divisions to UniSource Energy Corporation (NYSE:UNS) for a consideration of $230 million in cash. Unisource Energy Corp. was advised by Credit Suisse First Boston, Inc.
