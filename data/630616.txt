Advanced AMR Introduces Energy Information & Control System.
Advanced AMR Technologies (AAT), a division of AES Corp. announced the release of their Energy Information and Control System (EICS). This innovative wireless energy management system provides cost effective energy management solutions for AMR and other energy monitoring services. The EICS is the heart of Advanced AMRs wireless Automated Meter Reading (AMR) and energy management system. The wireless-to-Internet energy management system collects real time energy information from wireless remote monitors attached to electric, water, and gas meters. The real time data transmitted to EICS is available for analysis allowing Utilities, ESCOS and Energy Co-operatives to implement energy management strategies for load response, demand management, outage restoration, tamper control, and other energy conservation programs.
