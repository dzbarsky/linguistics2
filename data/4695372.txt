Service Corp. International Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2007.
"Service Corp. International reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $15.1 million or $0.05 per diluted share on revenue of $565.5 million compared to net income of $25.5 million or $0.09 per diluted share on revenue of $431.4 million for the second quarter ended June 30, 2006. For the six months, the company reported net income of $52.8 million or $0.18 per diluted share on revenue of $1,173 million compared to net income of $52.4 million or $0.18 per diluted share on revenue of $873.4 million for the second quarter ended June 30, 2006."
