Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 28, 2008; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 28, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $329 million, compared to revenue of $391 million reported for the same period in 2007. On a GAAP basis, Cadence recognized net income of $5 million, or $0.02 per share on a diluted basis, in the second quarter of 2008, compared to net income of $60 million, or $0.20 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2007. Using this non-GAAP measure, net income in the second quarter of 2008 was $38 million, or $0.14 per share on a diluted basis, as compared to $91 million, or $0.30 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2007.
