Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Revised Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year of 2010.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. revised earnings guidance for the first quarter and full year of 2010. For the quarter, the company now estimates non-GAAP earnings per share will be $0.80 versus its prior guidance of $0.73 to $0.75. This compares to non-GAAP earnings per share in the prior year's first quarter of $0.53. GAAP earnings per share are estimated to be $0.11 in the first quarter of 2010, as compared to $0.48 in the prior year's first quarter. First quarter revenue is currently expected to be approximately $605 million to $610 million in 2010, an increase of 9% from the prior year's first quarter. The Company's updated guidance reflects stronger than expected performance across all of its operating divisions in the first two and a half months of 2010. The Company is currently projecting Calvin Klein royalty revenue to increase approximately 8% in the first quarter of 2010 as compared to the prior year.
