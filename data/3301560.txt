Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Group Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 30, 2006.
Atheros Communications Inc. reported consolidated earnings results for the nine months ended September 30, 2006. For the period, the company reported net income of $19,888,000 or $0.36 per diluted share on net revenue of $213,885,000 against net income of $3,605,000 or $0.07 per diluted share on net revenue of $130,407,000 for the same period of last year.
