Discovery Communications, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year Ending December 31, 2010.
Discovery Communications, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company's total revenues were $926 million against $837 million a year ago. Operating income was $312 million against $221 million a year ago. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $247 million against $155 million a year ago. Income from continuing operations, net of taxes was $164 million or $0.37 per diluted share against $103 million or $0.22 per diluted share a year ago. Net income available to the company's stockholders was $186 million or $0.43 per diluted share against $94 million or $0.22 per diluted share a year ago. The results reflect the strong operating performance and $43 million of lower expense in the current year from the unrealized change in the fair value of mark-to-market share-based compensation.
