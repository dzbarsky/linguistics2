Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN) completed the acquisition of Celestry Design Technologies, Inc..
"Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN) signed a letter of intent to acquire Celestry Design Technologies, Inc. for $63.3 million on December 20, 2002. The purchase consideration includes $54 million in cash and assumption of option valued at $9.3 million. Authosis Ventures and Harbinger Venture Management will sell their stakes in the transaction. Zhihong Liu, President and Chief Executive Officer of Celestry, has been named a Corporate Vice President and will report to Yaakov Milstain, Corporate Vice President and General Manager of the Cadence Custom IC group. Cadence will retain most of Celestry's employees and will continue to support all of Celestry's customers and products. The transaction has been approved by antitrust authorities. Cadence Design Systems, Inc. (NYSE: CDN) completed the acquisition"
