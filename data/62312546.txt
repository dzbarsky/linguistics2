Cadence Design Systems Inc. Provides Operating Cash Flow Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2009.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. provides operating cash flow guidance for the third quarter and full year of 2009. For the third quarter, the company expect operating cash flow is expected to be in a range of zero to $5 million, including severance payments related to the June restructuring of approximately $15 million. For the full year 2009, the company expects operating cash flow to be approximately break even for the full year 2009, including severance payments. They are also tightening the range on capital expenditures for 2009 to approximately $40 million to $45 million from previous estimate of $40 million to $50 million."
