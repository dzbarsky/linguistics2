Service Corp. International Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2008 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2008.
"Service Corp. International reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $31.4 million or $0.12 diluted earnings per share on revenues of $548.8 million compared to the net income of $15.2 million or $0.05 diluted earnings per share on revenues of $565.5 million for the same quarter year ago. Income from continuing operations was $31.7 million or $0.12 per diluted share against $12.9 million or $0.04 per diluted share last year. Revenues decreased $16.7 million, or 3.0%, as a result of significant divestiture activity throughout 2007 which included the sale of approximately 400 locations that generated more than $400 million of proceeds.
