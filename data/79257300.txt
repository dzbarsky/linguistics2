Fluor Corporation Reports CapEx for the Third Quarter Ended September 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2009; Approves Dividend Payable on January 5, 2010.
"Fluor Corporation reports earnings results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2009. The company reported that capital expenditures for the quarter were $54 million and return on equity remains very strong at 25%. The company also announced that its Board of Directors approved normal quarterly dividend of $0.125 payable on January 5, 2010."
