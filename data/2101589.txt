Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Service Corp. International, Five Funeral Homes and Nine Cemeteries; Buyer(s): Carriage Services Inc.; Seller(s): Service Corp. International; Transaction Size: $12.6 mm.
"Carriage Services, Inc. signed a definitive agreement to acquire funeral homes and cemeteries from Service Corporation International. The acquisition is subject to certain conditions. Five funeral homes and nine cemeteries of Service Corp. International  was acquired by Carriage Services Inc. on 03/30/1999 for $12.6 million in cash. The acquisition was accounted for under the purchase method of accounting."
