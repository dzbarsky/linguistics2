Dolby Laboratories Announces Implementation of New Solutions.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. has announced that its technologies will be implemented in new products from leading manufacturers. The Dolby Volume audio processing technology will be featured in two new audio/visual receivers from Harman Kardon, the AVR 755 and AVR 655 7.1 receivers, which utilise Dolby Volume in order to provide consistent volume levels across any content, channel programming or input sources. Dolby Volume will also be used by Toshiba in its new Regza ZH500 and ZV500 series LCD High Definition televisions, launched in Japan earlier in the year.
