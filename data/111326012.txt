"Illumina Inc. (NasdaqGS: ILMN) acquired remaining interest in Helixis, Inc. from Domain Associates, Advanced Technology Ventures, and Okapi Ventures LP of Okapi Venture Capital and others for approximately $110 million in cash on April 30, 2010. As reported under the terms of agreement, Illumina Inc. will pay $70 million in cash as up-front payment (net of $2.6 million of cash acquired) and contingent consideration payments that may range from $0 to $35 million based on the achievement of certain revenue-based milestones by December 31, 2011. Following the acquisition, Illumina will appoint Helixis Chief Executive Officer, Alex Dickinson, as Senior Vice President of PCR Solutions. Cara Stewart of Remarx Media Inc. acted as PR advisor to Okapi Venture Capital. Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati acted as legal advisor to Helixis, Inc. Illumina Inc.
