AK Steel Reports Second Quarter Results of 2003.
"AK Steel Holding Corporation reported a net loss of $78.2 million, or $0.72 per diluted share of common stock, for the second quarter of 2003. Net sales were $1,021.4 million on shipments of 1,399,000 tons. Net loss for the six months ended June 30, 2003 was ( $119.0) million, or ( $1.10) per share, compared to ( $9.4) million, or ( $0.09) per share for the same period of prior year."
