Illumina Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 3, 2010.
Illumina Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 3, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $237,309,000 compared to $158,360,000 for the period ended September 30, 2009. Income from operations was $57,335,000 compared to $29,299,000 for the period ended September 30, 2009. Income before income taxes was $54,710,000 compared to $27,463,000 for the period ended September 30, 2009. Net income was $35,447,000 or $0.24 diluted per share compared to $17,077,000 or $0.12 diluted per share for the period ended September 30, 2009. Net cash provided by operating activities was $54,828,000 compared to $19,997,000 for the period ended September 30, 2009. Purchases of property and equipment were $13,111,000 compared to $19,697,000 for the period ended September 30, 2009.
