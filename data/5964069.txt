Cephalon Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2008; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year 2008; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2009.
"Cephalon Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2008. For the third quarter, the company reported a profit of $112 million or $1.64 a share, reversing a loss of $306.7 million a year ago when the company suffered charges. On an adjusted, diluted basis, factoring out certain items, it earned $1.18 a share, compared with 92 cents a share a year prior. Revenue rose to $498.5 million from $438.4 million.
