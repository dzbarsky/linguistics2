Fluor Reports Earnings Results for Its Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2005 ; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2005.
"Fluor Corp. announced earnings results for its second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2005. For the period the company reported a net loss of $16.4 million, or 19 cents per share, compared with net earnings of $44.8 million, or 54 cents per share, in the second quarter of 2004. Revenues for the quarter increased 32% to $2.9 billion compared with $2.2 billion a year ago. The company reaffirms earnings guidance for the full year of 2005, adjusted for the impact of the 77 cents per share charge, otherwise remains essentially unchanged and translates to a revised range of $1.55 to $1.75 per share."
