Fluor Corporation to Build Procter & Gamble Facility Expansion.
Fluor Corporation announced that it has been selected by Procter & Gamble to expand its Charmin tissue and Bounty towel production facility in Cape Girardeau, Mo. Fluor will perform engineering, procurement and construction services at the facility. The contract value to Fluor, which was not disclosed, was booked in the first quarter of 2003.
