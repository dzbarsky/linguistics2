Cadence Reports Fourth Quarter and 2002 Results.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. announced total fourth-quarter revenue of $276 million compared to $378 million in the same period last year. Total revenue for 2002 was $1.29 billion compared to $1.43 billion in 2001, and pro forma diluted earnings per share were $0.63 compared to $0.84 in 2001. On a GAAP basis, the company reported net income of $72 million and diluted earnings per share of $0.27 for the year."
