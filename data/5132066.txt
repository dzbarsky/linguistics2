Gentex Corporation Announces Board Resignations.
Gentex Corporation announced (on March 26, 2007) that Enoch Jen planned to retire as Senior Vice President effective January 2, 2009. On January 22, 2008, Mr. Jen informed that he now plans to retire as Senior Vice President on January 2, 2010, and intends to work on a part-time consulting basis through March 31, 2011.
