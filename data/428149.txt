Fluor Corp. Reports Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2003 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for 2004.
"Fluor Corp. announced results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2003. Fluor's net earnings for the fourth quarter of 2003 were $51.5 million, or 63 cents per share. This compares with $48.2 million, or 61 cents per share a year ago. Revenues from continuing operations in the fourth quarter were $2.4 billion, compared with $2.5 billion last year. Fluor expects 2004 earnings ranging from $2.00 to $2.40 per share."
