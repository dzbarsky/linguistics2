Dyncorp International, KBR and Fluor Wins Contracts for Logistics Support in Iraq and Afghanistan.
DynCorp International Inc., KBR Inc., and Fluor Corp. were selected by the Pentagon to provide a range of logistics and support services for one year to U.S. and allied forces during combat and peacekeeping operations. Three defense companies were each awarded $5 billion contracts from the Army to provide food and shelter to U.S. troops in Iraq, Afghanistan and Kuwait. KBR has been the prime contractor on the deal since December 2001. The deal includes nine one-year options which could be worth up to $150 billion.
