Principal Financial Group Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 18, 2010.
"Principal Financial Group Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 18, 2010, at 09:00 Central Daylight. Location: 711 High Street, Des Moines, Iowa,United States. Agenda: To consider the election of four Class III Directors; to consider the approval of the Principal Financial Group Inc. 2010 Stock Incentive Plan; to consider ratification of the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as the company's independent auditors for the year ending December 31, 2010; and to consider such other matters as may properly come before the meeting ."
