Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2008.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the three-month and six-month period ended June 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $6.9 million or $0.03 per diluted share on total revenues of $1.18 billion against net earnings of $84.8 million or $0.38 per diluted share on total revenues of $1.50 billion for the same period a year ago. For the six months, the company reported net earnings of $34.2 million or $0.16 per diluted share on total revenues of $2.32 billion against net earnings of $168.2 million or $0.75 per diluted share on total revenues of $2.86 billion for the same period a year ago."
