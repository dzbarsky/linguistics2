Fluor Corporation Wins $1.3 Billion Contract.
Fluor Corporation received a $1.3 billion chunk of Repsol SA's (rep) $4.8 billion refinery expansion in Cartagena, Spain. The award includes project-management, design and engineering, and procurement and construction services. Fluor booked the deal into its backlog for the fourth quarter of 2007. The project is expected to break ground early this year and be commissioned in 2011.
