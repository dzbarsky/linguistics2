Hansen Natural Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009.
Hansen Natural Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $41,565,000 and operating income of $65,777,000 or $0.44 diluted earnings per share on net sales of $244,206,000 against net income of $28,811,000 and operating income of $42,828,000 or $0.29 diluted earnings per share on net sales of $212,178,000 for the same period a year ago. Income before provision for income taxes for the period was $63,254,000 compared to $46,454,000 for the same period a year ago.
