Frontier Communications Corp. closed an Equity Buyback in the amount of $200 mm.
"The Board of Directors of Citizens Communications Co. (NYSE: CZN) authorized a share repurchase program on February 26, 2008. Under the program, the company will repurchase up to $200 million of its common stock over the next 12 months. Frontier Communications Corp. (NYSE:FTR) completed its share repurchase plan on October 3, 2008. Under the plan, the company has repurchased 17,778,339 shares for a total cost of $200 million."
