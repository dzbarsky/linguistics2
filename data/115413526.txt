Fluor Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2010; Reaffirms Earnings Outlook for the Full Year of 2010; Provides Earnings Outlook for the Full Year of 2011.
"Fluor Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2010. Revenue rose 2% to $5.5 billion compared with $5.4 billion in the third quarter of 2009. The company reported a net loss attributable to company of $54 million, compared with net earnings attributable to company of $162 million a year ago. This resulted in a loss per diluted share of $0.30, which compares with earnings per diluted share of $0.89 for the same period last year. Third quarter results include previously announced charges of $163 million, or $0.90 per diluted share, for estimated cost increases on the Greater Gabbard Offshore Wind Farm project, and approximately $95 million, or $0.32 per diluted share, due to an adverse legal ruling relating to the collectability of potential claim awards and other amounts due the company on the completed SR-125 road project. Earnings before income taxes were $24.9 million against $265.9 million for the same period a year ago. Net ea"
