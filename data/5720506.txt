AK Steel Reportedly Holding Sale Talks.
Financial Times reported on dealreporter.com that AK Steel Holding Corp. is looking to sell itself and is holding discussions with many parties that include ThyssenKrupp AG, Evraz Group SA, Companhia Sider?rgica Nacional (CSN) and Joint Stock Company Severstal. The website reported citing unnamed sources that the company is seeking an all-cash deal. Reuters reported that ThyssenKrupp, Evraz and AK Steel could not be reached immediately for comment. Severstal declined to comment. A CSN spokeswoman said the company has a policy of not commenting on market rumors.
