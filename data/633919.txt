Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Announces Preliminary Results for the Third Quarter Ended July 31, 2004.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced preliminary results for the third quarter of fiscal 2004 (Q3 04) ended July 31, 2004. Brocade expects to report net revenue for third quarter 2004 in a range of $149.5 to 150.5 million and GAAP net income per share of $0.06 to $0.07. The GAAP earnings include a pre-tax gain of $3.5 million, equivalent to $0.01 per share, related to repurchases of $47.4 million of the company's convertible subordinated debt.
