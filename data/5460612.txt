AES Corp. (NYSE: AES) agreed to acquire Mountain View Power Partners LLC from Centennial Power Inc. on January 3, 2008. The financial terms of the transaction were not disclosed. FTC granted early clearance of antitrust concerns under the HSR Act. Carl Reisner and Dale Sarro of Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison LLP acted as the legal advisor for Centennial.
