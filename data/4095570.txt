Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended March 30, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2007.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended March 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $129.0 million, compared to $104.7 million, an increase of 23%. Net income was $39.1 million, or $0.34 per diluted share, compared to $28.0 million, or $0.25 per diluted share, for the second quarter of fiscal 2006. Net income reflected stock-based compensation expense of $4.8 million compared to $5.1 million in the second quarter of fiscal 2006. The company provides earnings guidance for the full year of fiscal 2007. The company expects revenue to be $435 million to $450 million. Net income is now expected to be $110 million to $115 million.
