Molex Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended September 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter Ending December 2010; Approves Quarterly Cash Dividend Payable on January 25, 2011.
Molex Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended September 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $897.7 million exceeded the high end of the guidance provided on August 3, 2010 and increased 6% from $847.3 million for the fourth quarter ended June 2010 and 33% from $674.0 million for the first quarter ended September 2009. Net income was $75.1 million or $0.43 per diluted share compared with net income of $39.8 million or $0.23 per diluted share for the June 2010 quarter and net loss of $15.1 million or $0.09 per diluted share for the September 2009 quarter. Net income for the current quarter was impacted by investigative and legal costs related to unauthorized activities in Japan of $5.5 million ($3.5 million after-tax or $0.02 per share). Income from operations was $112.48 million against loss from operations of $15.66 million for the same period a year ago.
