Cancelled Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: National Steel Corp., Substantially all assets; Buyer(s): AK Steel Holding Corp.; Seller(s): National Steel Corp.; Transaction Size: $1125.12 mm.
"National Steel Corporation (OTCBB:NSTL.Q) entered into the definitive agreement to sale substantially all of National Steel's principal steel making and finishing assets to AK Steel Corporation (NYSE: AKS) as on January 30, 2003. As reported under the terms of the agreement, AK Steel will pay approximately $925 million in cash and the assumption of certain liabilities approximating $200 million. The transaction is subject to the approval of the Bankruptcy Court and other conditions, including termination or expiration of the waiting period under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act and the execution and ratification of a new collective bargaining agreement by the United Steelworkers of America satisfactory to AK Steel for the National Steel employees who will become employees of AK Steel. Lazard & Co. LLC was the financial advisor for National Steel Corp. National Steel Corporation (OTCBB:NSTL.Q) cancelled the transaction to be acquired by AK Steel Corporation (NYSE: AKS) as on May 20, 2003."
