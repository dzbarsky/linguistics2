"Atheros Communications Inc. (NasdaqGS: ATHR) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Opulan Technologies Corp. from KLM Capital Group, Acorn Campus Ventures, AsiaVest Partners and Enspire Capital for $95 million in cash on July 19, 2010. The consideration is net of cash acquired of $1.1 million. Of the consideration payable to Opulan, $7.2 million of the closing consideration will be held in escrow for one year following the transaction. Under the terms of the agreement, Atheros may be required to pay up to $24.42 million to Opulan shareholders subject to the attainment of specified revenue and employee retention targets over the twelve month period from September 1, 2010 through August 31, 2011.
