Principal Financial Group Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2004.
"Principal Financial Group Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year 2004. Quarterly income grew to $213.5 million, or 70 cents per share, from $203.9 million, or 63 cents, in the year-ago period. Total revenue for the period was $2.24 billion, a gain of 3% from $2.17 billion a year earlier. For full year, earnings grew to $825.6 million, or $2.62 per share, from $746.3 million, or $2.28 per share, in 2003. On an operating basis, earnings were $764.8 million, or $2.43 per share. Total revenue gained 4% to reach $8.42 billion."
