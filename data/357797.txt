Health Net's Second Quarter Earnings For The Second Quarter Ended 2002 ; Announces Share Repurchase Program.
"Health Net Inc. announced that 2002's second quarter earnings per diluted share climbed to $.51, compared with a loss of $.12 in the comparable year earlier period. The 2002 second quarter results include $.05 per diluted share of earnings from the elimination of the amortization of goodwill. Revenues for the period are $2,469,818,000 and Net Loss for the period are $49,814,000 or $0.47 Diluted per share. On May 2, 2002, Health Net announced a $250 million share repurchase program, pursuant to which it may repurchase from time to time, through open market purchases or through privately negotiated transactions, up to $250 million of its Class A Common Stock. In the second quarter of 2002, Health Net repurchased one million shares at an average price of $28.37."
