Siemens Energy Automation GmbH & Co. KG Announces Agreement with Fluor Corporation to Expand their Existing Partnership.
Siemens Energy Automation GmbH & Co. KG announced an agreement with Fluor Corporation to expand their existing partnership and to make Siemens Fluor's preferred supplier. Any monetary value to the agreement was not revealed, but the agreement will be long term and the two companies will coordinate their work and planning.
