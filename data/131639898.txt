"Fluor Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2011. For the quarter, the companys net earnings attributable to the company were $140 million, or $0.78 per diluted share, compared with $137 million, or $0.76 per diluted share in the first quarter of 2010. The company reported revenue of $5,057.8 million and earnings before income taxes of $241.1 million compared to revenue of $4,918.9 million and earnings before income taxes of $233.1 million over the same period last year. Cash provided by operating activities was $370.5 million against cash used in operating activities $246 million a year ago. Capital expenditures were $55.6 million against $48 million a year ago.
