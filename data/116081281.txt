Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for Fourth Quarter and Year Ended October 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Outlook for the First Quarter of 2011; Provides Cash from Operating Activities Outlook for the Fiscal Year 2011.
Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for fourth quarter and year ended October 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $23.4 million or $0.05 per diluted share on total net revenues of $550.4 million against net income of $32.1 million or $0.07 per diluted share on total net revenues of $521.8 million for same period a year ago. Income from operations for the period was $55.8 million against $47.1 million for same period a year ago. Income before tax for the period was $29.4 million against $24.8 million for same period a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities for the period was $106.4 million against $155.3 million for same period a year ago. Purchases of property and equipment for the period were $45.7 million against $44.5 million for same period a year ago.
