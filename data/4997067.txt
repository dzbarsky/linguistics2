Principal Financial Group Inc. announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $500 mm.
The Board of Directors of Principal Financial Group Inc. (NYSE:PFG) authorized a share repurchase plan on November 27, 2007. Under the plan, the company intends to repurchase up to $500 million worth of the companys outstanding common stock. Repurchases shall be made in the open market or through privately negotiated transactions, from time to time, depending on market conditions. The stock repurchase program may be modified, extended or terminated at any time by the Board of Directors.
