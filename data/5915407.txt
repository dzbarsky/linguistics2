Starbucks Coffee Announces Earnings Results for the Half Year Ended September 8, 2008.
Starbucks Coffee announced earnings results for the half year ended September 8, 2008. For the period, the company had sales of $17.3 million, down from $17.4 million. Same store growth was 4.4%. Ebitda was $1.7 million, a 21% down on the same period last year.
