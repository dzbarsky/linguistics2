Service Corp. International Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2010.
Service Corp. International reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010. For the period, the company reported revenues of $555.3 million compared to $513.9 million a year ago. Operating income was $95.9 million compared to $69.1 million a year ago. Net income attributable to common stockholders $40.3 million or $0.16 diluted per share compared to $23.1 million or $0.09 diluted per share a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations excluding special items was $37.7 million or $0.15 diluted per share compared to $30.0 million or $0.12 diluted per share a year ago. The increase was primary attributable to higher revenues and gross profit, particularly in the funeral segment, reflecting the Keystone and Palm Mortuaries acquisitions. Net cash provided by operating activities was $77.1 million compared to $69.7 million a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities excluding special items was $78.6 million compared to $69.7 million a year ago.
