Starbucks Taps Grocery Executive to Lead North American Division.
Jim Donald, chairman and chief executive of the Pathmark Stores will join Starbucks Corporation as new president. He will replace Behar, who came out of retirement to run the division. Donald, who has been with Pathmark since 1996, has 32 years of experience in the retail and food industries Including executive positions at Safeway, Wal-Mart and Albertsons. He will oversee Starbucks' 4,500-plus stores in North America.
