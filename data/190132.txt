The AES Corporation Sued in Securities Fraud Class Action.
Notice is hereby given that a class action lawsuit was filed in the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia, on behalf of all purchasers of the securities of AES Corporation. The complaint charges that defendants violated Sections 10(b) and 20(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, and Rule 10b-5 promulgated thereunder, by issuing a series of material misrepresentations to the market between April 26, 2001 and February 14, 2002, thereby artificially inflating the price of AES securities.
