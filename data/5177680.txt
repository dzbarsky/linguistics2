Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended January 26, 2008.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended January 26, 2008. For the period, the company reported revenues of $347.8 million, up 55% from the same period a year ago. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net income was $19.8 million, $0.05 per share diluted. This represents a 38% decrease from GAAP net income of $32.0 million, or $0.08 per share diluted from the prior quarter, which included a one time gain of $11 million. Non-GAAP net income was $64.2 million or $0.16 per share diluted. This represents a 3% decrease from non-GAAP net income of $66.5 million, $0.16 per share diluted in fourth quarter of 2007, reflecting a sequential increase in the Company's effective non-GAAP tax rate and a 30% increase from non-GAAP net income of $49.4 million, $0.17 per share diluted in first quarter of 2007. The company also reported income from operations of $45,263,000 against income from operations of $39,413,000 for the same period a year ago.
