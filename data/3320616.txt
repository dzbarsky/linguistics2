Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Announces Earnings Results for the Full Year Ended Oct. 28, 2006.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced earnings results for the full year ended Oct. 28, 2006. For the year, the company reported a net income of $67,629,000 or $0.25 diluted per share on net revenues of $750,592,000 against net income of $43,121,000 or $0.16 diluted per share on net revenues of $574,120,000 for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net income of $122,757,000 or $0.45 per diluted share against $69,261,000 or $0.26 per diluted share a year ago.
