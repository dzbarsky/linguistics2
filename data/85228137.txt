Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. (NasdaqGS: BRCD) sold office building at 1600 Technology Drive, San Jose, California..
"Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. (NasdaqGS: BRCD) entered into an agreement to sell office building at 1600 Technology Drive, San Jose, California for $30.4 million on January 25, 2010. In addition, Brocade entered into a lease agreement pursuant to which the undisclosed purchaser leased the office building to Brocade, commencing as of the closing date of the sale. The deal is subject to consummation of due diligence. Jodi Fedor of SSL Law Firm LLP acted as the legal advisor for Brocade Communications. Anton N. Natsis of Allen Matkins Leck Gamble Mallory & Natsis, LLP acted as the legal advisor for the buyer in the transaction. Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. (NasdaqGS: BRCD) completed the sale of office building at 1600 Technology Drive, San Jose, California on January 28, 2010."
