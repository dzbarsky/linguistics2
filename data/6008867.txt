The AES Corporation announces an Equity Buyback in the amount of $400 mm.
The Board of Directors of The AES Corporation authorized a share repurchase plan on August 7, 2008. Pursuant to the plan, the company intends to repurchase up to $400 million worth of its outstanding common stock. Repurchase of shares may take place for a six month period. The stock repurchase program may be modified, extended or terminated by the Board of Directors at any time. As of September 30, 2008, the company has repurchased 10,691,267 own shares, at an average price of $13.38 per share, for an aggregate value of $143.05 million.
