Hansen Natural Corp. Reports Earnings Results for Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2005.
"Hansen Natural Corp. reported earnings results for fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2005. For the period, the company reported net income of $18,440,197 or $0.75 per diluted share on net sales of $98,010,085 compared to net income of $7,326,726 or $0.31 per diluted share on net sales of $50,337,332 for the same period a year ago. For full year period the company reported net income of $62,775,976 or $2.59 per diluted share on net sales of $348,886,366 compared to net income of $20,386,804 or $0.86 pe share on net sales of $180,341,135 for the same period a year ago. Record sales and profits for 2005 were primarily attributable to substantially increased sales volumes of the company's Monster Energy drinks and, to a lesser extent, of Lost energy drinks and Joker Mad Energy drinks."
