Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2006.
Atheros Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the third-quarter of 2006. For the third quarter, the company reported that net income rose to $6.32 million, or 11 cents a share, from $2.15 million, or 4 cents a share, a year ago. Excluding items such as stock-based compensation and a deferred tax asset valuation allowance, the company's earnings rose to 19 cents a share from 5 cents a year ago. Revenue rose to $79.6 million from $45.8 million a year earlier.
