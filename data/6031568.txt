Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended November 2, 2008 ; Revised Down its Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2008.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended November 2, 2008. The company reported GAAP net income of $53,699,000 or $1.03 per share on total revenue of $727,484,000. GAAP net sales for the quarter was $636,210,000. Non-GAAP net income for the quarter was $57,482,000 or $1.10 per share on total revenue of $698,887,000 compared to net income of $60,879,000 or $1.05 per share on total revenue of $696,370,000 for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP net sales for the quarter was $607,613,000 compared to net sales of $611,399,000 for the same period a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported GAAP net income of $129,706,000 or $2.48 per share on total revenue of $1,914,149,000. GAAP net sales for the quarter was $1,659,676,000. Non-GAAP net income for the quarter was $138,572,000 or $2.65 per share on total revenue of $1,835,765,000 compared to net income of $152,985,000 or $2.65 per share on total revenue of $1,840,652,000 for the same period a yea"
