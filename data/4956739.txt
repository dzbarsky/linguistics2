Cephalon Announces Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 30, 2007.
Cephalon Inc. reported earnings results for the nine months ended September 30, 2007. For the period the company reported net loss of $235,886 or $3.55 per diluted share on the revenue of $1,322,667 compared to the net income of $149,725 or $2.17 per diluted share for the same period a year ago.
