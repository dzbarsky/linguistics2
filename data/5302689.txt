Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. entered into an agreement to acquire Rhapsody Networks, Inc. for approximately $174.88 million..
"Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. entered into an agreement to acquire Rhapsody Networks, Inc. for approximately $174.88 million on November 5, 2002. Under the terms of the acquisition agreement, Brocade has acquired all outstanding shares of Rhapsody in exchange for 20.5 million shares of Brocade common stock. In addition, Brocade will issue an additional 2.9 million shares of common stock if the Rhapsody business successfully completes specified product development milestones by November 30, 2003. The transaction is subject to regulatory and antitrust approvals, approval by Rhapsody Networks shareholders, listing of new shares on the stock exchange, obtaining third party approvals and other customary closing conditions. The acquisition is expected to close in January 2003. Kenneth Guernsey and Eric Reischneider of Cooley Godwars acted as legal advisors while Goldman Sachs & Co. acted as financial advisor to Rhapsody Networks. Katharine Martin and Larry Sonsini of Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati acted"
