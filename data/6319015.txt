Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Announces Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended February 1, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2010 ; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. announced consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended February 1, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported loss before interest and taxes was $49.78 million and pre-tax loss was $56.85 million compared to earnings before interest and taxes was $52.72 million and pre-tax income was $48.23 million for the same period a year ago. The net loss was $37.94 million or $0.74 per diluted share on total revenue of $577.79 million compared to net income of $30.33 million or $0.55 per diluted share on total revenue of $584.52 million for the same period a year ago. The Non-GAAP net income was $15.68 million or $0.30 per diluted share on total Non-GAAP revenue of $561.29 million. For the full year, the company reported earnings before interest and taxes was $173.75 million and pre-tax earnings was $146.30 million compared to $311.83 million and $294.82 million respectively for the same period a year ago.
