Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended September 30, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Year 2007.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended September 30, 2006. For the fourth, quarter company reported profit of $25.2 million, or 22 cents per share, compared with a profit of $16.8 million, or 15 cents per share, a year ago. Revenue jumped 29% to $102.1 million from $78.9 million a year ago. The company provides earnings guidance for the year 2007. For the year 2007, company expects profit in between 83 cents and 92 cents per share on revenue between $420 million and $450 million."
