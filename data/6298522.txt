Fluor Corporation, Annual General Meeting, May 06, 2009.
Fluor Corporation, Annual General Meeting, May 06, 2009, at 09:00 Central Daylight. Location: Omni Mandalay Hotel at Las Colinas, 221 East Las Colinas Boulevard, Irving, TX 75039,United States. Agenda: To consider the election of four Class I directors to serve until the 2012 annual meeting of shareholders and until their respective successors are elected and qualified; to consider the ratification of the appointment by the Audit Committee of Ernst & Young LLP as independent registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2009; and to consider such other matters as may be properly presented at the meeting or any adjournment thereof.
