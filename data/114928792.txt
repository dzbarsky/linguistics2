Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2010.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2010. Revenue in the third quarter of 2010 was a record $247.1 million, up 58% compared to $156.6 million reported in the third quarter of 2009. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the company recorded net income in the third quarter of 2010 of $28.1 million or $0.39 per diluted share. Net income in the third quarter of 2009 was $38.6 million or $0.60 per diluted share. Income from operations was $29.5 million against $16.4 million reported last year. Non-GAAP net income in the third quarter of 2010 was $48.8 million or $0.67 per diluted share, compared to $29.3 million or $0.46 per diluted share in the third quarter of 2009. Non-GAAP income from operations was $54.5 million against $30.3 million reported last year. For the nine months, the company has reported income from operations of $80.9 million and net income of $77.6 million or $1."
