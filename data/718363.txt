Illumina Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 3, 2004 ; Company Also Announces Planned Resignation of Kish as CFO in the Second Quarter of 2005.
"Illumina Inc. announced its financial results for the third quarter and nine-month period ended October 3, 2004. For the quarter, the Company reported total revenue of $13.5 million compared to total revenue of $8.2 million in the third quarter of 2003. For the period net loss was $2.0 million or $0.05 per share compared to a net loss of $5.5 million or $0.17 per share in the third quarter of 2003. The Company also announced that Mr. Kish would resign in the second quarter of 2005 and return to the East Coast to be closer to family. He will continue in his role as CFO while the Company initiates a search for his successor."
