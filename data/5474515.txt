Fluor Corporation Announces Intention of Retirement of Lord Robin Renwick as Member of the Board of Directors and Chairman of the Governance Committee and Member of the Audit and Executive Committees Following August 1st Meeting ; Announces Amendments to Articles of Incorporation.
"Fluor Corporation announced that after twelve years of service, on May 6, 2008, Lord Robin Renwick, a current member of the Board of Directors of the company and also the Chairman of the Governance Committee and member of the Audit and Executive Committees, notified the board of his decision to retire from the board of directors immediately following its August 1st meeting. On May 7, 2008, the shareholders of Fluor approved an amendment to Article Fourth of the Amended and Restated Certificate of Incorporation of the company, effective as of May 7, 2008, to increase the authorized number of shares of common stock from 150,000,000 to 375,000,000."
