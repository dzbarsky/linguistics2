Network Appliance Partnerships Strengthen Full-line Enterprise Storage Solutions Strategy.
Network Appliance Inc. announced deepened and expanded partnerships with Brocade, Oracle Corp. and VERITAS Software Corp. that enhance the company's enterprise solutions portfolio. These partnerships allow Network Appliance to offer customers the broadest range of storage solutions designed to handle SAN and NAS storage environments. This provides customers maximum flexibility and firmly establishes Network Appliance as the leading full-line enterprise storage solutions supplier for data-intensive enterprise. These partnerships accelerate Network Appliance's move into different segments of the storage market.
