Health Net Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter of 2007.
Health Net Inc. reported $92 million, or 80 cents a share, in earnings in its second quarter 2007, up from $77 million, or 65 cents a share, in the year-ago period. Revenue was $3.5 billion in the quarter, up 6.1% from $3.3 billion in the year-ago period.
