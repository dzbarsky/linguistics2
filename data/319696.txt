Gentex Reports Record Third Quarter 2002 Results.
"Gentex Corp. reported record financial results for the quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2002. For the first nine months of 2002, the Company reported a 25 percent increase in revenues to a record $287.9 million, and a 28 percent increase in net income to a record $61.7 million, or 81 cents per diluted share. For the comparable 2001 period, Gentex reported revenues of $230.6 million and net income of $48.4 million, or 64 cents per diluted share."
