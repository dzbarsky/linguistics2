Fluor Corporation Announces Cancellation of Kuwaiti Refinery Contract.
Fluor Corporation announced that it has received notification from Kuwait National Petroleum Company to stop work on the utilities and offsites for the al-Zour refinery. Fluor has approximately 300 employees performing engineering work on the project. The remaining contract value of approximately $2.1 billion will be removed from the company's backlog in the first quarter.
