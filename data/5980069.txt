Update to Molex Inc.'s Equity Buyback on October 31, 2008.
On October 31, 2008, Molex Inc. purchased 2,000,000 shares of its common stock (MOLX) from Frederick A. Krehbiel, Molexs Co- Chairman of the Board, at an average price of $13.33 per share and an aggregate cost of $26.67 million, in cash. As of October 31, 2008, the company repurchased 732,000 shares of Class A Common Stock (MOLXA) and 2,900,000 shares of Common Stock (MOLX) at a total cost of $65.47 million.
