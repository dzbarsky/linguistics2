Atheros Communications Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2006.
"Atheros Communications Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $73.2 million, up 69% from $43.4 million in the second quarter of 2005. GAAP net income was $6.8 million or $0.12 per diluted share compared to net loss of $216,000 or $0.00 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2005. Non-GAAP net income was $9,508,000 or $0.17 per diluted share compared to net income of $401,000 or $0.01 per diluted share in the second quarter of 2005. The company announced that tremendous growth in the quarter was due to significant increases in revenue, operating income and earnings. For the six months ended June 30, 2006, the company reported revenue of $134,277,000 compared to $84,607,000 for the same period last year. GAAP net income was $13,567,000 or $0.25 per diluted share compared to net income of $1,453,000 or $0.03 per diluted share for the same period last year.
