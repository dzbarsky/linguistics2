Brocade first quarter revenue beats estimate for the fiscal year 2002.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported its first-quarter financial results in line with analysts' forecasts and noted an increase in deferred revenue exiting the quarter. The company (BRCD) posted earnings of $11.7 million, or 5 cents a share, compared with the year-ago profit of $32.5 million, or 13 cents a share. Revenue for the quarter totaled $123.1 million compared with the year-ago total of $165 million. On average, analysts expected revenue of about $118.8 million for the latest quarter.
