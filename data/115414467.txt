Fluor Corporation Announces Executive Changes.
Fluor Corporation announced that its chief operating officer (COO) David T. Seaton will succeed Alan L. Boeckmann as chief executive officer (CEO) and also become a member of the company's board of directors. The company also said that Boeckmann will retire from the company but serve on its board as non-executive chairman. Both moves will occur at the company's board of directors' meeting on February 3, 2011. Seaton is a board member of The Mosaic Company, Fluor Arabia Ltd. and the Fluor Foundation. He is also a board member of the American Petroleum Institute and the U.S.-Saudi Arabian Business Council.
