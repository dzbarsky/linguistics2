Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: American Memorial Life Insurance Company; Buyer(s): Service Corp. International; Seller(s): Great American Financial Resources Inc.; Transaction Size: $164 mm.
"Service Corporation International (NYSE: SRV) announced that it will acquire the pre-need funeral services division, American Memorial Life Insurance Company of American Annuity Group, Inc. (NYSE: AAG). The deal was valued for $164 million in cash. The acquisition is subject to approval under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Antitrust Improvements Act and state insurance regulations in South Dakota, Montana and Arkansas. The transaction is expected to close in the fourth quarter of 1998. Keating, Muething & Klekamp advised American Memorial Life while Paul Bird of Debevoise & Plimpton and Mark Watts of Locke, Lidell & Sapt and J.P. Morgan & Co., Inc. advised  Service Corp. International on the deal. Kissel Blake Inc., was the proxy solicitor to Service Corp. International. Service Corporation International acquired American Memorial Life Insurance Company from American Annuity Group, Inc. on 07/17/2005."
