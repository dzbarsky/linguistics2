Brocade Announces Preliminary Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ending July 30, 2005.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced preliminary results for the third quarter of fiscal 2005 ending July 30, 2005. Brocade expects to report net revenue in a range of $121 to $122 million and diluted GAAP net loss per share of $0.03 to $0.04. These results are below previous guidance for revenue in a range of $140 to $145 million, and diluted non-GAAP net income per share in a range of $0.05 to $0.06.
