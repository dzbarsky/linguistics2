Fidelity National Information Services Inc. Reports Consolidated Un-Audited Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Year of 2009.
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. reported consolidated un-audited earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2008. For the quarter, the company's consolidated revenue increased 0.8% to $862.0 million in U.S. dollars and increased 4.3% on a constant currency basis compared to $855.3 million in 2007. GAAP net earnings from continuing operations totaled $49.9 million, or $0.26 per diluted share compared to $47.6 million or $0.24 per diluted share in the 2007 quarter. GAAP net earnings was $28.8 million or $0.15 per diluted share compared to GAAP net earnings was $108.4 million or $0.55 per diluted share for the same period last year. Non-GAAP adjusted net earnings from continuing operations increased 41.2% to $0.48 per share, compared to $0.34 in the prior year, and increased 44.1% to $0.49 per share in constant currency.
