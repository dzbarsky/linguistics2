Capitol Federal Financial Announces Quarterly 2003 Dividend.
Capitol Federal Financial. announced that its Board of Directors declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.50 per share on outstanding CFFN common stock. The dividend is payable on November 21, 2003 to stockholders of record as of the close of business on November 7, 2003. The Board also reconfirms its intent to pay a calendar year-end dividend in 2003.
