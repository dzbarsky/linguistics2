Illumina Inc. Announces Consolidated Unaudited Financial Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 29, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2008; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2008; Proposes Amendment to Certificate of Incorporation.
"Illumina Inc. announced consolidated unaudited financial results for the second quarter and six months ended June 29, 2008. For the second quarter, the company reported GAAP net income of $15.4 million or $0.23 per diluted share on revenue of $140.2 million compared to net income of $9.3 million or $0.16 per diluted share on revenue of $84.5 million in the same period of 2007. The company's net income on a non-GAAP basis was $27.5 million or $0.44 per diluted share compared to $16.8 million or $0.29 per diluted share for the second quarter of 2007. The company provided earnings guidance for the third quarter and revised earnings guidance for the full year of 2008.
