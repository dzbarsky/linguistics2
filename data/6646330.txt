Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Capital Expenditure Results for the First Quarter Ended April 4, 2009; Provides Financial Guidance for the Second Quarter and Full Year of 2009.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported capital expenditure results for the first quarter ended April 4, 2009. For the period, the company's capital expenditures were $15 million. This included $5 million of remaining payments for new engineering building in San Jose which is now complete. For the full year, the company's capital expenditures are expected to be in the range of $40 million to $50 million, a decrease of approximately $30 million from historical spending levels."
