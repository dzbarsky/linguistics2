Cephalon Inc. Reports Consolidated Unaudited Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2010 and Updates Earnings Guidance for Full Year of 2010.
"Cephalon Inc. reported consolidated unaudited earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported sales of $712.4 million, a 32% increase compared to sales of $539.0 million for the second quarter 2009. Adjusted net income for the second quarter of 2010 was $165.3 million or $2.05 diluted per share as compared to adjusted net income for the second quarter of 2010 was $110,708,000 or $1.44 diluted per share for the same period last year. Income from operation was $191,861,000, income before income taxes was $155,647,000, net income attributable to the company was $89,064,000 or $1.11 diluted per share as compared to income from operation was $106,052,000, income before income taxes was $118,972,000, net income attributable to the company was $84,764,000 or $1.11 diluted per share for the same period last year.
