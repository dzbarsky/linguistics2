Avery Dennison Corporation Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended January 1, 2011; Declares Quarterly Dividend, Payable on March 16, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year of 2011.
"Avery Dennison Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended January 1, 2011. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $114.2 million or $1.06 per diluted share on net sales of $1,637.1 million compared to net income of $49.9 million or $0.47 per diluted share on net sales of $1,521.8 million for the same period of last year. Income from operations before taxes was $71.6 million compared to $26.5 million for the same period of last year. Adjusted non-GAAP operating income before taxes and interest expense was $96.8 million compared to $73.7 million for the same period of last year. Adjusted non-GAAP net income was $105.3 million or $0.98 per diluted share compared to $46.8 million or $0.44 per diluted share for the same period of last year. For the year, the company reported net income of $316.9 million or $2.97 per diluted share on net sales of $6,512.7 million compared to net loss of $746.7 million or $7.21 per diluted share on net sales"
