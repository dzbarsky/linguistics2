Service Corp. International Announces Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009.
Service Corp. International announced earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $34.38 million and net income attributable to common stockholders was $34.53 million or $0.14 per diluted share on revenue of $510.6 million compared to net income and net income attributable to common stockholders was $41.52 million or $0.16 per diluted share on revenue of $573.5 million for the same period a year ago. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $54.66 million compared to $66.47 million for the same period a year ago. Income from continuing operations was $34.38 million or $0.14 per diluted share compared to $41.50 million or $0.16 per diluted share for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $85.6 million compared to $99.4 million for the same period a year ago.
