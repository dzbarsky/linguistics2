KeyCorp Declares Regular Dividends on Common Shares and Preferred Stock, Series A Payable on December 15, 2008.
KeyCorp announced that its Board of Directors approved a regular cash dividend of $0.0625 per share of its common shares for the fourth quarter, 2008, a reduction from the third quarter level of $0.1875 per share. Figured on an annual basis, the new dividend level would be $0.25 per share. The dividend is payable December 15, 2008 to shareholders of record on December 2, 2008. The company also declared a cash dividend of $1.9375 per share of its Non-Cumulative Perpetual Convertible Preferred Stock, Series A was declared for fourth quarter, 2008. The dividend is payable December 15, 2008 to shareholders of record on November 28, 2008.
