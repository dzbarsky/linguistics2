Illumina, Inc. (NASDAQ:ILMN) signed a definitive agreement to acquire CyVera Corporation for $20 million..
Illumina, Inc. (NASDAQ:ILMN) signed a definitive agreement to acquire CyVera Corporation for $20 million on February 22, 2005. The purchase consideration includes $ 14.96 million in common stock of Illumina, Inc. and $2.5 million in assumed liabilities. The purchase consideration also includes an escrow payment of $2.54 million in common stock of Illumina, Inc. The agreement also provides for a termination fee of $0.5 million to be paid to Illumina by CyVera. The transaction is subject to approval by shareholders of CyVera Corporation, third party approval and resignation of Directors of CyVera Corporation. The transaction has been approved by Board of Directors of CyVera Corporation and Illumina, Inc. The transaction is expected to close by end of March 2005. Frank Marco of Wiggin & Dana LLP acted as legal advisor to CyVera Corporation. Mark Weeks of Heller Ehrman White & McAuliffe LLP acted as legal advisor to Illumina, Inc. Morris, Nichols, Arsht & Tunnell acted as a legal advisor to CyVera Corporation.
