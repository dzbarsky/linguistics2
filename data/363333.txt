Starbucks Reports Strong Second Quarter 2002 Results.
"Starbucks Corp. announced revenues and earnings for the second quarter of fiscal 2002. For the second quarter ended March 31, 2002, consolidated net revenues increased 24 percent to $783 million from $629 million for the second quarter of fiscal 2001. Net earnings of $32.1 million, or $0.08 per share, for the 13-week period ended March 31, 2002, included a one-time charge of $18 million for litigation settlement announced on April 19, 2002. For the 26 weeks ended March 31, 2002, consolidated net revenues increased 23 percent to $1.6 billion from $1.3 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001. Retail revenues increased 24 percent to $1.3 billion, and specialty revenues increased 15 percent to $242 million. Comparable store sales for the 26-week period ended March 31, 2002 increased 4 percent as compared to the same 26-week period in fiscal 2001.
