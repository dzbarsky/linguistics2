HJ Heinz Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of Fiscal 2009.
HJ Heinz Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter of fiscal 2009. For the quarter, the company reported profit totaled $277 million, or 87 cents a share, from $227 million, or 71 cents a share, in the same quarter a year before. Revenue for the quarter was $2.61 billion compared to $2.52 billion in the year-ago period.
