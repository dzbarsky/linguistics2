KeyCorp Cuts 120 Technology Jobs.
KeyCorp announced that it has cut 120 technology jobs, 100 of them in Greater Cleveland. Employees were notified on March 18, 2009, and the cuts will take effect during the next few months. The cuts come from Key's 2,150 technology jobs locally and 3,300 nationwide. The decision followed an assessment of Key's technology and operations department, its existing projects and the bank's goals. The Cleveland bank is 'realigning and prioritizing projects. Affected employees will be eligible for other positions at Key. Otherwise, they'll get severance pay and outplacement help.
