Starbucks Reports July Revenues for 2002.
Starbucks Corp. announced consolidated net revenues of $261 million for the four-week period ended July 28, 2002, an increase of 26 percent from consolidated net revenues of $207 million for the same period in fiscal 2001. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 7 percent for the four weeks ended July 28, 2002 as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2001. For the 43 weeks ended July 28, 2002, consolidated net revenues were $2.7 billion, an increase of 24 percent from consolidated net revenues of $2.2 billion for the same period in fiscal 2001. Comparable store sales increased 6 percent for the 43 weeks ended July 28, 2002, as compared to the same 43-week period in fiscal 2001.
