Big Lots Inc. Appoints Christopher T. Chapin as Senior Vice President of Store Operations.
Big Lots Inc. announced the appointment of Christopher T. Chapin as Senior Vice President of Store Operations, effective immediately. Mr. Chapin will report to CEO Steve Fishman. Chapin will oversee store standards, field operations, customer service, process development, and program implementation for the company's 1,300+ closeout retail stores in 47 states. He most recently served as president of FacilitySource.
