Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Year to Date Ended June 25, 2010; Announces Non-Cash Impairment Charge; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2010.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and year to date ended June 25, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to the company was $63,452,000 or $0.56 per basic share and $0.55 per diluted share on total revenue of $230,269,000 against net income attributable to the company was $51,146,000 or $0.45 per basic share and $0.44 per diluted share on total revenue of $171,244,000 for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $95,940,000 compared to $78,419,000 a year ago. Income before provision for income taxes was $98,082,000 compared to $79,019,000 a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $74,969,000 compared to $65,197,000 a year ago. Purchases of property, plant, and equipment were $6,989,000 compared to $5,684,000 a year ago.
