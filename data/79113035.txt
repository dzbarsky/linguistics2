Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Fiscal Year Ended September 25, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal 2010.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended September 25, 2009. For the fourth quarter, Dolby reported total revenue of $163.9 million, compared to $163.1 million for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008. Fourth quarter net income was $44.3 million, or $0.38 per diluted share, compared to $48.6 million, or $0.42 per diluted share, for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008. Operating income was $65.26 million compared to $66.68 million in the previous year period. Income before provision for income taxes and controlling interest was $67.22 million agianst $71.43 million reported a year ago. Net cash provided operating activities was $102.46 million compared to $77.96 million in the previous year period. Cash invested in purchase of property and equipment was $4.76 million compared to $5.94 million in the previous year period. For fiscal year 2009, Dolby reported total revenue of $719.5 million, compared to $640.2 million for fiscal year 2008, an increase of 12%."
