Starbucks Reports results for Four Weeks ended on 27th October, 2002.
Starbucks Corp. reported consolidated net revenues of $279 million for the four-week period ended October 27, 2002, an increase of 24 percent from consolidated net revenues of $225 million for the same period in fiscal 2002. On a comparable store sales basis (stores open for at least 13 months), sales at Company-operated stores increased 10 percent for the four weeks ended October 27, 2002, as compared to the same four-week period in fiscal 2002.
