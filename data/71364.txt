Gentex Authorizes Share Repurchase Plan.
Gentex Corporation announced that the Company's Executive Committee of the Board of Directors has authorized a share repurchase plan. Under the share repurchase plan, the Company may, from time to time, purchase up to four million shares of its common stock based on a number of factors Including market conditions, the market price of the Company's common stock, anti-dilutive effect on earnings, available cash and other factors as the Company deems appropriate.
