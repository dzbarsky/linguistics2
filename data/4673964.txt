Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended June 30, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2007.
"Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended June 30, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $119.6 million compared to a total revenues of $93.7 million, an increase of 28%. Net income was $29.7 million or $0.26 per diluted share compared to net income of $19.1 million or $0.17 per diluted share for the third quarter of fiscal 2006. The company provided earnings guidance for the full year of fiscal 2007. For the period, the company expects revenue to be approximately $465 million to $475 million.
