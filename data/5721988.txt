ThyssenKrupp Denies Interest In AK Steel.
ThyssenKrupp AG said that it is not interested in acquiring AK Steel Holding Corp. and has not held any talks with AK. Deal reporter had posted a report on the Financial Times website on July 31, 2008, stating that AK Steel held sale talks with several companies. The report added that ThyssenKrupp is closely looking at AK Steel.
