Capitol Federal Financial Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended June 30, 2007 ; Declares Dividend Payable on August 17, 2007.
"Capitol Federal Financial Reported Earnings Results for the third quarter and nine months ended June 30, 2007. Net income for the quarter ending June 30, 2007 was $7.3 million or $0.10 diluted earnings per share compared to $11.3 million or $0.15 diluted earnings per share for the same period in the prior fiscal year. The $4.0 million decrease in net income was primarily a result of an increase in interest expense on deposits. Total interest and dividend income for the quarter was $101.5 million compared to $102.4 million for the prior year quarter. The $900,000 decrease was primarily a result of a decrease in interest income on loans receivable of $2.9 million, a decrease of $2.0 million in interest income on mortgage-related securities, and a decrease of $265,000 in interest income on cash and cash equivalents, partially offset by an increase in interest income on investment securities of $4.3 million.
