Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. to Reaffirm Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter, Fourth Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2008.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. announced that it will reaffirm earnings guidance for the third quarter, fourth quarter and full year of fiscal 2008. For the third quarter, previously the company said it expects earnings per share between $1.02 and $1.03 per share, with revenue of $705 million. For the full year of fiscal 2008, previously, the company said it expects earnings between $3.15 and $3.17 per share."
