News Corp Reportedly Ready To Sell Sky News To Avoid Lengthy Review Of Bskyb Bid.
News Corp is reported to have told the British Department for Culture, Media and Sport that it is ready to divest Sky News or other assets in order to avoid a lengthy review of its bid to acquire BSkyB. FT.com reported citing three people familiar with talks between News Corp and the Department for Culture, Media and Sport that one of the options proposed would be to separate Sky News from BSkyB. The report added that British broadcasting regulator Ofcom has recommended the government to refer News Corps bid to the Competition Authority, on the basis that it could reduce the diversity of news provision in the UK.
