AES Reports Results for the First-Quarter ended March 31, 2004; Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for 2004.
"The AES Corp. reported results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2004. The company reported net income of $48 million or $0.08 per diluted share on sales of $2.3 billion compared to net income of $93 million or $0.17 per diluted share on sales of $1.9 billion reported for the same period a year ago. AES reaffirmed its 2004 diluted earnings per share from continuing operations guidance of $0.62, excluding the $0.03 per share cost associated with the previously disclosed Gener debt transaction costs."
