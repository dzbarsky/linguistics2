U.S. Steel in talks to buy AK Steel.
U.S. Steel Corp. is in talks to acquire AK Steel Corp. in a stock transaction that would potentially create the world's sixth-largest steel producer, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette reported on Friday March 03. Citing documents obtained by the paper, the Post-Gazette said U.S. Steel directors were told on Tuesday Feb 28 that AK Steel had been presented a preliminary price based on a stock transaction. However, the paper said U.S. Steel has also indicated it would be flexible about using a mix of stock and cash.
