Brocade Announces Appointment of Robert R. Walker to Board of Directors.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that Robert R. Walker has joined its Board of Directors. Walker's career as a finance and operations executive spans nearly three decades. Before his retirement in 2001, he served as Chief Financial Officer of Agilent Technologies from its inception in 1999 to 2001. Walker currently serves as a director of Liberate Technologies and Electro Scientific Industries, and he is Vice Chairman of Financial Executives International.
