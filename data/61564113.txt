Fidelity National Information Services Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009 ; Revises Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2009.
Fidelity National Information Services Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported processing and services revenues of $834.8 million, operating income of $117.4 million, earnings from continuing operations before income taxes of $91.6 million, net earnings from continuing operations of $60.0 million or $0.31 diluted per share, net earnings of $59.6 million and net earnings attributable to common stockholders of $59.2 million or $0.31 diluted per share against processing and services revenues of $869.7 million, operating income of $57.9 million, earnings from continuing operations before income taxes of $17.1 million, net earnings from continuing operations of $13.6 million or $0.07 diluted per share, net earnings of $72.8 million and net earnings attributable to common stockholders of $71.9 million or $0.37 diluted per share for the same period a year ago.
