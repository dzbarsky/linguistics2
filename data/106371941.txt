Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Bests Cisco to Win City of Casselberry Municipal Network.
Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. announced that the City of Casselberry, Fla., recently completed a comprehensive network infrastructure upgrade, replacing its legacy Cisco network with Brocade. The City of Casselberry selected Brocade because of Brocade solutions' proven reliability and lower total cost of ownership, when compared to others. The new Brocade infrastructure enables the city to reliably develop, deploy and maintain a variety of advance services, including IP-based emergency response services and public works communications. It also provides the future-readiness to protect the City's investments while supporting anticipated capacity and user number growth as well as emerging technologies.
