Massey Energy Co. agreed to Spin-Off Fluor Corporation..
Massey Energy Company announced the spin-off of Fluor Corporation on June 7, 2000. Under the agreement, Each Massey Energy shareholder will receive one share of Fluor common stock for every share of Massey Energy common stock. The Board of Directors of Fluor Corporation unanimously approved the transaction. The transaction is subject to shareholder approval, establishment of new capital structures, and a favorable ruling by the Internal Revenue Service. The deal is expected to be completed by the end of the calendar year.
