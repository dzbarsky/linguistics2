Dolby Labs Announces Financial Results for the First Quarter of 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for Year of 2011.
"Dolby Labs announced financial results for the first quarter of 2011. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $242.7 million. EPS was $0.75. The company provided earnings guidance for year of 2011. For the year, the company expects revenue of $930 to $970 million and EPS of $2.82 to $2.98 per share."
