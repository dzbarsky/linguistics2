Gentex Begins Constructin of 300,000 Square Foot Building.
Gentex Corp. announced that it has begun construction on a 300,000-square-foot building to house technology and manufacturing operations. The building, expected to open in early 2006, will include a manufacturing plant that will produce interior auto-dimming mirrors and electronic components related to mirrors. The plant will be able to produce as many as 7 million mirrors annually.
