Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended January 1, 2011; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter Ending April 2, 2011 and Full Year Ending December 31, 2011.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended January 1, 2011. For the quarter, the company's total revenues were $249,018,000 against $220,279,000 a year ago. Loss from operations was $9,982,000 against $11,256,000 a year ago. Loss before provision (benefit) for income taxes was $17,872,000 against $13,813,000 a year ago. Net loss was $21,237,000 or $0.08 per basic and diluted share against net income of $1,790,000 or $0.01 per basic and diluted share a year ago. Diluted net income per share on a non-GAAP basis was $0.07 against $0.06 a year ago. Net income on a non-GAAP basis was $17,564,000 against $15,062,000 a year ago. Operating cash flow was $57 million. For the year, the company's total revenues were $935,954,000 against $852,632,000 a year ago. Loss from operations was $13,166,000 against $123,591,000 a year ago. Loss before provision (benefit) for income taxes was $46,968,000 against $153,505,000 a year ago. Net income was $1"
