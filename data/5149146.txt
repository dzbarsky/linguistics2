Starbucks Corp., Annual General Meeting, Feb 26, 2002.
Starbucks Corp., Annual General Meeting, Feb 26, 2002, at 10:00 US Pacific Time. Location: Benaroya Hall, 200 University Street, Seattle, Washington, United States. Agenda: To elect three Class 3 directors to serve until the Annual Meeting of Shareholders to be held in early 2005; to ratify the selection of Deloitte & Touche LLP as the Company's independent auditors for the fiscal year ending September 29, 2002; to approve the material terms of the Company's Executive Management Bonus Plan; to act on a shareholder proposal to declassify the Company's Board of Directors; and to act on a shareholder proposal to identify and label all food products manufactured or sold by the Company that may contain genetically modified ingredients.
