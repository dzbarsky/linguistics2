The AES Corp. Reports Financials Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2001; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Year 2002.
"The AES Corp. announced that net income for 2001 was $727 million, before deducting the non-cash foreign currency transaction losses at Brazilian affiliates, the mark-to-market losses from FAS 133, the nonrecurring severance and transaction costs recorded in conjunction with the IPALCO pooling-of-interests transaction, and discontinued operations. Diluted earnings per share, before such charges, were $1.35 for the year. Net income and diluted earnings per share for the year, after all charges, were $273 million and $0.51, respectively. For the year, revenues increased 24% to $9.3 billion. AES expects its earnings per share for 2002 to be in the range of$1.50-$1.65.
