Brocade Communications Systems Inc. Cuts 9% of its Staff; Announces Settlement of a Lawsuit with Rhapsody Shareholders.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. cut 9% of its staff and announced the job cuts of 110 workers to further optimize its business model and align its organization to accelerate profitability and improve revenue growth, according to a statement. Brocade Communications Systems settled a lawsuit with Rhapsody shareholders for $6.9 million. Brocade took a $6.9 million charge to account for the settlement of claims by former Rhapsody shareholders regarding an earn-out payment associated with the Brocade's takeover of Rhapsody Networks in 2002."
