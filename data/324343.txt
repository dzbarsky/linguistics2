Service Corporation International Announces The Resignation Of Jerald L. Pullins, President And Chief Operating Officer.
Service Corp. International announced that Jerald L. Pullins, President and Chief Operating Officer of SCI, has resigned to pursue personal interests. Mr. Pullins will continue working with the Company in a consulting capacity. Robert L. Waltrip, SCI's Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, will assume Mr. Pullins responsibilities on an interim basis until the Board of Directors elects a successor.
