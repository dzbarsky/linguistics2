Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2008; Restates Quarterly Financial Statements for the Periods Ended March 29, 2008 and June 28, 2008.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2008. The company reported a third quarter loss of $169 million, or 67 cents a share, compared to net income in the same period last year of $73 million, or 24 cents a share. The company had revenue of $232 million, compared to revenue of $401 million reported for the same period in 2007. Excluding items, the company's loss would have been $23 million, or 9 cents a share, compared to income of $97 million, or 33 cents a share in the year-ago quarter. The company expects a fourth quarter loss of 27 cents to 29 cents a share on revenue in the range of $215 million to $225 million."
