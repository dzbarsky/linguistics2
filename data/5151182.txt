Principal Financial Group Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2007; Announces Impairments Charges.
Principal Financial Group Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company announced net income available to common stockholders of $34.1 million or $0.13 per diluted share compared to $284.1 million, or $1.04 per diluted share for the year ago period. Total revenues were $2,564.1 million against $2,559.6 million for the year ago period. Net income was $42.4 million against $292.4 million for the year ago period.
