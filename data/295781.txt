Thailand's Leading Mobile Telecommunications Service Provider Standardizes on Brocade Storage Networking Infrastructure.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced that Advanced Info Service plc Thailand's leading mobile telecommunications service provider, has chosen a Brocade SAN to improve the efficiency and reliability of data management and storage and backup, while allowing for future infrastructure enhancement and expansion as data storage needs increase. In addition, the company has seen a 60 percent improvement in backup and recovery time as a result of its SAN implementation.
