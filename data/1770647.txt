Brocade Communications Systems Announces Appointment of Bjarne Poulsen as Regional Sales Manager for UK & Nordics.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced the appointment of Bjarne Poulsen as Regional Sales Manager for the UK and Nordics. Poulsen assumes sales responsibility for the UK, Ireland and Nordics and will report to Ulrich Plechschmidt, Director of EMEA & Latin America. Poulsen joins Brocade with a wealth of experience. Prior to Brocade, Poulsen spent several years directing various consulting projects for technology companies while running Nishan Systems.
