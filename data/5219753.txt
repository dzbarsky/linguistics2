Hansen Natural Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2007.
"Hansen Natural Corp. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2007. Net sales for the fourth quarter increased 63.0% to $246.6 million from $151.3 million a year ago. Net income for the 2007 fourth quarter increased 103.1% to $45.1 million, or $0.45 per diluted share, from $22.2 million, or $0.23 per diluted share last year. For the year, net sales increased 49.3% to $904.5 million from $605.8 million a year ago. Net income for the year increased 52.5% to $149.4 million, or $1.51 per diluted share, from $97.9 million, or $0.99 per diluted share, last year."
