Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 1, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2007 and Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2007.
"Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended July 1, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $84.5 million, a 103% increase over the $41.6 million reported in the second quarter of 2006. The company reported second quarter net income of $9.3 million, or $0.16 per diluted share, compared to net income of $6.8 million, or $0.14 per diluted share in the comparable period of 2006. Net income for the second quarter includes non-cash charges of $0.8 million associated with the January 26, 2007 acquisition of Solexa, and $7.8 million in non-cash stock compensation expense associated with SFAS No. 123R. Excluding the impact of these items and net of pro forma tax expense, net income on a non-GAAP basis was $16.8 million, or $0.29 per diluted share, for the second quarter of 2007, compared to $10.1 million, or $0.21 per diluted share for the second quarter of 2006.
