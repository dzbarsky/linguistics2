OHIO-DA Supports Cadence/MatrixOne Partnership, Alliance Supported as Part of OHIO-DA's 'Corporate Interoperability Initiative.
OHIO Design Automation Inc., the world's leading provider of intelligent electronic visualization and collaboration solutions, affirms their support for the Cadence Design Systems and MatrixOne alliance announced earlier, as part of OHIO-DA's Corporate Interoperability Initiative.OHIO-DA maintains strategic corporate partnerships with both Cadence and MatrixOne. The combination of these two industry leaders has the potential to deliver a Product Lifecycle Management solution tailored specifically for the electronics industry. The advanced solutions to be offered by this alliance, coupled with the integration of InterComm, OHIO-DA's ECAD visualization and collaboration solution, will achieve a new standard in the design anywhere, build anywhere, philosophy.
