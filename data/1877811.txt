New York Times Co. Announces Revnues Results for the Month of August 2005; Provides Earnings Outlook for the Third Quarter of 2005.
"New York Times Co. announced revenues results for the month of August 2005. The company announced that in August 2005 advertising revenues for the Company's business units increased 1.7% and total Company revenues for the month were $233,294,000 aganist the revenues of $231,910,000 an increase of 0.6% compared with August 2004. The company provides earnings outlook for the third quarter of 2005. Third-quarter earnings per share are expected to be in the range of 11 to 14 cents compared to 33 cents last year."
