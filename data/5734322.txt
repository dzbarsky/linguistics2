News Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended June 30, 2008 ; Declares Dividend Payable on October 15, 2008.
News Corp. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended June 30, 2008. The company reported fourth quarter net income of $1.1 billion or $0.43 per share, an increase of $239 million, or 27%, from the $890 million or $0.28 per share on a diluted combined basis reported in the fourth quarter a year ago. The year-on-year growth in the quarter primarily reflects increased consolidated operating income and gains from the sale of the Company's interests in Fox Sports Bay Area and Gemstar-TV Guide International, as well as a gain from the unrealized change in fair value of certain outstanding exchangeable debt securities. Partially offsetting these gains was a decrease in earnings from affiliates, primarily from the absence of DIRECTV earnings and a further writedown of BSkyB's ITV investment.
