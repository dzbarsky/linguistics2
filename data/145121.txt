Health Net of California Names Tom Carter Vice President of Small Business Group Segment.
Tom Carter has been named vice president of Health Net Inc. of California's Small Business Group segment. He is responsible for oversight of sales and administrative functions for the segment, which is focused on employers with between 2 and 50 employees. He is based in Woodland Hills. Tom's intimate knowledge of the California marketplace and his extensive experience with health plan operations make him a vital new member of senior management team.
