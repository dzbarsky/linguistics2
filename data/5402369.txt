Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2008.
Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported a net profit of $27,245,000 or $0.13 diluted earnings per share on revenue 1,138,841,000 against net profit of $83,399,000 or $0.37 diluted earnings per share on revenue 1,369,062,000 for the same period a year ago.
