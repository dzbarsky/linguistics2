Hansen Natural Corp. Reports Financial Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2004.
"Hansen Natural Corp. reported record sales and profits for the second quarter ended June 30, 2004. Net sales for the second quarter increased 62.1% to $46.1 million from $28.4 million a year ago. Net income for the second quarter increased 156.8% to $5.1 million, or $0.43 per diluted share, from $2.0 million, or $0.19 per diluted share, last year. Net sales for the six months ended June 30, 2004 were up 53.2% to $77.4 million from $50.5 million a year ago. Net income for the six months ended June 30, 2004 increased 178.2 %to $7.3 million, or $0.63 per diluted share, from $2.6 million, or $0.25 per diluted share, last year."
