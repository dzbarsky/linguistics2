Big Lots Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended January 31, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Fiscal Year 2010.
Big Lots Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended January 31, 2009. The company reported net income of $78.8 million, or $0.96 per diluted share and $0.97 per basic share, for fourth quarter of fiscal 2008. This compares to net income of $92.0 million, or $1.04 per diluted share and $1.05 per basic share for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2007. For the fourth quarter of fiscal 2008, income from continuing operations was $81.8 million, or $1.00 per diluted share and $1.01 per basic share, compared to income from continuing operations of $85.6 million, or $0.97 per diluted share and $0.97 per basic share, for the same period of fiscal 2007. Record income from continuing operations of $1.00 per diluted share versus income from continuing operations (on a non-GAAP basis) of $0.93 per diluted share last year.
