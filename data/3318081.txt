Phillips-Van Heusen Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2006; Revised Earnings Guidance for 2006; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2006 and Fiscal Year 2007.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2006. For the third quarter, the company reported net earnings of $50.8 million, compared with $40.3 million in the same period last year, as sales rose and the company's gross margin improved. The company posted net income available to stockholders of $50.8 million, or 89 cents a share, up from $37.1 million, or 73 cents a share, last year. Revenue rose 6.6% to $568.3 million from $533.2 million. The company expects 2007 per-share earnings of $2.97 to $3.05, on revenue growth of 5% to 7%."
