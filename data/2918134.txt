Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended April 30, 2006; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2006; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2006.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended April 30, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported GAAP net income of $48.7 million, or $0.87 per share, which was 89% ahead of 2005 GAAP net income of $25.0 million, or $0.46 per share. Non-GAAP earnings per share of $0.74 a 76% improvement over first quarter 2005 non-GAAP earnings per share of $0.42. Total revenue in the first quarter 2006 increased 7% to $506.4 million from $472.1 million in the prior year. For the full year of 2006, GAAP earnings per share is projected to be $2.30 to $2.36, which compares with $1.85 in 2005. Non-GAAP"
