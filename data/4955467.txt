Cephalon Inc. Reports Earning Results for the Third Quarter of 2007.
Cephalon Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2007. For the period, the company reported a loss on lower revenue and a hefty reserve to settle a government probe. The company lost $306.8 million, or $4.58 per share, compared with a profit of $95.7 million, or $1.43 per share, during the same period a year prior. Excluding the settlement reserve, the company said it earned $1.08 per share in the latest quarter. Revenue fell to $438.4 million from $482.3 million.
