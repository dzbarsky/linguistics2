The McGraw-Hill Companies Inc., Annual General Meeting, Apr 29, 2009.
The McGraw-Hill Companies Inc., Annual General Meeting, Apr 29, 2009, at 11:00 US Eastern Time. Location: 1221 Avenue of the Americas, New York, NY 10020-1095,United States. Agenda: To elect three directors; to reapprove the performance goals under the company's 2002 Stock Incentive Plan; to ratify the appointment of the company's independent Registered Public Accounting Firm for 2009; to vote on five shareholder proposals, if properly presented at the meeting; and to consider any other business, if properly raised.
