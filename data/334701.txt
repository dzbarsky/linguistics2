AES Reports Third Quarter ended 2002 year earnings.
"The AES Corp. announced results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2002. Sales and services for the third quarter ended 2002 $2,138 million and $1,845million compared to the same period prior year. Net loss for the third quarter ended 2002 $314 million or $0.58 loss per share and a income of $3 million or $0.01 per share compared to the same period prior year. sales and services for the nine months ended 2002 $6,498 million and $5,806 million compared to the same period prior year. Net loss for the third quarter ended 2002 $743 million or $1.38 loss per share and a income of $229 million or $0.43 per share compared to the same period prior year."
