Hansen Natural Reports Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2004.
Hansen Natural Corp. reported record financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2004. Net sales for the first quarter increased 41.7% to $31.3 million from $22.1 million a year ago. Net income for the first quarter increased 244.9% to $2.2 million, or $0.19 per diluted share, from $633,000, or $0.06 per diluted share, last year.
