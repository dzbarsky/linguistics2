Cephalon Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008.
Cephalon Inc. reported earnings results for the nine months ended September 30, 2008. For the period, the company reported net income of $210,962,000 or $2.79 diluted earnings per share on revenues of $1,434,416,000 compared to the net loss of $235,886,000 or $3.55 diluted loss per share on revenues of $1,322,667,000 for the same period year ago.
