Phillips-van Heusen Corp. Raises Earnings Outlook for the Year 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of Fiscal 2007.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. raised earnings outlook for the year 2007. For the period, the company projected income of $3.06 per share to $3.10 per share. Previously, the company forecast 2007 earnings between $3 and $3.06 per share. Phillips-Van Heusen also forecast full year revenue of about $2.41 billion, representing growth of 15% over 2006. For the second quarter, Phillips-Van Heusen said it would earn 61 cents per share including 3 cents per share in start-up costs. The company forecast second-quarter revenue of $545 million."
