Kirby Corp. Announces Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2007; Provides Earnings Guidance for the First Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Kirby Corp. announced earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net earnings of $34.4 million, or $0.64 per share, compared with net earnings of $23.4 million, or $0.44 per share, for the 2006 fourth quarter. Consolidated revenues for the 2007 fourth quarter were a record $307.9 million, an increase of 22% over the $251.4 million reported for the 2006 fourth quarter. For the 2008 year, guidance range is $2.55 to $2.70 per share compared with 2007 net earnings of $2.29 per share."
