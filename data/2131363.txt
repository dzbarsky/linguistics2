Closed Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Tower Group International Inc.; Buyer(s): FedEx Corporation; Seller(s): McGraw-Hill Companies Inc..
"The McGraw-Hill Companies (NYSE: MHP) announced that it has entered into an agreement to sell Tower Group International, its transportation logistics subsidiary, to FedEx Corporation, based in Memphis. The transaction is scheduled to be completed by the end of February, subject to obtaining necessary government approvals as required in the U.S. under the Hart-Scott-Rodino Act and in Canada under the Canadian Competition Act. Terms were not disclosed. Merrill Lynch & Co. Inc. advised Fedex Corp. on the transaction. On February 29, 2000, The McGraw-Hill Companies (NYSE:MHP) announced that it has completed the sale of Tower Group International to FedEx Corporation."
