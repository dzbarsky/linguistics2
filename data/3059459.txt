Health Net Inc. Board of Directors Appoints Vicki B. Escarra to Serve as Member.
Health Net Inc.'s Board of Directors announced the appointment of Ms. Vicki B. Escarra as its newest Board member. Ms. Escarra brings the total number of board members to nine. Ms. Escarra is the president and CEO of America's Second Harvest - The Nation's Food Bank Network. America's Second Harvest, a network of more than 200 food banks, supports approximately 50,000 local charities operating more than 94,000 programs. Most recently, from May 2001 until October 2004, she was Delta's executive vice president and chief marketing officer. Ms. Escarra also is a director of A.G. Edwards Inc.
