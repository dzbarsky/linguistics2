KeyCorp Announces New Appointments.
KeyCorp announced that Mary Monroe, senior vice president, will lead its efforts to serve commercial banking and corporate clients in the Treasure Valley. As commercial banking team manager, Monroe will work with a seasoned team of bankers to provide Treasure Valley companies with expert service. With more than 30 years experience in banking, Monroe has also served as a commercial lender, a relationship manager and branch manager in Boise, Ketchum, and Milwaukee, Wis.
