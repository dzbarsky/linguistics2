Brocade Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2003 Financial Results.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported financial results for its second quarter of fiscal year 2003, which ended April 26, 2003 (Q2, 2003). Net revenue for second quarter 2003 was $130.9 million, which compares to $123.1 million reported in the first quarter of fiscal year 2003 (Q1, 2003), and $135.0 million reported in the second quarter of fiscal year 2002 (Q2, 2002). Reporting on GAAP basis, net loss for second quarter, 2003 was $146.0 million, or $0.57 per share. This compares to a GAAP net loss for first quarter 2003 of $6.9 million, or $0.03 per share, and GAAP net income of $14.0 million or $0.06 per share in second quarter, 2002.
