Health Net Names Jonathan Rollins as Vice President and Treasurer, Effective November 3, 2008.
Health Net Inc. announced that Jonathan Rollins, CFA, has been appointed vice president and treasurer, effective November 3, 2008. Rollins, 44, joins Health Net from Calyon Corporate and Investment Bank. While at Calyon, Rollins developed growth opportunities for its Healthcare Group. Rollins assumes the role of treasurer previously held by Wisdom Lu, who left the company earlier this year. In this position, Rollins will report to Health Net's chief financial officer and manage the company's banking, investment management and rating agency relationships. He also will direct the company's cash and investment strategy as well as manage its capital structure and cash flow.
