Panera Bread Co. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and First Six Months Ended June 30, 2009; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter of 2009; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2009; Plans to Open New Units in the Third Quarter of Fiscal 2009.
"Panera Bread Co. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and first six months ended June 30, 2009. Operating profit for the period was $32.88 million compared to $27.17 million for the same period last year.
