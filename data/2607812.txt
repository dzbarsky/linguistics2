Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended January 1, 2006 ; Provides Earnings Outlook for the First Quarter and Full Year of Fiscal 2006.
"Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended January 1, 2006. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $23.0 million, a 56% increase over the $14.8 million reported in the fourth quarter of 2004. Net income was $0.3 million, or $0.01 per basic and diluted share for the fourth quarter of 2005, compared to net income of $3.2 million, or $0.08 per diluted share, in the prior year. "
