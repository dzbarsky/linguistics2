Brocade Reports Results for Second Quarter and Six Months Ended May 1, 2004.
"Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported financial results for its second quarter and six months ended May 1, 2004. The company reported net loss of $2.0 million or $0.01 per share on revenue of $145.5 million compared to net loss of $146.0 million or $0.57 per share on revenue of $130.9 million reported for the same period a year ago. For six months, the company reported net loss of $38.7 million or $0.15 per share on revenue of $290.6 million compared to net loss of $152.9 million or $0.62 per share on revenue of $254.0 million reported for the same period a year ago."
