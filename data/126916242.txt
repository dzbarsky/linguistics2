Service Corp. International Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal 2011.
"Service Corp. International reported consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2010. For the fourth quarter, the company reported revenues of $571.3 million compared to $531.8 million a year ago. Operating income was $104.0 million compared to $91.3 million a year ago. Net income attributable to common stockholders was $36.5 million or $0.15 per basic and diluted share compared to $34.3 million or $0.13 per basic and diluted share a year ago. Income from continuing operations before income taxes was $72.0 million compared to $54.8 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations excluding special items were $45.0 million or $0.18 per diluted share compared to $35.7 million or $0.14 per diluted share a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $88.3 million compared to $66.8 million a year ago.
