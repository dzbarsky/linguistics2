Brocade Reports Financial Results for the First Quarter of Fiscal 2003.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. reported financial results for its first quarter of fiscal year 2003 ended January 25, 2003. Net revenue for First Quarter 03 was $123.1 million. This compares to $153.1 million reported in the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2002, and $123.1 million reported in the first quarter of fiscal year 2002. Reporting on a GAAP basis, net loss for First Quarter 03 was $6.9 million, or $0.03 per share. This compares to GAAP net income for Fourth Quarter 02 of $15.7 million, or $0.07 per share, and GAAP net income of $11.7 million or $0.05 per share in First Quarter 02.
