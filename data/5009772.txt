Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter Ended November 4, 2007.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the third quarter ended November 4, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $60.9 million, or $1.05 per share, against a profit of $50.8 million, or 89 cents per share in the prior-year quarter. Licensing revenue was higher across all product categories, especially fragrances, jeans and underwear. Wholesale and retail business also improved, helped by sales of Phillips-Van Heusen neckwear and Izod women's sportswear. Revenue rose 23% to $696.4 million from $568.3 million in the year-ago quarter, missing company expectations of $705 million.
