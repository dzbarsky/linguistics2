On February 14, 2008, Brocade Communications Systems, Inc announced that it has repurchased 11,100,000 shares for a total of $80 million, compared with $50.0 million spent in the first quarter of 2008, to repurchase 6,600,000 shares. Till October 27, 2007, the Company has repurchased 14,287,586 shares for a total of $117.54 million. As of January 26, 2008, the Company has repurchased approximately 25,387,586 shares for a total of approximately $197.54 million.
