Illumina Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2006 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2006 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2006.
"Illumina Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2006. For the third quarter, the company reported net earnings of $16.2 million, or 32 cents a share. During the same period a year ago, Illumina posted a net loss of $1.43 million, or 3 cents a share. The company reported revenue of $53.5 million against $19.5 million for the same period last year. For fiscal 2006, the company now sees pro forma earnings of $1 a share on revenue of $178 million to $182 million. The revenue estimate is an increase of $8 million to $12 million over the previous outlook."
