Cadence Launches Asia Nanometer Technology Symposium.
Cadence Design Systems Inc. announced the first annual Asia Cadence Technology Symposium (ACTS), August 20-30, 2002 for customers across the Asia-Pacific region. ACTS '02 will identify the coming nanometer design challenges and present nanometer methodologies and tools specifically designed to address them. In addition to Cadence technologists, speakers at ACTS '02' include distinguished leaders from the electronic design automation (EDA) Industry. ACTS is an excellent opportunity for engineers, designers and managers working in electronic design.
