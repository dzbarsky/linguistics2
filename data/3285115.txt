Citizens Communications Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter of 2006.
Citizens Communications Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter of 2006. The company reported that income for the quarter was $128.5 million, or 40 cents per share, up from $38.4 million, or 11 cents per share, last year. Revenue for the quarter was $507.2 million, up 1% from $501.2 million last year. The company reported that third-quarter profit surged, thanks to a large profit on the sale of its local exchange carrier business.
