Illumina Inc. Announces Unaudited Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 28, 2008 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year 2008; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2008.
Illumina Inc. announced unaudited earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 28, 2008. For the third quarter of 2008, Illumina reported revenue of $150.3 million, a 54% increase over the $97.5 million reported in the third quarter of 2007. This represents the Company's 29th consecutive quarter of revenue growth. The Company recorded a GAAP net loss of $7.3 million, or $0.06 per basic and diluted share, compared to net income of $14.5 million, or $0.12 per diluted share in the comparable period of 2007. Also included in the GAAP net loss was non-cash charges of $12.7 million in stock compensation expense, $2.7 million associated with the amortization of intangible assets and $0.6 million of accrued contingent compensation associated with the acquisition of Avantome Inc.
