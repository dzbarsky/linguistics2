Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended November 4, 2007 ; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of Fiscal 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of Fiscal 2008 and Full Year of Fiscal 2009.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported earnings results for the nine months ended November 4, 2007. For the period, the company reported net income of $153.0 million or $2.65 per share, which represents an increase of 23% over non-GAAP earnings per share of $2.16 in 2006. Earnings per share for the period increased 21% over 2006 GAAP net income of $114.3 million, or $2.19 per share. Total revenue was $1,840.7 million for the period compared with $1,533.6 million for the same period in 2006.
