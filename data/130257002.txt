KeyCorp Provides Net Interest Margin Guidance for the Second Quarter and Remainder of Year 2011.
"KeyCorp provided net interest margin guidance for the second quarter and remainder of year 2011. Looking at the second quarter of 2011, the company expects to see average earning assets and the net interest margin decline as a result of the movement of the escrow balances and the repurchase of the TARP preferred securities from the U.S. The company expects that net interest margin for the remainder of 2011 in the range of 3.15% to 3.25%."
