Atheros Communications Inc. Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2008 ; Announces Impairment Charges.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $4,758,000 or $0.08 diluted loss per share on the net revenue of $98,296,000 compared to net income of $13,428,000 or $0.22 diluted earning per share on the net revenue of $114,327,000 for the same period last year. The company announced impairment of long term investments of $4,648,000 for the quarter and $15,490,000 for the year compared to $2,277,000 for the quarter and year of 2007."
