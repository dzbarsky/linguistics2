Avery Dennison Revises Outlook for First Quarter 2005.
Avery Dennison Corporation announced that, based on preliminary results for the first quarter of 2005, diluted earnings per share are expected to be approximately $0.10 below the low end of its previous guidance. The Company previously expected diluted earnings per share for the first quarter would be in the range of $0.71 to $0.78, before costs associated with the plant closure. Severance and transition costs associated with the plant closure are expected to total approximately $0.04 per share for the quarter.
