Atheros Communications Inc. Announces its Dual-Band 802.11a/b/g Chipset, AR5002AP-2X.
Atheros Communications Inc. announced that its dual-band 802.11a/b/g chipset, AR5002AP-2X, is featured in Aruba's new multi-purpose, dual-band outdoor access point (AP). The Aruba 80 enables enterprises to operate 802.11a and 802.11b/g wireless networks concurrently. The Aruba 80 is a fully-hardened dual-radio 802.11a+b/g AP that's ideally suited for extending enterprise wireless networks to transportation loading bays, manufacturing sites, campuses, and office parks. The Aruba 80 access point uses both the 2.4GHz and 5GHz frequency bands, allowing enterprises to segment their wireless networks for improved performance and reliability.
