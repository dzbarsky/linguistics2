Atheros Communications Announces Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2007.
"Atheros Communications Inc. announced financial results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2007. Revenue in the third quarter was a record $106.3 million, up 34% from the $79.6 million reported in the third quarter of 2006. In accordance with U.S. generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP), the company recorded third quarter net income of $9.7 million or $0.16 per diluted share. This compares with net income of $6.3 million or $0.11 per diluted share in the third quarter of 2006. Non-GAAP net income in the third quarter was a record $16.9 million or $0.28 per diluted share, compared with non-GAAP net income of $10.4 million or $0.19 per diluted share in the third quarter of 2006. For the nine months, the company reported revenue of $302.6 million against $213.8 million, reported in the same period of 2006.
