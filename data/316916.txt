Higher Margins Drive Health Net's Third Quarter 2002 Earnings To $.55 Per Share; Expects Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2002 and 2003 Guidance.
"Health Net Inc. announced that 2002 third quarter earnings per diluted share reached $.55, compared with net earnings per diluted share of $.02 in the third quarter of 2001. Health Net reported net income in the third quarter of 2002 equal to $69,024,000. Net income in the third quarter of 2001 amounted to $2,326,000 or $.02 per diluted share including$79,667,000 in pretax charges ($50,205,000 after tax). Health Net's total revenues reached $2,577,650,000 in the third quarter compared with $2,544,939,000 in the third quarter of 2001 and$2,505,964,000 in the second quarter of 2002. Health Net expects its improving performance to continue through the fourth quarter of 2002 and into 2003. Accordingly, the company believes that a range of earnings for the fourth quarter of 2002 between $.57 and $.59 per share, and approximately $2.10 to $2.12 per share for the full year of 2002, is reasonable given the business conditions the company faces.
