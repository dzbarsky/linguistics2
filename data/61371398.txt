"Starbucks Corp. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended June 28, 2009. For the 13 weeks, the company reported net earnings were $151.5 million or $0.20 per diluted share on total revenue of $2,403.9 million against net loss was $6.7 million or $0.01 per diluted share on total revenue of $2,574.0 million for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $204.0 million against operating loss was $21.6 million for the same period a year ago. Earnings before income taxes was $217.3 million against loss before income taxes was $33.2 million for the same period a year ago. Non-GAAP operating income was $255.6 million against non-GAAP operating income was $177.6 million for the same period a year ago. Net income was $179.9 million or $0.24 per share against net income was $115.8 million or $0.16 per share for the same period a year ago.
