Panera Bread Reports System-Wide Comparable Bakery-Cafe Sales Increased 5.8% for the Four Weeks Ended February 23, 2002.
"Panera Bread Co. reported that system-wide comparable bakery-cafe sales increased 5.8% for the four weeks ended February 23, 2002. Average system-wide weekly sales increased 6.4% to $34,285 for the four weeks ended February 23, 2002 compared to $32,215 for the four weeks ended February 24, 2001."
