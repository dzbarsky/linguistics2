Phillips-Van Heusen Corporation signed an agreement to acquire Cluett Designer Group, Inc. from Cluett American Corp. for $46.9 million on June 12, 2000. Simpson Thacher & Bartlett LLP acted as legal advisor to Cluett Designer Group, Inc.
