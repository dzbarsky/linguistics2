KeyCorp Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2007.
"KeyCorp reported consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported a net income of $25 million or $0.06 diluted per share on net interest income of $710 million against a net income of $146 million or $0.36 diluted per share on net interest income of $712 million for the same period a year ago. Total interest income for the quarter is $1,447 million compared to $1,413 million a year ago. For the year, the company reported a net income of $919 million or $2.32 diluted per share on net interest income of $2,769 million against a net income of $1,055 million or $2.57 diluted per share on net interest income of $2,815 million for the same period a year ago. Total interest income for the year is $5,644 million compared to $5,380 million a year ago."
