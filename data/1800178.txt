Hansen Natural Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2005.
"Hansen Natural Corp. reported record earning results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2005. For the second quarter, the company reported that net sales increased 85.5% to $85.4 million from $46.1 million a year ago. Operating income tripled to $25.4 million from $8.4 million a year ago. Net income tripled to $15.2 million, or $0.63 per diluted share on a post-split basis, from $5.1 million or $0.22 per diluted share on a post-split basis, last year. For the six months ended June 30, 2005, the company reported that net sales were up 88.0% to $145.5 million from $77.4 million a year ago. Operating income advanced 232.6% to $40.1 million from $12.0 million a year ago. Net income for the 2005 year-to-date period increased 231.8% to $24.1 million, or $0.99 per diluted share on a post-split basis, from $7.3 million, or $0.31 per diluted share on a post-split basis, last year."
