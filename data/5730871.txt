Principal Financial Group Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2008 ; Announces Impairment Charges.
Principal Financial Group Inc. reported earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net income available to common stockholders of $168.3 million, or $0.64 per diluted share compared to $303.8 million, or $1.12 per diluted share for the three months ended June 30, 2007. The company reported operating earnings of $253.7 million compared to $282.9 million for second quarter 2007. Operating earnings per diluted share for second quarter 2008 were $0.97 compared to $1.05 for the same period in 2007. Total revenues were $2,658.2 million compared to $2,831.8 million for the same period last year. Net income was $176.6 million against $312.1 million for the same period last year.
