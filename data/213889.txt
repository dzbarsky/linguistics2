McData Corporation Files for Preliminary Injunction For Patent Infringement Claim Against Brocade Communications Systems.
McDATA Corporation announced that it has filed for a preliminary injunction to immediately enjoin Brocade Communications Systems Inc. from infringing McDATA's United States Patent No. 6,233,236, Method and Apparatus for Measuring Traffic Within a Switch. McDATA filed a complaint in the United States District Court for the District of Colorado against Brocade Communications Systems Inc. for infringement of McDATA's patent. This patent discloses and claims important aspects of the operation for filtering and measuring frame traffic within a switch.
