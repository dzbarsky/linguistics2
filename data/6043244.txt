Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE:FNF) cancelled the acquisition of LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. (NYSE:LFG)..
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. (NYSE:FNF) signed a definitive agreement to acquire LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. (NYSE:LFG) for approximately $129.1 million in stock and cash on November 7, 2008. Fidelity National Financial, Inc. will issue 0.993 shares for each common stock of LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. The exchange ratio will be reduced if the sale of Centennial Bank, which is a condition to closing the merger, results in net proceeds to us of less than $60.0 million. LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. will be wholly owned subsidiary of Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Fidelity National Financial, Inc. can terminate the agreement before November 21, 2008, based on due diligence investigation. Upon termination of agreement, LandAmerica Financial Group Inc. will pay $7.5 million as termination fee. Chicago Title Insurance Company, a subsidiary of Fidelity National Financial, has provided a $30 million stand-by secured credit facility.
