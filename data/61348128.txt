Starbucks Corp. to Open New Pilot Store in Seattle.
Starbucks Corp. announced that it plans to open a new store featuring alcoholic drinks for the first time in Seattle. The new cafe will be called 15th Avenue Coffee and Tea. The new shop will reportedly be opened in late July 2009 and will provide coffee and tea as well as wine and beer. As of now, Starbucks does not serve alcohol in any of its stores across the world. The company plans to expand in other areas based on consumer response.
