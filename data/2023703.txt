Cadence Design Systems Inc. entered into a definitive agreement to acquire CadMOS Design Technology Inc. for $106.23 million on December 29, 2000. The purchase prices comprises of $92.7 million cash and 488,970 shares to be issued if certain predetermined performance factors are achieved by CadMOS over the next three years. The acquisition is expected to be completed in the first quarter of 2001.
