Panera Bread Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Thirty-nine Weeks Ended September 25, 2007 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2007 and Fiscal 2008.
"Panera Bread Co. reported earnings results for the third quarter and thirty-nine weeks ended September 25, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported net income of $12 million, or $0.37 per diluted share compared with net income of $11 million, or $0.34 per diluted share, for the third quarter ended September 26, 2006. The company reported total revenues of $273,213,000 against $204,954,000 for the same period a year ago. The company provided earnings guidance for the fourth quarter of 2007 and fiscal 2008.
