Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended September 30, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter Ending December 31, 2008.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the first quarter ended September 30, 2008. Revenue for the quarter was $839.0 million, an increase of 5.9% over the same period last fiscal year. Net income for the September quarter was $44.3 million, or $0.25 per share. This compares with net income of $53.3 million, or $0.29 per share in the prior year quarter. The Company expects that revenue for the second fiscal quarter ending December 31, 2008, will be in a range of $750 to $800 million, and earnings per share will be in a range of $0.18 to $0.22. These estimates include a restructuring charge of approximately $0.04 per share."
