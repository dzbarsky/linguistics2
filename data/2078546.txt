Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Corporacion EDC, S.A.C.A.; Buyer(s): AES Corp..
The AES Corporation (NYSE:AES) has offered to acquire 51% shares, including shares represented by American Depositary Shares, of Corporacion EDC (BVC: EDC, OTC: ELDAY) and C.A. La Electricidad de Caracas for $863 million. Under the terms of the deal, The AES Corporation will pay $0.47 per share. AES Corporation will make an offer of 926,462,000 shares and 17,719,350 ADS's. Morgan Stanley Dean Witter acted as the financial ddvisor and dealer manager for AES Corporation.
