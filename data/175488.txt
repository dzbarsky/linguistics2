Service Corporation International Announces Fourth Quarter and Full Year Earnings Results for 2001.
"Service Corporation International announced results from continuing operations of $.09 per diluted share for the fourth quarter of 2001 and $.23 per diluted share for full year 2001. Including these non-recurring items, the Company reported losses of $2.03 and $1.55 per diluted share for the three months ended December 31, 2001 and 2000, respectively, and losses of $2.10 and $4.93 per diluted share for the years ended 2001 and 2000, respectively. For three months ended 2001 and 2000 total revenues were $630.9 million and $629.0 million, net loss was $591.8 million and $421.5 million. For twelve months ended 2001 and 2000 total revenues were $2,510.3 million and $2,564.7 million, net loss was $597.8 million and $1,343.3 million respectively. Diluted Loss Per Share was $2.10 as compared to the Diluted Loss Per Share of $4.93 for the same period of last year."
