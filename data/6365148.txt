Principal Financial Group Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 19, 2009.
Principal Financial Group Inc., Annual General Meeting, May 19, 2009, at 09:00 Central Daylight. Location: 711 High Street, Des Moines, IA 50392-0100,United States. Agenda: To elect four directors, one for a one year term and three for three year terms; to approve an amendment to the company's Employee Stock Purchase Plan that will increase the number of shares of common stock available for issuance under the plan by 7,740,757 shares; and ratify the appointment of Ernst & Young LLP as independent auditors for the year ending December 31, 2009; and to act on any other business as may properly come before the meeting or any adjournment or postponement of the meeting.
