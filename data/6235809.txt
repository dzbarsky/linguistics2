Brocade and InfoVista Collaborate to Deliver Enhanced Network Performance Management and Reporting.
InfoVista SA announced that it is working with Brocade Communications Systems Inc. to deliver enhanced performance management and more flexible customer reporting through the Brocade Network Monitoring Service (NMS) and help customers reduce the cost and complexity of monitoring and managing the data center. Leveraging InfoVista's VistaInsight for Networks, Brocade customers now have increased insight into their network infrastructure, enabling proactive action to reduce costs and drive operational efficiencies. Working with InfoVista, Brocade can help its customers accelerate problem resolution, perform detailed trend analysis, and more effectively plan capacity higher quality of service delivery for NMS customers.
