Brocade Communications Systems Introduces Data Center Virtualization Services Practice.
Brocade Communications Systems Inc. announced the expansion of its industry-leading professional services offerings with a new family of Data Center Virtualization Services designed to optimize application and fabric performance, improve data protection, and unify management across the data center. The new services address the needs of IT professionals who want to transform their existing data centers into consolidated, dynamic, next-generation infrastructures that incorporate advanced virtualization capabilities offered by companies such as VMware. Networked storage is an essential requirement in many virtualized environments, providing advanced capabilities such as data mobility. Brocade Data Center Virtualization Services are available now.
