Molex Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended March 31, 2008 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter Ending June 30, 2008.
"Molex Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended March 31, 2008. Revenue for the quarter ended March 31, 2008 was $822.3 million, an increase of 1.9% compared with $807 million reported in the same period last year. Net income for the March quarter was $50.3 million, or $0.28 per diluted share compared with net income of $65.3 million or $0.35 per diluted share for the same period last year. Included in the current quarter was an after-tax charge of $4.9 million or $0.03 per share relating to the previously announced restructuring program and an income tax charge of $10.5 million or $0.06 per share due to foreign tax credit adjustments resulting from the recent filing of fiscal 2007 tax return. Revenue for the nine-months ended March 31, 2008 was $2.46 billion, a decrease of 0.7% compared with $2.47 billion reported in the prior fiscal year.
