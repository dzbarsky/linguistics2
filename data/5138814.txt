Cadence Design Systems Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Year Ended December 31, 2007; Provides Earnings Outlook for the First Quarter and Full Year of 2008.
"Cadence Design Systems Inc. reported earnings results for the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2007. For the quarter, the company reported revenue of $458 million, an increase of 6% over the $431 million reported for the same period in 2006. On a GAAP basis, the company recognized net income of $120 million, or $0.41 per share on a diluted basis, in the fourth quarter of 2007, compared to $48 million, or $0.16 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2006. Using this non-GAAP measure, net income in the fourth quarter of 2007 was $133 million, or $0.46 per share on a diluted basis, as compared to $116 million, or $0.38 per share on a diluted basis, in the same period in 2006. Revenues for the fiscal year 2007 totaled $1.62 billion, an increase of 9% over 2006 total revenues of $1.48 billion. Net income for the fiscal year 2007 was $296 million, or $1.01 per share, compared to net income of $143 million, or $0.46 per share for the year 2006.
