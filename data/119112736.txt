Dolby Laboratories Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended December 31, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2011.
Dolby Laboratories Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended December 31, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net income attributable to company of $86,387,000 or $0.76 diluted earnings per share on revenue of $242,712,000 compared to net income attributable to company of $69,086,000 or $0.59 diluted earnings per share on revenue of $221,216,000 for the same period a year ago. Operating income was $109,202,000 and income before provision for income taxes was $111,066,000 compared to operating income of $104,172,000 and income before provision for income taxes of $106,379,000 for the same period a year ago. Net cash provided by operating activities was $70,652,000 and purchases of property, plant and equipment was $9,646,000 compared to net cash provided by operating activities of $98,464,000 and purchases of property, plant and equipment of $10,619,000 for the same period a year ago.
