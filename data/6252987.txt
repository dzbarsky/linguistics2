Harsco Corp. Wins New Infrastructure Maintenance Support Orders.
Harsco Corp. announced the receipt of four new US orders, valued at approximately USD 2 million, for industrial plant maintenance support. Pursuant to the agreement, the company will provide rental scaffolding and professional installation and dismantling services to support the scheduled maintenance programmes of four large energy plants in Texas, Louisiana, West Virginia and Florida, and include the addition of one new customer to the company.
