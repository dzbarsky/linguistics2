The AES Corporation reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company has reported income from continuing operations before income taxes and equity in earnings of affiliates or $518 million, income from continuing operations of $418 million, income from continuing operations attributable to company of $51 million or $0.08 per diluted share and net loss attributable to company of $48 million or $0.07 per diluted share on revenue of $3,820 million compared to income from continuing operations before income taxes and equity in earnings of affiliates or $134 million, income from continuing operations of $80 million, loss from continuing operations attributable to company of $66 million or $0.10 per diluted share and net loss attributable to company of $47 million or $0.07 per diluted share on revenue of $3,439 million reported in the same period last year.
