Illumina Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended July 3, 2005 ; Provides Earnings Outlook for Third Quarter and Full Year of 2005.
"Illumina Inc. announced earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended July 3, 2005. Total revenue for the quarter was $15.8 million, which represents a 38% increase compared to total revenue of $11.5 million, recorded in the second quarter of 2004. The Company reported a net loss of $18.5 million, or $0.46 per basic and diluted share, for the second quarter of 2005 against a net loss of $3.5 million, or $0.10 per basic and diluted share, in the second quarter of 2004. For fiscal 2005, the company expects total revenue to range from $70 million to $76 million.
