Gentex Corp. Ships Auto-Dimming Mirrors for the New 2009 Honda Civic.
Gentex Corp. has announced that it has begun shipping auto-dimming mirrors for the new 2009 Honda Civic that will be sold in the United Kingdom, Germany and other European Union countries. A Gentex interior base auto-dimming mirror will be available as an option on all 2009 Civic trim levels sold in European Union countries. The mirrors darken automatically in response to the headlamp glare of rearward-approaching vehicles.
