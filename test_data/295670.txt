Panera Bread Signs Two Agreements to Develop 30 Bakery-Cafes In Southern California.
Panera Bread Co. announced agreements with two new franchisees to develop 30 Panera Bread bakery-cafes in southern California. Paul Saber agreed to open 15 bakery-cafes in north San Diego, and Ted and Sheri Hoover signed an agreement to open 15 bakery-cafes in south Orange County. Both franchisees are scheduled to open their first bakery-cafes by the end of 2003.
