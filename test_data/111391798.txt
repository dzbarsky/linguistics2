Hansen Natural Corporation Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2010.
"Hansen Natural Corporation announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2010. For the quarter, net sales was $365.7 million, operating income was $109.7 million, income before income tax was $110 million and net income was $63.84 million or $0.69 per diluted share against net sales of $300.3 million, operating income of $92.8 million, income before income tax of $93.2 million and net income of $57.3 million or $0.60 per diluted share for the same period a year ago. For the six months, net sales was $603.8 million, operating income was $160.5 million, income before income tax was $161.8 million and net income was $96.4 million or $1.04 per diluted share against net sales of $544.4 million, operating income of $158.6 million, income before income tax of $156.4 million and net income of $98.9 million or $1.04 per diluted share for the same period a year ago."
