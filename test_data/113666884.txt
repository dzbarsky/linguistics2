Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. Appoints K P Unnikrishnan as Director-Marketing for Asia Pacific.
Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. announced the appointment of Mr. K P Unnikrishnan as director (marketing) for Asia Pacific with immediate effect. Mr. Unnikrishnan joins Brocade from Sun Microsystems where he was regional marketing director for Emerging Markets, responsible for executing global marketing strategy. Mr. Unnikrishnan will lead the regional marketing team to help drive marketing excellence in Asia Pacific through building brand awareness for Brocade in the ethernet market, generate qualified sales leads, and spearhead integrated programs to help bring the 'Brocade One' unifying network architecture and strategy to market throughout the region.
