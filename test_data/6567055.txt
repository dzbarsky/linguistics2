Kirby Corporation Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the First Quarter Ended March 31, 2009 ; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Second Quarter of 2009; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Year of 2009.
Kirby Corporation announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009. The company reported net earnings attributable to the company for the first quarter ended March 31, 2009 of $28.0 million, or $0.52 per diluted share, compared with net earnings attributable to the company of $36.6 million, or $0.68 per diluted share, for the 2008 first quarter. The 2009 first quarter net earnings included a $4.0 million before taxes, or $0.05 per share, charge for early retirements and staff reductions. Consolidated revenues for the 2009 first quarter were $277.7 million compared with revenues of $330.6 million reported for the 2008 first quarter. The company reported net earnings of $28.46 million, operating income of $277.66 million and earnings before taxes on income of $45.92 million compared to net earnings of $36.81 million, operating income of $330.57 million and earnings before taxes on income of $59.56 million for the same period of last year.
