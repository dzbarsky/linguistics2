Aes Corp. Announced Second Quarter 2002 Results.
The AES Corp. announced it had lost $115 million in the second quarter and cut its 2002 profit forecast for the second time this year. The loss was 22 cents a share, compared with net income of $115 million, or 21 cents a share, a year earlier, the company said in a statement. It forecast a per-share profit of $1 to $1.10, down from a forecast of $1.35 to $1.45 in April.
