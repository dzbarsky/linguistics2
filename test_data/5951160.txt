Fidelity National Financial Inc. Reports Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2008.
"Fidelity National Financial Inc. reported earnings results for the third quarter and nine-month ended September 30, 2008. For the quarter, the company reported net loss of $198.3 million or $0.95 diluted loss per share on total revenue of $989.7 million as compared to net earnings of $6.5 million or $0.03 diluted loss per share on total revenue of $1.36 billion for the same period last year. For nine months, the company reported net loss of $164.1 million or $0.78 diluted loss per share on total revenue of $3.31 billion as compared to net earnings of $174.7 million or $0.79 diluted earnings per share on total revenue of $4.22 billion for the same period last year."
