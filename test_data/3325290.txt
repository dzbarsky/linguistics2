Citizens Communications Co. Reports Earnings Results for the Nine Months Ended September 30, 2006.
Citizens Communications Co. reported earnings results for the nine months ended September 30, 2006. For the period, the company reported net income attributable to common shareholders of $280,644,000 or $0.87 per share on revenues of $1,520,971,000 against net income attributable to common shareholders of $125,594,000 or $0.37 per share on revenues of $1,499,678,000 for the same period a year ago.
