Announced Merger/Acquisition Transaction - Target: Constellation NewEnergy Inc.; Buyer(s): Constellation Energy Group Inc.; Seller(s): AES Corp.; Transaction Size: $260 mm.
Under terms of the agreement, Constellation acquired 100 percent ownership of NewEnergy for approximately $260 million, which includes $120 million for the working capital of the company. For AES NewEnergy Inc. Ulrika Ekman and Jospeh Hadley of Davis Polk & Wardwell were the legal counselors. Morgan Stanley was the financial advisor. For Constellation Energy Mark Director and Goerge Stamas of Kirkland & Ellis were the legal counselors.
