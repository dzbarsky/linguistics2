Fidelity National Financial, Inc. Reports Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended September 30, 2010.
"Fidelity National Financial, Inc. reported unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended September 30, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported total revenue of $1,424.5 million against $1,467.2 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations before taxes were $127.5 million against $108.4 million a year ago. Net earnings from continuing operations were $83.8 million against $76.7 million a year ago. Net earnings attributable to common shareholders were $83.2 million or $0.36 per diluted share against $73.4 million or $0.32 per diluted share a year ago. Cash flow from operations was $21.2 million against $84.5 million a year ago. For the nine months, the company reported total revenue of $4,133.4 million against $4,373.2 million a year ago. Earnings from continuing operations before taxes were $383.5 million against $239.0 million a year ago. Net earnings from continuing operations were $243.1 million against $156.9 million a year ago.
