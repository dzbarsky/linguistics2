Hansen Natural Corporation Announces Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended June 30, 2009.
"Hansen Natural Corporation announced earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended June 30, 2009. For the quarter, the company's net sales increased 6.4% to $300.2 million from $282.2 million a year ago. Operating income for the 2009 second quarter increased 18.7% to $92.8 million from $78.2 million in the 2008 comparable quarter. Net income for the 2009 second quarter increased 14.0% to $57.3 million, or $0.60 per diluted share, compared with $50.2 million, or $0.51 per diluted share in the same quarter last year. Income before provision for income taxes for the period was $93,184,000 compared to $80,959,000 for the same period a year ago. For the first half, net sales increased 10.1% to $544.5 million from $494.4 million in the same period last year. Operating income increased 31.0% to $158.6 million from $121.0 million in the first six months of 2008.
