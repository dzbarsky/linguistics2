Health Evolution Partners, Innovation 2010, May-05-2010 through May-07-2010. Venue: The Ritz-Carlton,, Laguna Niguel,, Dana Point, CA, United States. The conference is to explore new areas where innovation is needed, identify how conditions for commercial success can be fostered and plan how the health care system can take advantage of these ideas.
