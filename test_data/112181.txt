Construction Firm Fluor Posts Profit for Quarter, Fiscal 2001.
"Fluor Corp. managed to turn a profit in the fourth quarter and for fiscal 2001. The world's largest publicly traded engineering and construction company earned $28.5 million on revenue of $2.6 billion in the quarter ended Dec. 31. For fiscal 2001, Fluor reported net income of $19.4 million, compared with $124 million for the fiscal year ended Oct. 31, 2000."
