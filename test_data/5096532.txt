Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Revised Up its Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ending February 3, 2008; Reaffirms its Earnings Guidance for the Full Fiscal Year 2009.
"Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. revised earnings guidance for the fourth quarter and full year ending February 3, 2008. For the fourth quarter, the company is estimating that earnings per share will be at least $0.53, which is at the high end of the guidance previously announced and represents a 12.8% increase over the year ago period. The company is currently estimating revenues in the range of $585 million to $590 million, or an increase of 5% to 6% compared to the prior year. For the full year of fiscal year 2009, the company reaffirmed its earnings per share guidance of $3.55 to $3.65. The company is adjusting its revenue guidance, with revenues currently estimated to be 7% higher than 2007 revenues."
