Service Corporation International Reports Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year of 2003.
"Service Corp. International (SCI) announced results for the fourth quarter and full year of 2003. SCI reported diluted earnings per share of $0.10 for the fourth quarter of 2003 compared to a diluted loss of $0.01 per share for the fourth quarter of 2002. Net Income for the quarter ended was $30.4 million compare to the same period last year net loss of $4.3 million. Total revenue for the quarter ended was $602.3 million compare to the same period last revenue of $586.4 million. For the full year of 2003, SCI reported diluted earnings per share of $0.28 compared to a diluted loss of $0.79 per share for 2002. Net Income for the year ended was $85.1 million compare to the same period last year net loss of $232.5 million. Total revenue for the year ended was $2,341.7 million compare to the same period last revenue of $2,323.6 million."
