Service Corp. International Restates Second Quarter Earnings Results of 2005.
Service Corp. International restated second quarter earnings of 2005 that were slightly lower than previously reported. The company reported net income of $13.7 million, or 4 cents a share for the second quarter. In August, the company had reported net income of $14 million, or 5 cents a share, on revenue of $ 440.8 million. Revenue remained unchanged in the revision.
