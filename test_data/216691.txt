Service Corporation International Announces Third Quarter 2002 GAAP Earnings.
"Service Corporation International announced results for the third quarter of 2002 GAAP Results. For the quarter revenues were $543.8 million against $583.0 million for the same period a year ago. Net income for the period was $4.1 million or $0.01 per share against $4.3 million or $0.02 per share for the same period a year ago. Nine Months Ended September 30, 2002, revenues were $1,695.9 million against $1,879.5 million for the same period a year ago. For the period, net loss was $227.7 million or $#0.77 per share against net loss of $6.0 million or $0.02 per share for the same period a year ago."
