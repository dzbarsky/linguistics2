Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. Reports Consolidated Earnings Results for the Second Quarter and Six Months Ended August 01, 2010; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2010.
Phillips-Van Heusen Corp. reported consolidated earnings results for the second quarter and six months ended August 01, 2010. For the quarter, the company reported net sales of $1,011,439,000, loss before interest and taxes of $44,146,000, pretax loss of $83,371,000 and net loss of $54,587,000 or $0.83 per diluted share compared to net sales of $457,410,000, earnings before interest and taxes of $51,449,000, pretax income of $43,464,000 and net income of $26,557,000 or $0.51 per diluted share over the same period last year. The company reported non-GAAP net income of $51,612,000 or $0.72 per diluted share compared to non-GAAP net income of $31,280,000 or $0.60 per diluted share over the same period last year. Non-GAAP earnings before interest and taxes were $121,936,000 against $57,705,000 over the same period last year.
