Panera Bread Reports System-Wide Comparable Bakery-Cafe Sales Increased 6.6% For the Four Weeks Ended January 26, 2002.
Panera Bread Co. reported that system-wide comparable bakery-cafe sales increased 6.6% for the four weeks ended January 26, 2002. Average system-wide weekly sales increased 12.7% to $32,166 for the four weeks ended January 26, 2002 compared to $28,529 for the four weeks ended January 27, 2001.
