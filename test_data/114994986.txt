Avery Dennison Corporation Reports Preliminary Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Third Quarter and Nine Months Ended October 2, 2010; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year 2010.
"Avery Dennison Corporation reported preliminary unaudited consolidated earnings results for the third quarter and nine months ended October 2, 2010. For the quarter, the company's net sales were $1,640.8 million against $1,549.3 million a year ago. Income from operations before taxes was $77.0 million against $58.3 million a year ago. Net income was $64.2 million or $0.60 diluted per share against $62.5 million or $0.59 diluted per share a year ago. Adjusted non-GAAP operating income before taxes and interest expense was $106.6 million against $112.9 million a year ago. Adjusted non-GAAP net income was $66.0 million or $0.62 diluted per share against $86.8 million or $0.82 diluted per share a year ago. For the nine months, the company's net sales were $4,875.6 million against $4,430.9 million a year ago. Income from operations before taxes was $279.7 million against loss from operations before taxes of $817.4 million a year ago. Net income was $202.7 million or $1.90 diluted per share against $796.6 million"
