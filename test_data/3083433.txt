"Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. entered into a definitive agreement to acquire McDATA Corporation for a reported deal value of $732.58 million on August 7, 2006. The consideration is to be paid at 0.75 stock of Brocade per outstanding McDATA stock. In addition, all issued and outstanding options to purchase McDATA common stock will be converted into options to acquire Brocade stock. The equity is valued at $723.81 million and the options at $8.77 million. As of August 4, 2006, McDATA has 118.69 million class A shares outstanding and 35.64 million class B shares outstanding.
