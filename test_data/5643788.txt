Kimberly-Clark Corp. Reports Earnings Results for the Second Quarter Ended June 30, 2008; Revised Earnings Guidance for the Full Year of 2008.
"Kimberly-Clark Corp. reported earnings results for the second quarter ended June 30, 2008. For the period, the company reported preliminary adjusted earnings were $1.03 per share, compared with its previous outlook for adjusted earnings of $1.08 to $1.11 a share. The company revised earnings guidance for the year of 2008. For the period, the company sees adjusted earnings in the range of $4.20 to $4.30 a share. It had previously expected between $4.45 and $4.60 a share."
