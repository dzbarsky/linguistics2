Kirby Corp. Reaffirms Earnings Guidance for the Third Quarter and Full Year of 2005.
"Kirby Corp. announced that it is confirming its 2005 third quarter earnings guidance of $0.65 to $0.70 per share. For the year, the company expects earnings of $2.50 to $2.60 per share. The company estimates the effects of Hurricane Katrina will be in the $0.04 to $0.05 per share range."
