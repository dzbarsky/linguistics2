KeyCorp Appoints Timothy J. Lathe as Executive Vice President.
KeyCorp announced that the Timothy J. Lathe joined as executive vice president and will lead Key's Wealth Management group. Lathe will oversee the Key business groups that provide investment, banking, insurance, and trust services to affluent individuals and families as part of the broader Key Community Banking organization. The two principal business groups that comprise Wealth Management are Key Private Bank and Key Wealth Management. Lathe has spent most of his career with National City Corporation, where he most recently was executive vice president and head of the Private Client Group and also served as chairman, NatCity Investments Inc. and led the ultra-affluent segment.
