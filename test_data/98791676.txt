The AES Corporation Announces Unaudited Consolidated Earnings Results for the Fourth Quarter and Full Year Ended December 31, 2009; Reports Impairment Charges for the Fourth Quarter of 2009; Revises Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal 2010 and 2011.
The AES Corporation announced unaudited consolidated earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2009. For the quarter, the company reported income from continuing operations before income taxes and equity in earnings of affiliates of $518 million, net loss attributable to the parent company of $48 million or $0.07 diluted per share on the revenue of $3,820 million compared to income from continuing operations before income taxes and equity in earnings of affiliates of $134 million, net loss attributable to the parent company of $47 million or $0.07 diluted per share on the revenue of $3,439 million for the same period last year. Of that revenue amount, $421 million was due to favorable translation impact due to a weaker U.S. Dollar relative to foreign currencies.
