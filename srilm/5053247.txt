KeyCorp Declares Increase in First Quarter Dividend Payable on March 14, 2008; Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fourth Quarter of 2007."KeyCorp Board increased the 2008 first quarter regular quarterly cash dividend by 2.7% to $0.375 per common share, payable on March 14, 2008 to shareholders of record on March 4, 2008.The company provides earnings guidance for the fourth quarter of 2007.Fourth quarter loss per share is seen at $0.05, with items.KEY also expects to incur additional losses (including both realized and unrealized losses) of $55 million to $65 million in fourth quarter due to fixed-income market losses."
