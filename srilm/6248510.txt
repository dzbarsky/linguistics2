Brocade Communications Systems, Inc.Provides Earnings Guidance for the Fiscal Year 2009 and 2010."Brocade Communications Systems, Inc. provides earnings guidance for the fiscal year 2009 and 2010.For 2009, the company expects non-GAAP diluted EPS to be in a range of $0.40 to $0.50.For 2010, the company modeling a revenue range of $2.1 billion to $2.2 billion and assuming moderate level of success, the range of revenue in FY 2010 could be $2.2 billion to $2.6 billion.The company said that could result in EPS growth over FY 2009 guidance of 30% to 45%."
