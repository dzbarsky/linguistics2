Memorial Hermann Healthcare System Selects the Principal Financial Group for Integrated Retirement Plan.A world-class health care system has chosen the Principal Financial Group Inc. as the integrated retirement plan service provider for its defined contribution and nonqualified plans.The contracts for Houston-based Memorial Hermann Healthcare System became effective March 1, 2008.The plans serve more than 15,000 participants within 11 hospitals and numerous specialty facilities throughout the greater Houston community.Memorial Hermann offers world-class innovations, top-quality care and the resources of its university-affiliated teaching hospital in the Texas Medical Center.
